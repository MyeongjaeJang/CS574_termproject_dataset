Telia Lithuania picks new ad agencies Monday 26 March 2018 | 13:01 CET | News 
Operator Telia Lithuania has signed cooperation agreements with advertising agencies McCann Vilnius and Not Perfect I Y&R Vilnius, reports Vz.lt. The operator previously worked with the advertising agencies Milk, New! and McCann Vilnius in recent years. 
Not Perfect I Y&R has been the advertising partner of rival mobile operator Tele2 Latvia for a long period.
