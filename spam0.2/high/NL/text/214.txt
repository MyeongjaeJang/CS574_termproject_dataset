Konga appoints Ijogun as chairman  21 CET | News 
Nigerian e-commerce company Konga.com has announced the appointment of Olusiji Ijogun as chairman effective 01 April. Ijogun has over three decades' experience in finance and management at a number of businesses in various sectors. 
Ijogun holds a B.Sc (Hons.) degree from the Faculty of Business Administration at the University of Lagos. He is a Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Cost and Management Accountants UK and Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria. He currently serves as the Chairman of Ackmen Nigeria and CEO of Innodel Consulting.
