Orion Telekom offers free services for a month with friend referral  17 CET | News 
Croatian operator Orion Telekom announced a promotional offer of free services for a month if a customer signs up a friend to the company's services. The new customer receives 50 percent off their services for the first two months, and the existing customer gets one month free service. The offer is valid until 15 April and applies to any service package, excluding call costs.
