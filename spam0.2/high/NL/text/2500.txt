Vivaldi web browser picks DuckDuckGo for private search   14:01 CET | News 
The web browser Vivaldi announced an agreement to use DuckDuckGo as the default search engine on its 'Private Windows' service. Opening a Private Window in Vivaldi allows users to browse in a more secure way, with sites visited, cookies and temporary files not stored. 
With this integration, Vivaldi continues its focus on privacy and security for its users. It does not track or gather any private information from its users.
