NigComSat discusses cooperation with Turksat Thursday 22 March 2018 | 09:12 CET | News 
The Nigerian Communications Satellite (NigComSat) is engaging Turkish satellite operator Turksat on the possibility of expanding their market in various industries. The two parties held talks at the Satellite 2018 Conference and Exhibition in the US. NigComSat said the parties are exploring technical and commercial agreements that would expand their satellite footprints, let customers use bandwidth from both companies, and exchange Nigerian and Turkish content on their broadcast platforms for the viewing audience. 
The firm says that it is already considering the strength of joint marketing, mutual business development under the commercial and technical agreements, and the required time frame to put all this into action.
