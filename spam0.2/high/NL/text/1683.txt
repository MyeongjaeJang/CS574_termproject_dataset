BT Sport renews Speedway GB live broadcast rights Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 11:05 CET | News 
BT Sport has secured the rights to continue showing Speedway GB for a further two years until 2020. The broadcaster will show 33 speedway events exclusively live in 2018, including 18 Speedway GB fixtures, 11 FIM Speedway Grand Prix, and 4 Monster Energy FIM Speedway of Nations events.
