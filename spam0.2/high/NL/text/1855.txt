Russian online ad market approaches value of TV advertising   15:13 CET | News 
The Russian online advertising market totaled RUB 166.3 billion in 2017, nearly as big as the TV advertising segment worth RUB 170.9 billion, according to the Russian Industry Group of Communications Agencies. The whole advertising market totaled RUB 417 billion, up by 14 percent year-on-year. 
Online advertising revenues reached RUB 54.9 billion in the third quarter of 2017, exceeding TV ad spending of RUB 51.3 billion.
