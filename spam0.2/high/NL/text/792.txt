VNPT launches Karo OTT service   07:51 CET | News Vietnamese operator VNPT has introduced an OTT application dubbed 'Karo'. The app is available for businesses as well as residential users. Karo will provide a multi-platform communication and enterprise management tool. The application is currently available on Google Play and Apple Store. Customers are offered VND 10,000 to their main account and 300 minutes calling from Karo to VinaPhone subscribers per month for three months.
