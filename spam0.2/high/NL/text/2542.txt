Beeline updates Everything plans to offer flexible bundles Thursday 22 March 2018 | 14:33 CET | News 
Russian mobile operator Beeline has updated its Everything! plans to make them more flexible. Under the name EverythingMe, customers can choose the amount of voice, data and SMS included on a monthly basis and modify these amounts according to their needs each month. For example, they can swap minutes for more MB and still keep the same monthly price. There is no limit on the number of changes made to the plan allowances.
