Online shop 220.lv plans upgrade as mobile purchases jump 25%   15:38 CET | News 
Latvian online shop 220.lv plans to invest EUR 1 million in the upgrade of its mobile platform in the next few years, reports Db.lv. The mobile application and a mobile version of the shop's website will be improved. In 2017, the amount of purchases from mobile devices grew by 25 percent on the online shop.
