Ukrtelecom owed almost UAH 190 mln by state for subsidised services  47 CET | News 
Ukrainian national operator Ukrtelecom announced that state institutions owed the operator almost UAH 190 million for services provided to subsidised users as of the beginning of this year. The debt has gradually increased, from UAH 168 million at the end of 2016 and UAH 39 million at the end of 2015. Almost 500,000 customers use such services from Ukrtelecom across the country.
