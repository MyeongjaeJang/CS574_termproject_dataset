MTS Belarus offers mobile devices promo to fixed subs  10 CET | News 
Mobile operator MTS Belarus has introduced a promotion of mobile devices in its shops for subscribers of its fixed services. Customers using 'Household Connect' and 'Everything!' services from the operator are offered smartphones and tablets for discounted rates, as well as special installment conditions when signing annual contracts. 
The promotion is valid until 31 May and includes dozens of models of smartphone.
