Nokia 7 plus to come with free Google Home Mini in UK   09:51 CET | News 
HMD Global announced that the new Nokia 7 plus smartphone will come with a free Google Home Mini smart speaker in the UK. The offer is valid the first month of sales after the phone's launch on 02 May. 
The Nokia 7 plus, an Android One device first unveiled at Mobile World Congress last month, will sell for GBP 349 in the UK. Customers can use voice commands over the Google Assistant on the phone to control the smart speaker.
