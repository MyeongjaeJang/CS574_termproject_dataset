Polycom promotes Hug to area sales VP for EMEA, APAC   14:44 CET | News Polycom promoted Andrew Hug to the role of area sales vice president, systems engineers, EMEA and APAC. In his new role, Hug will focus on identifying and sharing the unique collaboration and communication needs and feedback from the thousands of EMEA and APAC customers with the Polycom engineering team. 
Hug will lead the charge on providing customer insights, analysing customer feedback, applying international best practices for product innovation, and enabling more fluid cross-company technical knowledge-sharing. He will report directly to Marco Landi, senior vice president, Asia Pacific, Africa and Europe. Hug joined the company in 2001 from Accord where he was the support director covering EMEA & Asia, and was responsible for both pre- and post-sales support.
