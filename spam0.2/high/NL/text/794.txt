TPG Telecom launches mobile data plan for seniors   06:15 CET | News Singaporean mobile operator TPG Telecom has introduced a mobile plan for seniors. The package includes a SIM card, 3GB for data traffic per month, as well as unlimited calls to local mobile numbers. The plan will mainly target senior citizens aged 65 and above, Channel NewsAsia reports. The data included in the plan aims to encourage the elderly to go online, and use WhatsApp and WeChat apps, as well as navigate with map apps.
