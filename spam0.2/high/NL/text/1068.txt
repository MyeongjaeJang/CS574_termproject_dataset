Slovak Telekom, O2 Slovakia are neck and neck in LTE coverage race Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 13:29 CET | News 
After Slovak Telekom recently announced an increase in its LTE network coverage by four percentage points from 89 percent of the population at the end of 2017 to 93 percent, claiming leadership, previous 4G leader O2 Slovakia responded that it has extended its own reach, reported Zive.sk. O2 Slovakia announced that since the beginning of the year, it has added 50 locations to the LTE coverage map and its current coverage is 93.56 percent of the population.
