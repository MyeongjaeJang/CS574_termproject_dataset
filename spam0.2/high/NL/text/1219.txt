Huawei starts public cloud service in Russia  38 CET | News 
Huawei has founded the new Huawei Cloud unit at its Russian branch, reports Tdaily.ru. The company also launched the first public cloud service across the country. The service works under the Huawei brand, in cooperation with the Russian data centre operator 3data. 
Huawei 3data Cloud is a public cloud service for corporate customers, operating as an open platform for the launch of SaaS and PaaS offers from partners. The pay-as-you-go billing model is applied for use of the service.
