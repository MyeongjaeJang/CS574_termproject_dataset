Beeline Uzbekistan unit moves to MUIC innovation centre   15:52 CET | News 
Uzbekistan company BeeLab, a subsidiary of mobile operator Beeline Uzbekistan, has moved into the Mirzo Ulugbek Innovation Centre (MUIC), reports Uzreport.uz. The company plans to export over USD 500,000 worth of IT-services and products this year.
