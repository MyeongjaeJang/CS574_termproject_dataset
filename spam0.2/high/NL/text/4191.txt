T-Mobile Czech Republic starts Apple carrier billing Monday 26 March 2018 | 11:15 CET | News 
T-Mobile Czech Republic announced that it's started offering carrier billing for Apple. That means its customers can make purchases on the App Store and bill them to their mobile invoice or prepaid credit, without the need for a credit card.
