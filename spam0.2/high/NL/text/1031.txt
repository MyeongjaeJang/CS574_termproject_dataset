Amazon Germany launches Easter Sales week Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 13:01 CET | News 
Amazon Germany has launched its Easter Sales week, offering discounts of up to 50 percent as well as thousands of daily “Lightning Deals” on select products through 26 March. Amazon said it has slashed prices for a number of products for its Easter Sales, including reducing the price of the Amazon Echo Dot, Amazon Echo, Amazon Echo Spot and Amazon Echo Show by 27 percent. 
The price of the Amazon Fire TV Stick and Amazon Fire TV have both dropped 25 percent, while the Amazon Fire 7 Tablet, Fire HD 8 Tablet, Amazon Fire HD 10 Tablet, Amazon Fire 7 Kids Edition Tablet and Amazon Fire 8 HD Kids Edition Tablet have seen their prices drop 22 percent.
