Telenor Bulgaria supports Earth Hour initiative   09:35 CET | News Telenor Bulgaria supported the Earth Hour initiative of the international environmental organisation WWF for a tenth year in a row. On 24 March, between 8.30hrs and 9.30hrs, Telenor turned off the lights at its administrative buildings, while the shops of the operator throughout the country switched to low light mode. The company's systems providing the network operation and customer service were left online. Telenor is not only a participant but also an active ambassador of the initiative. Each year the company reminds and encourages its customers, partners, employees and social media users to get involved and show their commitment to the initiative.
