City and University of Amsterdam to set up special lab for AI   12:00 CET | News The University of Amsterdam (UvA) and the municipality of Amsterdam are establishing an institution for research into artificial intelligence (AI). At the Amsterdam Science Park, there will be a 'co-creation building' for education, research and companies. The municipality will contribute EUR 4 million, with construction set to start next year and complete in 2021. 
The university has already participated in projects with a number of companies, including Qualcomm and Bosch.
