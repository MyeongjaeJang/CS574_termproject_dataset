Elisa pledges to reduce emissions   13:33 CET | News 
Finnish operator Elisa said it has set two goals to reduce its emissions and under the international Science Based Targets initiative. The first aims is to reduce its emissions (Scopes 1 and 2) by 50 percent by 2025 compared with 2016. The second is to reduce emissions related to travel and waste, as well as emissions from the supply chain (Scope 3), by 12 percent compared with 2016, again by 2025. 
Elisa said it has previously signed the Finnish Commitment 2050 where it commits to become carbon neutral in its use of energy by 2050. The Science Based Targets initiative is a collaboration by the charity CDP, the United Nations Global Compact, World Resources Institute (WRI) and the World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF) and the We Mean Business Coalition.
