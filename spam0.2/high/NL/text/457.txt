By: Zubair Ahmad (Islamabad) 
Chitral is geographically the largest District of KPK province, bordering with Gilgit-Baltistan and Afghanistan. It is a beautiful, mountainous, districts, divided by rivers and mountains. It is also the home of the Kalasha people, a unique ethnic group. 
The Melph Valley of Chitral, though wonderfully picturesque, has been hidden from the eyes of the tourists. Here are some photographs of the Melhp Valley, home to some of the most amazing views one can imagine.
