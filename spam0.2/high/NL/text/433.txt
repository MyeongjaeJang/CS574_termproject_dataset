ER-Telecom expands network in Chuvashia  39 CET | News 
Russian operator ER-Telecom, working under the Dom.ru brand, has launched a new network section on its city network in the Republic of Chuvashia. The fragment will be used for providing cable TV and data services in the cities of Cheboksary and Novocheboksarsk. 
The regional branch of the federal telecommunications regulator Roskomnadzor has cleared the exploitation of the newly-deployed network.
