'Imagine with Orange' crowdsourcing platform opens in Tunisia   10:50 CET | News 
Orange Tunisia has announced the launch of its first initiatives on the crowdsourcing platform of the Orange group, 'Imagine with Orange'. The operator has already awarded a prize to KarhabtiCare, a mobile app for car owners, as part of an innovation challenge in partnership with car dealer Italcar. After this pilot, it has now officially opened its first contest on the topic of 'smart agriculture' in collaboration with local start-up Iris Technologies.
