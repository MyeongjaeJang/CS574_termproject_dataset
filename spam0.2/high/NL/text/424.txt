IDC expands fibre network to new areas  28 CET | News 
Moldova-based operator IDC, working in the Republic of Transnistria, has expanded the coverage of its fibre-optic network to new streets and apartment houses in the city of Bendery and the settlements of Parkany and Sukleya. 
Subscribers connecting to internet services from IDC over its fibre network are also able to use IPTV services free. The operator offers end-user devices for PRB 1 when concluding subscription contracts for two years.
