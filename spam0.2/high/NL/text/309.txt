Etisalat launches 'Hello Business Pitch' competition   11:58 CET | News 
Etisalat has announced the launch of its ‘Hello Business Pitch’ competition designed to encourage home grown SMBs and start-ups as well as to promote the growth and innovation of businesses in the UAE. 
The competition will provide an opportunity for start-ups to pitch their business ideas in front of an expert panel of judges. A panel of judges will select the top five winners who will be awarded prizes worth up to AED 180,000 in the form of business devices, Etisalat services and National Bonds at the Hello Business Hub on 02 May. 
SMBs and start-ups are invited to submit their entries to 15 April.
