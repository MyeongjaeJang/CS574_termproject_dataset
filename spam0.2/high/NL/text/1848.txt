Spain's Pepephone launches family-oriented Inimitable plan  04 CET | News Spanish operator Pepephone, a Masmovil brand, has announced the launch of Trio Inimitable, its first residential plan for users requiring more than one mobile line. The offer comes with two Inimitable mobile lines with unlimited calls and 19GB of data each plus ADSL broadband at 20Mbps for EUR 53.80 a month, rising to EUR 62.80 a month for 100Mbps symmetric fibre.
