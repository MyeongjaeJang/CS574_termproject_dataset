Megafon, Qualcomm test 5G in Moscow Friday 23 March 2018 | 12:43 CET | News 
Russian mobile operator Megafon, in cooperation with Qualcomm, has tested 5G services on the 24.25-27.5 GHz band, reports Tdaily.ru. The operator used a segment of its LTE network in downtown Moscow.
