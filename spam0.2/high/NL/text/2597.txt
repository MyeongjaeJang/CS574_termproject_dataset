EIB appraising Vodafone Spain's EUR 600 mln funding request   14:53 CET | News The European Investment Bank has announced that it’s appraising Vodafone Spain’s request for EUR 600 million to help fund the upgrade of its fixed cable network and related fixed infrastructure throughout the country as well as “developments in mobile network evolution, including new capabilities and virtualisation.”
In a statement , the EIB said the funding is expected to increase broadband penetration rates, including in convergence areas with lower access ratios, and to result in significant improvements in service quality. The total cost of the project is estimated at EUR 1.257 billion.
