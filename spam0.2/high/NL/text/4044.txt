Deutsche Telekom to sponsor performances at 2018 Beethovenfest in Bonn Friday 23 March 2018 | 11:37 CET | News Deutsche Telekom announced it is supporting a set of events at the 2018 Beethovenfest in Bonn running from 31 August to 23 September. On 15 September, a concert for experimental classical music will be held with an audiovisual experience built around Beethoven’s music organized by the Music:Eyes ensemble, while on 20 September, pianist Alberto Ferro will hold a recital honoring Claude Debussy. The Music:Eyes event will also be organized by students managers supported by Deutsche Telekom, said the company. Ferro was also the winner of last year’s International Telekom Beethoven Competition Bonn.
