ER-Telecom adds 24 new TV channels   11:11 CET | News 
Russian operator ER-Telecom, working under the Dom.ru brand, has added 24 new channels to its pay-TV offer, reports Rostov.kp.ru. The whole offer now contains 219 channels, including 85 in HD. 
The list contains mainly Russian channels. Numerous channels also have been switched from the SD to HD standard.
