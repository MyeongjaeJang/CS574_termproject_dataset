Oi launches Managed Services Management platform   11:24 CET | News Brazilian operator Oi has launched the Managed Services Management (GIS MSS) platform, which enables integrated management of security services for corporate customers. The GIS MSS platform has an intuitive portal that can be accessed by multiple devices and has a management team 24 hours a day, seven days a week. 
The operator has also developed Oi Smart Cloud 4.0, in partnership with Hewlett Packard Enterprise and Ormuco. The solution enables managed cloud deployments and efficient application and data management within and outside the customer data centre with security and agility, and utilizes AI capabilities to provide maximum operations efficiency with a single multi-cloud management software.
