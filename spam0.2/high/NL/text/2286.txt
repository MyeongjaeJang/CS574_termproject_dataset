Antenna Hungaria offers uncoded access to Viasat3 Thursday 22 March 2018 | 07:44 CET | News 
Antenna Hungaria said it started offering uncoded access to the Viasat3 channel to nearly 750,000 MinDig TV households until 15 April . This is possible following an agreement with Sony Pictures Television CE. Viasat3's live broadcast will be available in the MinDig TV application beyond the DVB-T system during the promotional period, the company said in a press release.
