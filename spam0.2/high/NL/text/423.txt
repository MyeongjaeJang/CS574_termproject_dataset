Russian watchdog investigates gambling ads on Google  29 CET | News 
Russian federal competition regulator FAS has started an investigation into Google, reports Vedomosti. The investigation was begun after Google ran an a dvert featuring online casino Volcano. This kind of advertising for a search company is prohibited in Russia. The investigation was started after a complaint was filed by an undisclosed user. 
Google has been told to provide the watchdog with copies of agreements related to the advert.
