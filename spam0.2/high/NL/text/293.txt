maandag 19 maart 2018 19:30 - 21:00 Famelab@UT On 19 March 9 young UT students and scientists will present their research during engaging three-minute pitches in front of a general audience. The contestants have to impress the judging panel and the public, presenting a scientific topic of their choice. No slides, only props are allowed and the pitches are in English. The presentations will be judged on Content, Clarity, Charisma. Do you want to be present and to cheer on the contestants? Then join in this first FameLab@UT edition on 19 March and register now !
