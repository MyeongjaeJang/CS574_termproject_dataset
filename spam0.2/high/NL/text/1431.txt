Transit Wireless names Hintze as VP business development Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 14:09 CET | News 
US telecommunications company Transit Wireless appointed Jim Hintze as its new VP of Business Development. He joins the executive team after serving as SVP of Sales at FiberLight where he was responsible for driving the company’s portfolio of network infrastructure services’ growth. Hintze brings over 25 years of sales and marketing leadership experience within the telecommunications industry. In his role, Hintze will leverage the significant fiber, data centre , and mobile capabilities of Transit Wireless, and the company’s significant footprint of Wi-Fi access points to both optimise existing customer care and to expand into new businesses, including emerging IoT and Smart City applications.
