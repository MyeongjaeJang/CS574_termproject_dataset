Skymax offers promo to new subscribers in Yurga   10:52 CET | News 
Russian ISP Skymax, working in the Kemerovo and Novosibirsk regions, offers a promotion for customers living in private residences in the town of Yurga. They can connect to services from Skymax for a RUB 500 activation fee. The offer is valid in the city of Yugra in the Kemerovo region.
