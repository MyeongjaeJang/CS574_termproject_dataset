Lehmann to resign as fallout from cricket cheating crisis continues | Sky News Australia   www.skynews.com.au Australian cricket coach Darren Lehmann is poised to resign this week in the aftermath of Steve Smith's admission the team cheated during the third test against South Africa. Smith admitted that he and the senior leadership group advised batsman Cameron Bancroft to tamper with the ball to gain an unfair advantage over their opponent. Despite Smith claiming Lehmann didn't know about the plot, the coach is expected to step down from his position this week. Cricket Australia has launched an investigation into the matter.
