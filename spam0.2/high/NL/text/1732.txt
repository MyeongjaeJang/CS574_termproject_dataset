21 March 2018, 01:19 Four people killed in CTO in Chechnya 
Four armed men were blocked and killed during a counterterrorist operation conducted in the Achkhoi-Martan District of Chechnya, law enforcers have reported. 
According to the spokesman of the Chechen MIA, March 20, at about 10:00 p.m. Moscow, time in a forest of the Achkhoi-Martan District, on the border with Ingushetia, policemen, acting jointly with FSB agents, "found and blocked a group of militants." 
A CTO was launched, in the course of which four men were killed. There were no victims among law enforcers. The casualties are being identified, the "Interfax" reports with reference to its source from the Chechen MIA.
