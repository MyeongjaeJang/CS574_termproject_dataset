BCX achieves IT service management certification   10:57 CET | News 
South African ICT service provider BCX said that is has achieved the ISO 20000-1 IT Service Management certification, making it only the second South African-owned company to gain the international standard. The ISO 20000-1 certification is the globally recognised international standard for IT service management and is the global industry benchmark for IT services. 
The ISO 20000-1 certification is awarded for design, transition, delivery and improvement of services that fulfil service requirements and provide value for the customer and service provider. BCX chose to pursue this certification to give customers peace of mind about the company's IT service management standards and processes.
