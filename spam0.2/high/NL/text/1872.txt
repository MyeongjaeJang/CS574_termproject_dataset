Russia extends investigation into bulk SMS rates   15:26 CET | News 
Russian federal competition regulator FAS has prolonged the deadline for completing its investigation into the rates charged by mobile operators to banks for bulk SMS, reports Prime. The previous deadline was 20 March, and a new deadline hasn't been specified. 
The investigation is related to SMS rates at Beeline, Megafon, MTS and Tele2 Russia and was initiated by a complaint submitted to the watchdog by the National Council of the Financial Market.
