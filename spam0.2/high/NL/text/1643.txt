BT names Hila Meller as head of Security, Europe   11:18 CET | News 
BT has appointed Hila Meller as Vice President for Security, Europe. She replaces Luke Beeson, who has left the company. Based in Milan, Italy, Meller will lead a team of security professionals protecting international organisations. 
Meller joins BT from DXC Technology, formerly Hewlett-Packard Enterprise, where she held various regional leadership roles within the Security business division.
