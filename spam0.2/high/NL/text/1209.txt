Massimo Moreschini has been appointed Director of Rooms & Hotel Residences of Four Seasons Hotel Kyoto from February 2018. Most recently Massimo was Executive Assistant Manager, Rooms at Raffles Hotel Singapore. Past experiences include Rooms Division Director at Celtic Manor Resort, host of the 2010 Ryder Cup, and luxury London Hotels such as Claridge's and The Dorchester where he was engaged in both Food & Beverage and Rooms Division operations.
