Unitymedia to expand broadband network in Greven, Germany   12:13 CET | News 
Unitymedia has begun construction works to expand its broadband network in the city of Greven in North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany. Over 1,200 households in Greven’s district of Reckenfeld-Sued will have access to high-speed internet at speeds up to 400 Mbps. Unitymedia said it is investing over EUR 1.86 million into the upgrade without any public subsidies and laying 10.3 kms of fibre-optic cable as part of the upgrade. Households that now register for one of Unitymedia’s packages with at least two services will receive their connection for the standard price of EUR 398.
