Good Line introduces 4K Android set-top box   09:41 CET | News 
Russian ISP Good Line, working in the Kemerovo region, has started selling the new white-label Grand TV 4K set-top box. The Android-powered set-top box is based on a quad-core Cortex-A53 2.0 GHz processor and includes dual-band Wi-Fi. The set-top box can be managed by a smartphone after downloading the Grand TV mobile application.
