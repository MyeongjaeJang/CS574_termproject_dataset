Russian mobile operator Skylink, a subsidiary of Tele2 Russia providing LTE services on the 450 MHz band, has introduced a promotion of its services, reports Comnews.ru. Subscribers connecting to an unlimited tariff from the operator are offered a router for RUB 1. The promotion is valid in the cities and regions of Moscow and St Petersburg, as well as in the Tver and Novgorod regions until 31 July.
