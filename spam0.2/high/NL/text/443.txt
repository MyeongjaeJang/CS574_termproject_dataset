MTS Belarus LTE network reaches nearly 80% of urban population   15:40 CET | News 
Mobile operator MTS Belarus announced that its LTE network has reached 78.8 percent of the urban population across the country. The operator plans to expand its LTE services in municipal centres and villages this year.
