Innogy rolls out faster broadband in Waldboeckelheim   12:12 CET | News German regional operator Innogy said it will complete upgrades to its broadband network in the town of Waldboeckelheim in the German state of Rhineland-Palatinate by the end of May. Households in Waldboeckelheim will then have access to high-speed internet with download rates up to 120 Mbps after the upgraded network is activated.
