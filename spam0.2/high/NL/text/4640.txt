South Korea hits 7 mln Samsung Pay m-payment system users in February Tuesday 27 March 2018 | 06:57 CET | News South Korea reached nearly 7 million users of the Samsung Pay m-payment system in February. Around 6.94 million users of Android-powered smartphones used the Samsung Pay service at least once in February this year, Yonhap news agency reports, citing data compiled by WiseApp. The figure compares to 4.47 million Samsung Pay service users in February 2017.
