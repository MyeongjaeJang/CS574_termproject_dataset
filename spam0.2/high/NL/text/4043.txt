Colombian home internet penetration tops 50% in 2017 Friday 23 March 2018 | 11:44 CET | News Residential internet penetration in Colombia reached topped 50 percent for the first time in 2017, up from 45.8 percent in 2016, according to a report from national statistics agency Dane. Some 96.4 percent of homes had at least one mobile phone at the end of December while 44.3 percent had a desktop PC, laptop or tablet, said the report, adding that more than 8 in 10 Colombians accessed the internet via their smartphone, up from just over 70 percent the year earlier. Pay-TV subscriptions also rose, by 3.5 percent year on year, to 73.4 percent.
