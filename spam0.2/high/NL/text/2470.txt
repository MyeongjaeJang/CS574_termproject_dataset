Virgin Media launches new top speed of 350 Mbps on broadband services   12:29 CET | News 
Virgin Media is launching a new top speed for its broadband service of up to 350 Mbps, targeting data-hungry families. Customers already on the Vivid 300 plan will be transferred automatically to the new Vivid 350 connection at no extra cost. New customers can sign up online, with packages available from GBP 48 per month.
