Phonegroup joins Samena Council   15:13 CET | News 
Samena Council has announced that Phonegroup, a company actively involved in the region's wholesale and retail voice business, has joined its membership of telecom operators and technology companies. Wholesale carriers and VoIP retail providers which have either joined the Samena Council or are considering membership, will now be able to take advantage of Phonegroup's VoIP communication services.
