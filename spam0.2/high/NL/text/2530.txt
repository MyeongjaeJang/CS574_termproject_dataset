Vodafone wins data services contract at Aguas de Portugal   14:26 CET | News 
Vodafone Portugal announced that its won a contract to provider data services for the Aguas de Portugal (AdP) Group. Using both fibre and 4G networks, the company will connect 120 sites across Portugal for the water company and its 13 subsidiaries, providing scalable and reliable bandwidth. 
Vodafone won an international public tender for the contract. AdP provides services to around 80 percent of the Portuguese population and will receive 24/7/365 technical support from Vodafone.
