Ooredoo Oman offers bonus minutes to Bangladesh on 26 March upon International Bundle purchase Monday 26 March 2018 | 11:16 CET | News 
Ooredoo Oman has announced an offer for Bangladeshi national customers in celebration of Bangladesh's 47th Independence Day. On 26 March, prepaid and postpaid customers will get free bonus minutes when they subscribe to an International Bundle. The commemorative offer will be valid for one day only and will give prepaid and Shababiah customers 50 international calling bonus minutes, and post-paid customers 250 minutes to Bangladesh.
