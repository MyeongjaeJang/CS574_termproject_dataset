Telekom Slovenije opens NB-IoT testing to partners  18 CET | News 
Telekom Slovenije announced that it's set up a test environment for NB-IoT in Ljubljana and Maribor. Companies, independent developers and educational institutions can use the network to test their IoT applications in a live environment at the two sites. Telekom is also conducting its first field tests of NB-IoT in cooperation with the Faculties of Electrical Engineering at the University of Ljubljana and University of Maribor, in areas such as smart cities, e-mobility and e-health.
