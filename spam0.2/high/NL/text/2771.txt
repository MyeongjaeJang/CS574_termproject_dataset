Rabobank customers report problems with online banking   11:50 . Source: Wikipedia/Lofor 
Rabobank customers are reporting problems with online banking on Thursday morning. They can't check their balance on the bank's website or mobile banking app. Fault report website Allestoringen.nl received thousands of complaints from Rabobank customers on Thursday, NU.nl reports. 
The bank is aware of the fault and working to fix it, a spokesperson said to the newspaper. "Payments can still be processed, only the balance is not visible", the spokesperson said. 
He could not say how long it will take to fix this problem. Tags:
