A1's brand Yesss! launches deal with 5 GB data, 1,500 units for EUR 9.99/month Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 13:10 CET | News 
Austrian operator A1's brand Yesss! has introduced the tariff Complete L, which for EUR 9.99 permonth includes 5 GB data, LTE with the speed up to 100 Mbps and 1,500 units. 
Unused data can be transferred to the following month and the accumulated amount to be transferred is 15 GB a month. The package includes 1,500 minutes or SMS. From the total data allowance of 5 GB, 2.8 GB can be used in the EU roaming. Customers who need an extra portion of data can buy 1 GB Refill for EUR 3.90.
