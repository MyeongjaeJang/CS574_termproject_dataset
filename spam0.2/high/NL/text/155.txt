Destiny expands abroad with takeover of Motto in Netherlands  42 CET | News 
The Belgian operator Destiny has made its first acquisition abroad with the takeover of Dutch company Motto Communications. The acquisition gives it access to Motto's Flux platform for partners and resellers to distribute cloud services. 
The deal is part of Destony's strategy announced last year to grow its annual revenues and expand across Europe. The company has since adjusted its turnover target to EUR 42 million for this year. The takeover adds new developers and engineers, while giving Motto access to Destiny's cloud communications platform. 
In addition, Motto's CCO Robbert Heemskerk and founder Kay Reijnen are joining Destiny as shareholders and part of the management team. Heemskerk will be in charge of Sales & Operations for the Dutch market and Reijnen for software development.
