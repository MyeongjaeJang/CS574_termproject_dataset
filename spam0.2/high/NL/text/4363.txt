Rostelecom takes down obsolete radio-relay line connecting Moscow, Khabarovsk Monday 26 March 2018 | 15:57 CET | News 
Russian national operator Rostelecom has started work on taking down antennas of an obsolete radio-relay line connecting Moscow and Khabarovsk. Work is being carried out in the Kurgan region. The line crosses 178 settlements in 19 regions across the country. 
The radio relay line was replaced by the fibre-optic backbone Transit Europe-Asia.
