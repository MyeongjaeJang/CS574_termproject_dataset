Penki introduces TV-promo for new subs Monday 19 March 2018 | 15:56 CET | News 
Lithuanian regional fixed operator Penki, which earlier worked under the Skynet brand, has introduced a promotion for customers subscribing to its internet and Smart TV services for 24 months. Customers will get five thematic pay-TV packages for free. The promotion will be valid for people connecting until 15 April. 
The list of the packages includes Film, Children, Entertaining, Music and Knowledge
