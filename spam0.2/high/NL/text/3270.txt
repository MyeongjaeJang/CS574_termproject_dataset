Uniserve expands data centres capabilities across Canada   18:10 CET | News Uniserve Communications (Uniserve) announced it expanded access to data centre facilities across Canada, further strengthening its data centre portfolio to meet the evolving needs of its customers. The expansion, resulting from agreements with multiple Canadian telecommunications and data centre providers, gives Uniserve access to 43 Tier I, Tier II and Tier III data centre facilities across Canada. Seven of these are Tier III design/build certified, and located in Calgary, Ottawa, Toronto and Eastern Canada. 
Uniserve said it will continue to offer hosting at its own DC facility in Vancouver. This capability will be strictly reserved for Enterprise entities and those companies that need to be close to the downtown Vancouver business district. The remainder of the portfolio will be co-location. Uniserve can also package network systems and application management where required.
