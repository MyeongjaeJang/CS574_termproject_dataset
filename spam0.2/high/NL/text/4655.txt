Putin lays red roses at venue of deadly mall blaze in Kemerovo that killed 64 Published time: 27 Mar, 2018 06:26 Edited time: 28 Mar, 2018 07:18 Get short URL Russian President Vladimir Putin has visited the memorial set up by Kemerovo residents across from the shopping mall that became a deadly trap for 64 people on Sunday, most of whom were children. 
The Russian president laid a bouquet flowers – red roses – next to teddy bears and other toys that locals have continued to bring in since early Monday. The fatal fire, which is one of the deadliest in the post-Soviet era of Russia, completely destroyed the Kemerovo shopping center.
