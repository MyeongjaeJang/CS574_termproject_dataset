Ukrtelecom gets USD 10 mln credit line from ZTE   13:03 CET | News 
Ukrainian national operator Ukrtelecom has agreed with ZTE on a USD 10 million credit line, reports BizLigaNet. Joint projects will be funded using the credit line in the first stage, including an expansion of Ukrtelecom's fibre network and other infrastructure upgrades. Ukrtelecom will prepare the road map for joint projects ahead of the credit line taking effect from June.
