HTC's Vive Pro VR headset to go on sale in Germany on 5 April Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 13:02 CET | News 
HTC’s new Vive Pro VR headset will go on sale in stores in Germany on 05 April with a retail price of EUR 880. The new Vive Pro headset offers a higher resolution of 1440x1600 pixels per eye than Vive’s standard VR headset (1080x1200) and contains integrated headphones and a headband to better distribute the weight of the headset.
