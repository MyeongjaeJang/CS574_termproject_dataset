A total of 86 cyberattacks against government, semi-government and private sector entities in the UAE were foiled by the Telecommunications Regulations Authority (TRA) during the first two months of the year, Wam reports. The figures reveal a 66 percent decrease as compared to the corresponding period in 2017. The attacks stymied by the TRA's Computer Emergency Readiness Team reached 52 during February
