Home / News / Views sought on parental engagement from school leaders Views sought on parental engagement from school leaders EB News: 19/03/2018 - 10:30 
Parentkind, formerly PTA UK, has now joined up with Parent Councils UK to support schools directly to help engage their parents more effectively to achieve better educational outcomes for all children. 
They are keen to get views from schools leaders on the best ways to support these parental engagement efforts. 
To share your thoughts, you can simply fill in an anonymous survey at https://r1.dotmailer-surveys.com/1114x93a-da2va390
