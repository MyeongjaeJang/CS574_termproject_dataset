RCom bond holders approve asset monetization Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 10:15 CET | News Indian operator Reliance Communications (RCom) has announced that the holders of its USD 300 million bonds, at their meeting on 20 March, approved the sale of assets to Reliance Jio Infocomm and also monetization of other real estate assets.
