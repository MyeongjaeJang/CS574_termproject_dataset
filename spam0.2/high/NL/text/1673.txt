Velcom expands retail chain in Minsk   11:19 CET | News 
Belarusian mobile operator Velcom has opened its new retail outlet and service centre in the city of Minsk. The new shop is based in the commercial centre Green City. 
Customers are able to access services from the operator, as well as buy various end-user devices there. The new shop includes a demo-area for devices from Apple. 
The operator runs a total of 26 shops in the capital city.
