Ooredoo celebrates Earth Hour with launch of Galaxy S9 Monday 26 March 2018 | 14:57 CET | News 
Ooredoo Maldives celebrated Earth Hour on 24 March alongside the launch of Samsung’s latest flagship smartphone, the Galaxy S9+. Moments from Earth Hour celebrations were captured using Samsung Galaxy S9’s dual camera. 
Ooredoo customers can purchase the Samsung Galaxy S9 and S9+ combined with 3 GB of free data. Payments start from MVR 892 per month on a 12-month instalment plan, with a down payment of MVR 1,999 and any Ooredoo Postpaid Plan.
