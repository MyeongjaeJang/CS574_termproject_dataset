Jacobs leaves Qualcomm board due to possible bid   08:31 CET | News 
Qualcomm has dismissed Paul Jacobs from its board following news the former CEO and chairman was thinking of making a takeover bid for the company. He will not be re-nominated to the board at the shareholders meeting 23 March, and no replacement will be named.
