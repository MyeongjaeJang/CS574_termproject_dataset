Rostelecom to cut 10-15% of staff, invest more in innovation  39 CET | News 
Russian national operator Rostelecom plans to cut its staff by 10-15 percent over the coming five years, according to its new strategy. The company currently has 134,000 employees, with plans to reduce that number to 115,000-120,000. 
The operator plans to spend 1 percent of revenues on innovative projects, namely in the fields of IoT, AI, biometry, cyber-security and network virtualisation. 
Rostelecom also plans to merge the retail chains of Rostelecom and mobile operator Tele2 Russia.
