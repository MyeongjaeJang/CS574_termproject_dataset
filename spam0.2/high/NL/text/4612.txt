Zayo extends dark fibre network in project for Interdata Tuesday 27 March 2018 | 08:33 CET | News French IT network and security integrator Interdata has selected Zayo’s dark fibre infrastructure to provide business clients with high-speed interconnection solutions between data centres. Zayo will extend its network by an additional 50 km to support Interdata’s connectivity requirements, deploying two new dark fibre rings connecting data centres in the Ile-de-France region. The project will enable Zayo to reach a large data centre located south of Paris in Nozay, an area that will provide additional sales opportunities for the company.
