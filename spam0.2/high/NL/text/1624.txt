Rostelecom launches road traffic control system in Astrakhan   10:59 CET | News 
Russian national operator Rostelecom has launched the road traffic control system in the Astrakhan region. The system is able to identify traffic violations and manage lorry traffic. 
The project was carried out at the operator's cost, under a concession agreement signed by Rostelecom and the regional government in 2016. Rostelecom has deployed 60 new video-surveillance units and upgraded 75 units.
