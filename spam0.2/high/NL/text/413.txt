Ucell offers free data bundles with Artel phones Monday 19 March 2018 | 15:26 CET | News 
Uzbekistan mobile operator Ucell has introduced a joint promotion with the smartphone producer Artel Mobile. Customers buying Artel or General Mobile smartphones in Uzbekistan shops are offered mobile data traffic bundles free for three months.
