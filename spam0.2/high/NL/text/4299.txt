Deutsche Telekom Foundation supports university alliance with EUR 1.6 mln development grant   13:11 CET | News The Deutsche Telekom Foundation announced it is supporting a group of five universities in Germany to develop teaching concepts with digital media with a grant of EUR 1.6 million. The five universities — Berlin’s Humboldt University, the Technical University of Kaiserslautern, the Christian-Albrechts-University in Kiel, the University of Koblenz-Landau and the University of Wuerzburg — won out against 26 competitors in a nationwide competition, said Telekom. The winning universities now have three years to develop innovate ideas and teaching concepts in the STEM subjects as part of a development alliance launching this fall.
