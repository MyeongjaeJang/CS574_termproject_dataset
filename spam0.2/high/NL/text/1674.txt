Meizu introduces M6s smartphone in Russia   11:18 CET | News 
Meizu has started sales of the Meizu M6s smartphone in Russia, reports Cnews.ru. The device is based on the hexa-core Samsung Exynos 7872 processor and features a 5.7-inch screen, 16 megapixel main camera and 8 megapixel front camera. The smartphone is powered by the Android 7 OS. 
The price of a model with 32 GB internal memory is RUB 14,990, and the version of the device with 64 GB memory totals RUB 16,990.
