Costa Rica's ICE expands LTE-A, fibre networks Monday 19 March 2018 | 11:08 CET | News 
Costa Rica's state-owned operator ICE has announced a further expansion of the LTE-A and fibre-optic service available under its Kolbi brand. In separate statements, the company said customers in areas such as Limon, Puntarenas, Alajuela, Cartago, Ciudad Quesada, Paso Canoas, Alto de Guadalupe, Paseo Colon, La Sabana, Tibas, La Uruca and Montelimar could now access its recently-activated LTE-A network offering speeds of up to 50Mbps download and 30Mbps upload at no extra cost.
