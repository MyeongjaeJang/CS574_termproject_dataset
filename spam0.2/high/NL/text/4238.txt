Velcom expands pay-TV offer with Discovery channels Monday 26 March 2018 | 12:59 CET | News 
Belarusian mobile operator Velcom has expanded its pay-TV offer under the Voka TV brand with the channels Discovery Channel, Animal Planet and Eurosport 1. 
The Voka TV service is available for subscribers of fixed and mobile internet services from Velcom, as well as for users of internet services from other operators. A total of 110 channels are available over the GPON network, and 85 channels can be watched on other networks.
