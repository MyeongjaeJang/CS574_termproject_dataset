Facebook agrees new music licensing deals with Sacem Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 11:31 CET | News 
French music rights association Sacem announced that it has signed three new licensing deals with Facebook, setting the terms for the remuneration by the social network to the rights holders represented by Sacem. The music libraries covered by the multi-year agreements are those of Sacem, Wixen Music Publishing and Canadian rights group Socan. In December 2017, Sacem brokered a similar deal on behalf of Universal Music Publishing International, as part of an agreement between Facebook and Universal Music .
