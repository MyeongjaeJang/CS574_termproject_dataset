Rostelecom, Nokia launch 5G testing area in Skolkovo Thursday 22 March 2018 | 10:24 CET | News 
Russian national operator Rostelecom, in cooperation with Nokia and the Skolkovo fund, has launched a 5G testing area in the Skolkovo innovation park in Moscow. It is the first such area in the country. Companies and research institutions developing products for 5G have been invited to joint testing of equipment at the site.
