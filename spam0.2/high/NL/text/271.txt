IP-Only promotes Westerberg to replace Ringmar as CEO Monday 19 March 2018 | 12:35 CET | News 
Swedish operator IP-Only said it is promoting its deputy CEO Frida Westerberg to the role of president and CEO. She replaces Henrik Ringmar, who will remain with the company for a handover period. Westerberg's promotion comes as the company changes focus from acquiring residential customers, a maturing market, to the growing business sector. 
IP-Only chairman Gunnar Asp said the firm has expanded through acquisitions and organic growth, and is entering a new expansion phase. He said Westerberg has the competence and leadership skills required for the next stage.
