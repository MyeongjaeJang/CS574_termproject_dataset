Beltelecom reaches 100,000 users of self-service app   15:33 CET | News 
Belarusian national operator Beltelecom announced that over 100,000 of its customers are using its self-service mobile application 'My Beltelecom'. It passed the milestone in March after launching the in September 2017. 
The application enables customers to activate, manage and disconnect services, as well as to top up their credit. Versions of the application for Android and iOS-based devices are available.
