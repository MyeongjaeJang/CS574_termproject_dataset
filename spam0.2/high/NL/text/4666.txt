Austria's A1 launches #BusinessChange digitisation campaign Monday 26 March 2018 | 15:37 CET | News 
Austrian operator A1 has launched a print and placard campaign called #BusinessChange to advertise how its product and service portfolio can help companies digitise their businesses. The company has also launched an online content platform as part of the campaign to help inform companies about the opportunities and challenges of digitisation. A1 customers who have already digitised their business processes, including the Vienna airport and the Bruendlmayer winery, will provide testimonials of the experience working with A1 for the campaign.
