Antenna Hungaria offers uncoded access to National Geographic Monday 26 March 2018 | 20:21 CET | News Antenna Hungaria said it started offering uncoded access to the National Geographic channel to nearly 750,000 MinDig TV households between 23 March and 22 April. The company earlier announced it's also offering free access to Viasat3 until 15 April following an agreement with Sony Pictures Television CE.
