Telkom soft-launches online store   10:33 CET | News 
Telkom has soft-launched a new, online shop at store.telkom.co.za, reports Mybroadband. The site has product categories including smartphones and accessories, computers and tablets, TV and multimedia, and connectivity. The soft launch follows Telkom sending a survey to customers in July 2017, asking them if they were interested in buying electronics through its website.
