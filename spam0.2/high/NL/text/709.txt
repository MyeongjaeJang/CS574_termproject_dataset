Pharrell Visited an Elephant Rescue Center in India to Spread the Word About Protecting These Animals - One Green Planet www.onegreenplanet.org The rescued elephants living at Wildlife SOS in New Delhi, India recently had a very special guest! Grammy-award winning rapper, singer, producer, and all-around awesome person, Pharrell Williams stopped by to say hello and lend his voice to the plight of elephants.
