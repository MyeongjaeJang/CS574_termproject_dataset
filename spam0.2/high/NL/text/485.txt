Bezeq raises ILS 320 mln private loan   15:24 CET | News 
Israel operator Bezeq said it has raised a ILS 320 million private loan from an institutional entity. In a statement, Bezeq said the money was raised under terms similar to an earlier private loan from the banking institution, including a duration of 6.7 years and a fixed annual interest rate of 3.2 percent. 
On 15 January, Bezeq signed an agreement for a future placement of its series nine bonds and secured a ILS 600 million bank loan. The operator said it would be expanding its series nine bond issue by ILS 578 million.
