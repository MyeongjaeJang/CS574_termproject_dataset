Rostelecom debuts outdoor video-surveillance service in Tver  27 CET | News 
Russian national operator Rostelecom has started providing outdoor video-surveillance services in the Tver region. Users can access video content in real-time; it will be stored at the operator's data centres. Customers are offered water and temperatures-resistant outdoor video-cameras, the access to a cloud platform from the operator and a mobile application with the online interface. The standard rate of the camera totals RUB 5,990, customers are offered to purchase it on installment under a two-year contract for RUB 300 monthly rate.
