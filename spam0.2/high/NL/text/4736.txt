Germany's M-Net to broadcast Bavarian programming in HD   11:02 CET | News 
German regional operator M-Net said it will begin broadcasting twelve local Bavarian-based TV stations, including Muenchen.TV, Main TV and Niederbayern TV, in HD for its cable customers beginning 28 March. The stations will be broadcast on the channels S26 and 47 in cable networks in Munich, Augsburg, Wurzburg and Erlangen. Customers will have to run a station search on their digital TV devices to be able to receive the channels. 
M-Net said it will continue to broadcast the channels in analogue and digital SD formats as well.
