Mediacom Business to increase broadband speeds of some business tariff plans Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 15:48 CET | News Mediacom Business said it's increasing broadband speeds for tens of thousands of business customers nationwide from 1 April. Existing Mediacom Business customers in the 10 Mbps, 20 Mbps and 50 Mbps tiers of service will be upgraded to new 60 Mbps, 100 Mbps and 300 Mbps tiers, respectively. Customers will not be charged extra for this five to six-fold increase in their broadband speeds. 
In 2016, Mediacom announced a 3-year, USD 1 billion capital investment plan to, among other projects, upgrade and enhance the capacity of its fibre-rich digital network.
