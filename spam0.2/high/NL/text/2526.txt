Vodafone Spain cuts prices of second mobile lines Thursday 22 March 2018 | 10:26 CET | News Vodafone Spain has announced a series of reductions for users requiring more than one mobile line in a bid to compete with rivals such as Movistar and Yoigo. The new prices for additional mobile lines associated to the main contract are available to users who port their number from another operator. A second ‘+Lineas yu’ line with 60 minutes of calls, 3.5GB of data and access to zero-rated Chat Pass and Social Pass now costs EUR 10 compared to EUR 20 for the main line, while the ‘+Lineas S’, ‘+Lineas M’ and ‘+Lineas L’ lines now cost EUR 15, EUR 20 and EUR 25 respectively.
