Vodafone India expands VoLTE service to Uttar Pradesh West   15:16 CET | News Vodafone India has rolled out its Voice over LTE (VoLTE) service in Haryana. The service is currently available in the cities of Meerut, Agra, Dehradun, Bareilly, Saharanpur, Aligarh, Haldwani & Moradabad. Vodafone SuperNet 4G customers can access Vodafone VoLTE at no additional charges; all calls will be billed according to the customer's existing plan or pack benefits. Vodafone's VoLTE service in currently available in Mumbai, Delhi-NCR, Rajasthan, Gujarat and Maharastra & Goa, Haryana, Tamil Nadu, UP East, and will be gradually extended across the country over the next few months.
