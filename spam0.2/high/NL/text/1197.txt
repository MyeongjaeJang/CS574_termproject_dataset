Saint-Gobain Strengthens Its Glass Solutions Portfolio WEBWIRE – Tuesday, March 20, 2018 
Saint-Gobain has acquired Italian company Logli Massimo, the specialist in glass systems and accessories for the fixing and assembly of glass panels, in particular for railings, shower doors, partition walls and interior and exterior doors. 
Founded in 1989, today the company employs nearly 60 people. Already the leader in Italy, it is developing its position in France, Benelux and a number of other European countries. Integration with the Saint-Gobain Group will enable the company to accelerate its growth in Europe by being able to offer complete solutions to architects and tradespeople. 
This acquisition is consistent with the Groups strategy of expanding its range of downstream products towards high value-added solutions.
