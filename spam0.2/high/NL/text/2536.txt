Telia Sweden is running a promotion with HBO Nordic at one of its branch on Kungsgatan in central Stockholm from 23 to 24 March. To mark the start of series eight of HBO’s fantasy adventure serial ’Game of Thrones, Telia will be displaying the Iron Throne from Kings Landing in Westeros at the store. 
The operator added that it is making its streaming service available on mobile devices, enabling customers to watch TV and films wherever and whenever they want to through a Telia Play application. It said Telia Play provides fifteen television channels, both live and archived, as well as content from HBO Nordic, SF Kids, National Geographic Plus, and 10,000 other films for SEK 149 per month. Children's content carries no advertising.
