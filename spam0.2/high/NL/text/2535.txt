1&1 starts pre-orders for Huawei MediaPad M5 Pro tablet  30 CET | News 
Germany’s 1&1 is now taking pre-orders for the Huawei MediaPad M5 Pro tablet. The tablet can be combined with 1&1 Tablet Flat L tariff for EUR 16.99 per month and a one-time fee of EUR 249.99, while customers can also combine the MediaPad M5 Pro with 1&1’s Tablet Flat XL tariff for EUR 24.99 per month and no extra surcharge. Customers who pre-order the tablet with 1&1 will also receive a keyboard case valued at EUR 129 at no extra cost. 1&1 will begin shipping the tablet to customers at the end of March.
