NTT Com appoints SVP in charge of 2020 Project Tuesday 27 March 2018 | 10:16 CET | News NTT Communications (NTT Com) has announced changes in its organizational responsibilities. NTT Com has appointed Tetsuya Funabashi as senior executive vice-president in charge of the 2020 Project. Funabashi will also maintain his current responsibilities as senior executive vice-president in charge of technology, services, operations and information security.
