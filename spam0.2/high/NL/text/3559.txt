Megafon expands network in North-Western Russia Friday 23 March 2018 | 12:47 CET | News 
Russian mobile operator Megafon built 1,000 GSM, 3G and LTE base stations in North-Western Russia in 2017, reports Tdaily.ru. The operator also upgraded its network there.
