Court frees three accused of leaving for Somalia unlawfully March 20, 2018 0 
A Mandera court yesterday freed three persons accused of departing the country to Somalia unlawfully. 
The court heard from the prosecution that the three left the country via undesignated departure points on the porous Kenya-Somalia border. 
Hussein Abdi Ali, Bashar Mohamed Ahmed and Mohamed Huka Ibrahim were arrested by corporal Hussein and Sergeant Mohamed Maalim on November 14 last year at 11 am at Border Point 3.
