Ooredoo Qatar launches Aamali Mobile business plans   10:54 CET | News 
Ooredoo Qatar has launched Aamali Mobile, a service for businesses. Enterprises can gain full control of their mobile allowances by selecting tailored packages to meet the different needs of their employees. The mobile plans include varying allowances and add-on options for local and international call minutes, data and roaming minutes. 
Administrators can access employee usage reports instantly and can upgrade or downgrade their services at a click or swipe.
