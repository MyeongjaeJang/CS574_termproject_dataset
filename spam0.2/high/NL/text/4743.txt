Digiweb names former Microsoft exec Rellis as new CEO   11:16 CET | News 
Irish operator Digiweb Group announced the appointment of Paul Rellis as its new CEO. He is the former Chief Operations Officer for Microsoft Western Europe and former head of Microsoft Ireland and also led the Irish business association Ibec and the American Chamber of Commerce in Ireland. 
Colm Piercy, Digiweb founder, will move from CEO to chairman of the group, as he focuses more on his technology and property investments. He has led the company for 21 years, helping it expand to over 120 employees and a range of broadband and managed services for businesses under the Viatel brand and for residential customers under the Digiweb brand.
