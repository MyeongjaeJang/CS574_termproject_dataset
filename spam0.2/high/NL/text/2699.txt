Next Dutch Hackathon will take place in May   17:36 CET | News Seven Dutch organizations are organising the Dutch Open Hackathon for the fourth time on 26 and 27 May. The partners involved -including KPN and SIDN- propose to stimulate and support open innovation and technological progress in the Netherlands through this event. During the hackathon, participants will be challenged to devise creative solutions and applications in 36 hours, for concrete, everyday challenges.The Dutch Open Hackathon 2018 is an initiative of Big Data Innovation Hub, the Land Registry, Royal Institute of Engineers (KIVI), KPN, Police, PostNL and SIDN. 
The event will take place at the Dutch Innovation Factory in Zoetermeer. The hackathon will focus on four elements: At Home, Doing Business, In The City and On The Go.
