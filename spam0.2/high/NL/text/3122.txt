Inexio rolls out 100 Mbps internet across Germany in latest round of upgrades   13:51 CET | News German regional operator Inexio announced plans to roll out broadband internet to households in the towns of Weilheim (280 households), Nunkirchen (400 households), Nempitz (600 households), Oestringen and Leitersweiler. The company said it has already begun construction works in some of these locations, with the first broadband connections to be activated over the next few weeks.
