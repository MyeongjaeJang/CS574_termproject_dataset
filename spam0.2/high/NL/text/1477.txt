Samsung launches first 3D LED screen theater in Switzerland Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 07:58 CET | News Samsung Electronics has launched its first 3D LED screen theater in Switzerland. The Samsung cinema screen is available at Arena Cinemas, located inside Sihlcity shopping mall. The 3D Cinema LED is 10.3 meters wide and 5.4 meters tall, supports HD quality videos, and comes with High Dynamic Range capabilities. Both 2D and 3D movies can be played on the screen. 
Samsung introduced the Cinema LED screen in 2017, supplying related services to theaters in its home market and China. The latest installment marks the debut of the service in Europe, as well as the first global showcase of the 3D version.
