Hub One launches MyCybersecurity consultancy service for SMEs  23 CET | News 
French business ICT company Hub One has announced the launch of a consultancy service to assess the cyber-security needs of small and medium businesses. MyCybersecurity fits within Hub One's medium-term strategy to grow revenues by expanding into the cyber-security and software development sectors. The new consultancy service will target businesses working in areas where the company already has a strong presence, such as transport and logistics, distribution, and the airport industry.
