Cambridgeshire, Peterborough get GBP 5.5 mln digital connectivity boost   15:24 CET | News 
The Cambridgeshire and Peterborough Combined Authority (CPCA) in the UK will use a board meeting on 28 March to discuss plans to invest up to GBP 5.5 million in digital connectivity, including tackling poor mobile coverage. 
Agenda papers published for the meeting include plans for improving mobile, public Wi-Fi and broadband coverage for everyone. If the Combined Authority Board approves the funding, it will be used to expand the Connecting Cambridgeshire programme.
