Netia opens pop-up shops in Warsaw   10:28 CET | News 
Polish operator Netia has opened four pop-up stores in Warsaw in order to connect better with customers. They are located at the shopping malls Gallery Bemowo and Gallery Renova (Targowek), as well as the Tesco outlets Goclaw and Kabaty. 
The shops will promote the operator's latest offers, such as broadband with TV for PLN 49.90 per month, which is also the subject of a TV ad campaign. The shops are open work days as well as during Sunday shopping.
