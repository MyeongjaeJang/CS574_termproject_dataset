The residents of Kassala have complained about the deterioration of the health environment in the educational hospital and the lack of general doctors in addition to the interruption of the CT scanner for months. 
Residents from Kassala said the state government had not been able to repair the CT scanner for months, noting that patients are traveling to other states to complete diagnosis. 
They have pointed out to the high cost of magnetic resonance imaging in Kassala. 
They said that Kassala teaching hospital suffers from a great shortage of general doctors and deterioration in the hospital environment, pointing to the suspension of appointment of doctors without explaining the reasons. They have called on the state government to care for health services in the state.
