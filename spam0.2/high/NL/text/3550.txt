Vodafone Turkey provides mobile communication infrastructure to Royal Balloon Friday 23 March 2018 | 17:43 CET | News 
Vodafone Turkey was contracted to provide mobile communication infrastructure to Royal Balloon, a company providing hot air balloon rides in Cappadocia.
