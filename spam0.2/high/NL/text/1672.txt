InterDigital supplies FLIPS platform for UK 5G Smart Tourism trial   11:31 CET | News 
Mobile technology R&D company InterDigital has confirmed its participation in the UK 5G Smart Tourism Project, led by the West of England Combined Authority. The project will see 5G infrastructure deployed at the Roman Baths in the city of Bath, as well as in Bristol at the M Shed museum, in and around the 'We the Curious' science centre, and in Millennium Square. 
InterDigital is working with Bristol is Open (BIO) on the project, which will see the company's FLIPS MEC 5G network architecture used to support virtual and augmented reality cases in the tourism space. The 5G Smart Tourism Project is one of six projects selected by the UK Government to set up 5G testbeds.
