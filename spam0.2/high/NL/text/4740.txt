Hetzner will not pass on VAT rise as higher prices   10:57 CET | News 
South African hosting company Hetzner said that its hosting fees will not be affected by the increase in VAT on 01 April. It said that it had decided to adsorb the costs as it does not believe that the change in the VAT rate is an adequate trigger for a price increase. 
As global input costs continue to increase, it may not be able to avoid price adjustments indefinitely, it said. Hetzner added that it regularly reviews its pricing and measures the necessity of an increase against certain factors. Hetzner said it is always looking for the perfect price-to-performance ratio and regularly reviews its pricing against various factors.
