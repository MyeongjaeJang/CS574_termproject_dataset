Filiago offers discount on satellite internet tariffs through May   15:08 CET | News German satellite internet provider Filiago has slashed the prices of its Filiago 4.0 L, XL and Premium Unlimited tariffs for customers who register for satellite internet through 31 May. Under the promotion, customers pay a reduced price of EUR 29.95 per month for the first three months for all three tariffs, enabling savings of up to EUR 45 per month depending on the selected tariff. 
All three tariffs offer transmission speeds up to 30 Mbps and vary in terms of data volume. New customers can also receive three months of telephone service for free to assist with installation, while households can also pay an additional EUR 49 to have Filiago service technicians install their satellite equipment.
