Azerbaijan to launch upgrade of POS terminals   15:28 CET | News 
Azerbaijan plans to carry out a project on the upgrade of POS terminals used across the country, reports Trend. Imports of POS terminals will need to meet the requirements of all Azerbaijan banks. 
A total of 65,181 POS terminals were installed across the country at the end of January, including 36,801 POS in the capitol Baku. The amount of POS terminals was down by 8.4 percent year-on-year.
