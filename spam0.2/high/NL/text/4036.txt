Fastweb launches new Digital Academy courses Friday 23 March 2018 | 15:01 CET | News 
Italian operator Fastweb has announced the launch of a new set of courses aimed at young Italians looking for their first job, women and SMEs at its Fastweb Digital Academy. The operator said the initiative it set up with partner Fondazione Cariplo has helped create some 1,600 job opportunities in just 18 months of activity. The programme covers four different areas – Digital Design & Making, Digital Development, Digital Marketing & Collaboration, and Digital Soft Skill – with dedicated SME courses available for the first time in Milan and Palermo and soon in Pescara and Bari. The new courses start at the end of March.
