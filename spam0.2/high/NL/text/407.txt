Sparkle, Positive Technologies roll out signalling protection service   13:39 CET | News 
Sparkle, the international services arm of the Telecom Italia Group, has joined forces with Positive Technologies to launch suite of security services designed specifically for signalling connectivity. The Sparkle Signalling Protection Suite is an all-in-one package that protects the signalling network of mobile operators by detecting and blocking unauthorised traffic and preventing potential privacy issues, operational instability and revenue losses, said the company. The offering gives customers a set of advanced security services such as vulnerability testing, best practice compliance assessment, signalling firewall and security monitoring for real time detection of anomalous activities, added the partners.
