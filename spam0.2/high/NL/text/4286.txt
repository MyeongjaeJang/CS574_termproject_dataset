Volia opens new service centre in Ivano-Frankivsk   14:15 CET | News 
Ukrainian cable operator Vokia has opened a new retail outlet and service centre in the city of Ivano-Frankivsk. The new shop is based in the city centre and includes a demonstration area, self-service area and other premises. The operator has already opened seventeen such service centres across the country and has invested UAH 10 million in the centres in three years.
