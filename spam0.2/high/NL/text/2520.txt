Nearly 600,000 sign up to Claro Argentina 'roam like home' plans Thursday 22 March 2018 | 12:00 CET | News 
Claro Argentina announced that nearly 600,000 of its customers used its 'roam like home' offer over the summer months (December 2017 to February 2018). The service allows customers to pay local rates for data services throughout the American continent, including the US and Canada. Brazil, Chile, Paraguay and Uruguay were the destinations with the highest number of roaming users, said Claro. 
The America-wide roaming offer is available to customers subscribing to plans with 4GB, 7GB and 10GB of data. Incoming voice calls and SMS are free of charge while outgoing voice minutes and SMS are charged at local rates and Whatsapp use is zero-rated. Delegates from 19 countries in North, South and Central America and the Caribbean jointly committed to removing roaming fees throughout the American continent by 2022 at a recent meeting of the Inter-American Telecommunication Commission (CITEL) in Buenos Aires.
