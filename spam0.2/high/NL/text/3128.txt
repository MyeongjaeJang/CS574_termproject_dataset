Rostelecom expands PBX coverage in Vologda   12:46 CET | News 
Russian national operator Rostelecom has expanded the coverage of its PBX services in the Vologda region to the towns of Velikiy-Ustyg, Sokol, Sheksna and Babayevo. Business subscribers were already able to use the services in the cities of Vologda and Cherepovets. Some 250 business customers currently use the PBX service in the region.
