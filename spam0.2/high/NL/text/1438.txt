Gemalto provides biometric facial recognition for Dai Nippon m-banking platform Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 05:53 CET | News Digital security company Gemalto has been selected by Dai Nippon Printing (DNP), a financial services provider in Japan, to support mobile banking transactions on the local market. Leveraging Gemalto's facial biometric authentication service dubbed 'Mobile Protector', DNP has started to offer facial recognition to secure access to its mobile banking apps.
