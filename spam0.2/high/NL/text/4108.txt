Mobilezone rights issue nearly fully subscribed   09:33 CET | News 
Swiss retailer Mobilezone announced that 99.9 percent of the shares in its rights offering were taken up for purchase by shareholders. The sale of the nearly 8.6 million shares will result in gross proceeds of CHF 81.6 million for its planned acquisition of TPHCom . 
The new shares are expected to start trading on 27 March.
