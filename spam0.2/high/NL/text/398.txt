TIM Brasil names Cox as new chairman   14:54 CET | News 
TIM Brasil announced the resignation of its chairman Mario Cesar Pereira de Araujo and board member Manoel Horacio Francisco da Silva. As replacements, also for Enrico Zampone who resigned in February, the company named Joao Cox Neto, Celso Luis Loducca and Piergiorgio Peluso to the board. Cox, who was previously CEO of Telemig Celular and Claro Brasil, has been named the new chairman. 
The company also received resignation letters from Enrico Barsotti, Nicoletta Montella and Sabrina Valenza, who will step down from the board after the AGM on 19 April. Replacements will be named at the shareholders meeting.
