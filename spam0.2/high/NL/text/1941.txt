Synaptics ID fingerprint sensors featured on Vivo X21 UD smartphones  15 CET | News 
Synaptics announced its FS9500 Clear ID optical in-display fingerprint sensors are built-in the new Vivo X21 UD smartphone. The X21 UD is Vivo’s second-generation smartphone featuring Clear ID following the X20 Plus UD phone that was showcased at CES 2018 with Synaptics and now sold at retail. Synaptics said its FS9500 Clear ID optical in-display fingerprint sensor family is the world’s first mass production sensor for smartphones.
