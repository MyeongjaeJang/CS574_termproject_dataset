DocuSign files for IPO - report   11:53 CET | News Digital transaction platform DocuSign has filed for an IPO, TechCrunch reported, saying the public offering was filed behind closed doors. The company is aiming to launch the IPO in the second or third quarter of this year. 
DocuSign has raised over USD 500 million since its debut in 2003. Investors include Kleiner Perkins, Bain Capital, Intel Capital, GV (Google Ventures) and Dell.
