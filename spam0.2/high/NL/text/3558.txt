Vodafone Czech Republic targets seniors with new shop trainee scheme Friday 23 March 2018 | 12:52 CET | News 
Vodafone Czech Republic wants to invite retired seniors to participate in a pilot project of senior trainees working in the operator's shops. In cooperation with the agency McCann Prague, the operator first wants to examine whether seniors would be interested in such jobs. After training in smart technologies and the internet, senior trainees could also attract more senior customers to the operator's shops.
