Named Chairman, CEO At Aimbridge Hospitality in Plano - TX , USA 
Dave Johnson has taken on the additional title of chairman of Aimbridge Hospitality in addition to his current position of CEO, while Robert Burg has taken on the additional role of president in addition to his current position of COO. Aimbridge's board of directors asked Johnson, Aimbridge's current CEO and co-founder, to step into the role of chairman with former co-founder Les Bentley's retirement.
