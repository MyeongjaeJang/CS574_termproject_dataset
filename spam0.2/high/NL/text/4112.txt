Sentech appoints Leshope as new COO Monday 26 March 2018 | 10:10 CET | News 
South African state-owned signal distributor Sentech has appointed Tebogo Leshope as chief operations officer (COO). Leshope has been with Sentech for the past nineteen years, coming through the ranks. Leshope possesses telecommunications experience, with proven leadership capabilities in highly complex operations and telecommunications projects. 
He is qualified in electrical engineering, with a Bachelor of Technology degree from the University of Johannesburg, Project Management from UNISA and is a registered professional with the Engineering Council of South Africa, South African Institute of Electrical Engineers and the Institute of Directors Southern Africa.
