Tele2 Russia customers to get ad-free Wi-Fi on Moscow metro  26 CET | News 
Mobile operator Tele2 Russia and the Wi-Fi operator MaximaTelecom have introduced a new service in Moscow, reports Cnews.ru. The service enables Moscow subscribers of Tele2 to access the Wi-Fi network of MaximaTelecom on the Moscow Metro without watching advertising. The option will be available for two months from activation. 
MaximaTelecom offers its ad-free service on a paid basis normally.
