Dialog, Energizer launch 4G feature phone in Sri Lanka Friday 23 March 2018 | 11:28 CET | News Sri Lanka operator Dialog Axiata will be the exclusive network partner and 4G partner for the latest range of Energizer 4G devices. The releases by Energizer include a 4G feature phone, along with two additional 4G smartphone launches. The partnership enables Dialog to launch hard case phones in Sri Lanka. 
The recent line of 4G smartphones include the H240 4G feature phone, the Energy 400 LTE 4G smartphone and the Energy E5250 LTE 4G smartphone. All devices are 4G, shock proof, dust proof and water proof. The H240 4G feature phone includes a 2000mAh battery, 1GB RAM and 8GB internal storage. 
The Energy 400 LTE includes a 2500mAh battery, dual SIM capabilities, an LCD capacitive screen and 1GB RAM with 8GB internal storage. The H240 and the Energy 400 LTE runs on Android 6.0 Marshmallow. The Energy E520 LTE includes fingerprint detection, a 5.2-inch HD screen, 2GB RAM and 16GB internal storage. The device also comes with a 13-megapixel rear camera, 5-megapixel front camera, and 4000mAh battery. 
The Energizer devices are available at Dialog Customer Care Centres across Sri Lanka. The Energy E5250 LTE 4G smartphone is on sale for LKR 14,575, the Energy 400 LTE costs LKR 28,990, and the Energy E520 LTE is available for LKR 42,990. Free Headlines in your E-mail
