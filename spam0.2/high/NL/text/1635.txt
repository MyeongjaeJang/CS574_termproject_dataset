Canal+ launches DTH service in Myanmar on Thaicom 6 broadcast platform Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 10:18 CET | News Asian satellite operator Thaicom has signed a multi-year contract with Canal+ Overseas Myanmar, the Canal+ subsidiary in charge of broadcasting internationally and in Myanmar. Canal+ is part of France-based media company Vivendi. The contract is part of Canal+ Myanmar's launch of a pay-TV DTH service platform. Canal+ Overseas Myanmar has leased 4 transponders on the Thaicom 6 satellite and broadcast platform on Ku-band that will be used to deliver a new bouquet of initially 80 channels.
