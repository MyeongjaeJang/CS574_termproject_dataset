Aknet raises broadband speed   11:38 CET | News 
Kyrgyzstan ISP Aknet has raised broadband speeds for subscribers of its 'iTV Unlim Family' tariff line. The speed increases to 40 Mbps, and the speed caps have also been removed. Subscription rates are unchanged. 
The tariff line includes broadband, as well as pay-TV services. Up to 180 TV channels are available for subscribers.
