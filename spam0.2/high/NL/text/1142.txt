Portuguese telecom prices continue higher in February   09:22 CET | News 
Telecommunication prices in Portugal increased by 0.17 percent in February compared to the previous month and rose 0.35 percent year-on-year, according to the latest figures from Anacom. The monthly change was due to a new mobile broadband tariff at one provider. 
On an average basis over the past 12 months, prices rose 2.07 percent, or 0.81 percent points over inflation in Portugal. Telecom prices have been rising faster than the CPU since the start of 2014, the regulator said.
