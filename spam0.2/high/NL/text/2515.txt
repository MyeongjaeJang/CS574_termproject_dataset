Ucom offers new top-up option on U!Pay Thursday 22 March 2018 | 13:27 CET | News 
Armenian operator Ucom has introduced to its customers an opportunity to top-up their balances over the electronic wallet U!Pay, reports Arka.am. The option can be used by downloading the U!Pay application onto mobile devices.
