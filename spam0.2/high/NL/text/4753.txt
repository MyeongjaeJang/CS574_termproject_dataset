Telefonica fixed line services to be sold under Movistar brand in Argentina   11:21 CET | News 
Telefonica Argentina has announced that its 3 local commercial operations will be unified under the Movistar brand starting on 03 April. As in many of its other markets, the Movistar brand will be used for Telefonica and Speedy broadband and fixed line packages, while the OnVideo VoD and streaming service will be rebranded as Movistar Cine. 
The company has yet to launch pay-TV services in Argentina but in January regulator Enacom authorised the company to offer cable TV in Buenos Aires and the surrounding province as well as in Godoy Cruz, Guaymayen, Lujan de Cuyo and Mendoza.
