Slovak Telekom introduces carrier billing for Apple Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 13:09 CET | News 
Slovak Telekom has introduced payments in the App Store, Apple Music, iTunes and iBooks directly from pre-paid credit or in invoices for mobile services. The payment option is automatically available to all existing customers, both pre-paid and contract. 
The new payment option is easy to use. Customers using an Apple ID choose the payment option in the account settings for App Store, Apple Music, iTunes and iBooks on their iPhone, iPad, iPod touch and Mac, or iTunes on a PC.
