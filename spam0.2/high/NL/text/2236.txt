Greece consults on progress NGA network implementation Thursday 22 March 2018 | 07:23 CET | News 
Greek telecommunications authority EETT launched a national public consultation on the draft regulation on submission of periodic reports by providers on the progress of implementation of NGA networks and introducing VDSL vectoring into the access network. Responses must be submitted by the end of April. The authority also said it amended Annex 2B of its 813/004 decision from 7 June 2017 (Decision B) regarding the date of the implementation of the new generation networks which Vodafone and Wind intend to implement in certain outdoor cabinets.
