South Korea tops 10 million used handsets sold in 2017 Tuesday 27 March 2018 | 06:51 CET | News The number of used handsets sold in South Korea passed the 10 million units milestone in 2017, Yonhap news agency reports, citing data provided by Good Mobile and UPM. The study shows the combined volume of used mobile phones reached 10.5 million units in 2017, with a total value estimated at KRW 1.6 trillion. The figures translate to an average price of KRW 159,000. Monthly average sales volume reached 880,000 units. The report also shows that monthly volume hit nearly 1.15 million units in September 2017, when Samsung's Galaxy Note 8 went on sale.
