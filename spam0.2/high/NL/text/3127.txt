Mail.ru expands in e-sports with acquisition of ESforce   12:48 CET | News 
Russian internet company Mail.ru Group has acquired the e-sports holding company ESforce, reports Vedomosti. Mail.ru Group plans to consolidate ESforce in full. 
The company Highland Falls Investment and Russian businessman Anton Cherepennikov are the sellers. Analysts estimate the deal is worth around USD 100 million.
