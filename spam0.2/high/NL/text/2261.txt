Rostelecom presents dividend strategy for 2018-2020  42 CET | News 
Russian national operator Rostelecom plans to allocate at least 75 percent of its Free Cash Flow (FCF) for dividends in 2018-2020. The new strategy was approved by the management board of the operator. The dividends will be paid to owners of both privileged and ordinary shares. The minimum amount of the dividends per ordinary share will total RUB 5; the maximum amount of a sum allocated for annual dividends will not exceed 100 percent of its net profit.
