Vodafone Portugal starts campaign on Rock in Rio sponsorship Monday 19 March 2018 | 10:21 CET | News 
Vodafone Portugal announced the start of a multimedia marketing campaign to highlight its sponsorship of the Rock in Rio Lisbon festival. The company is displaying large guitars of 7m high at popular spots around Lisbon in the 100 days up to the event.
