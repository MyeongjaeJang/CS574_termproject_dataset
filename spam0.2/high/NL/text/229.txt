Ooredoo and Zain start selling Samsung Galaxy S9 and S9 Plus in Kuwait   10:08 CET | News 
Ooredoo Kuwait has begun selling the Galaxy S9 and S9 Plus in conjunction with Samsung Al Babtain. On launch day on 14 March, they offered free unlimited minutes, free unlimited SMS and 500 GB of mobile internet with either phone, starting at KWD 22.5. 
The smartphones are available in Ooredoo branches throughout Kuwait and its official distributors, in addition to its online shop. The Galaxy S9 is available with a plan starting at KWD 31.5 monthly including free unlimited minutes and SMS, with 100 GB of mobile internet. The S9 Plus starts from KWD 34.5 per month.
