Neotel offers faster internet in first year on new contracts  38 CET | News 
Macedonian operator Neotel announced a new offer to double internet speeds on new contracts. Customers who take a two-year contract for the Fiber 50 or neoTrio 50 packages will get double the speed, at 100 Mbps, in the first year for no extra cost. With the Fiber 100 and neoTrio 100 packages, the speed triples to 300 Mbps in the first year. The promotional offer lasts until 15 April.
