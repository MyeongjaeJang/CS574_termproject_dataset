Starbucks to Webcast 2018 Annual Meeting of Shareholders SEATTLE – WEBWIRE – Tuesday, March 20, 2018 
Starbucks Corporation ( NASDAQ: SBUX ) will hold its 26th Annual Meeting of Shareholders on Wednesday, March 21, 2018 at 10:00 a.m. PT. 
The event will be webcast and can be accessed at http://investor.starbucks.com . A replay of the webcast will be available on the companys website until Friday, September 21, 2018.
