Singtel, Twilio to jointly develop IoT platform for app developers Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 10:12 CET | News Singapore operator Singtel will cooperate with cloud communications platform Twilio to jointly develop an Internet of Things (IoT) platform. The platform will seek to make IoT more accessible to software developers and accelerate the adoption of IoT solutions in Singapore and the Asia-Pacific region, Singtel said. 
The partnership will leverage Singtel’s IoT network in Singapore and Twilio’s Programmable Wireless product to enable developers to manage the cellular connectivity of their connected device over a set of application programming interfaces (APIs). Under the terms of this partnership agreement, developers and enterprises will also be able to use Singtel IoT services for connectivity, network security, device management and advanced analytics.
