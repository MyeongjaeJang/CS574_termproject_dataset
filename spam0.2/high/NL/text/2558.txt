SSNf names Oresundskraft local network of the year   14:58 CET | News 
Swedish metropolitan grid association SSNf has named Helsingborg-based Oresundskraft as Local Network of the Year at the SSNF annual conference in Umea. The Robust Fibre intiative was named as the best broadband promoter.
