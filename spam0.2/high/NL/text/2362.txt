FremantleMedia CEO to join YouTube as EMEA head   11:03 CET | News 
European media business RTL Group announced that Cecile Frot-Coutaz will be stepping down as CEO of subsidiary FremantleMedia. It has started a process to select a new CEO and said it was talking with her to ensure a smooth transition. According to Variety, she will join YouTube as its head of operations in Europe, the Middle East and Africa (EMEA).
