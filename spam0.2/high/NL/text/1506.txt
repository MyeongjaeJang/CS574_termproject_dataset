Mtel, Telenor Bulgaria take pre-orders for Xperia XZ2  13 CET | News Bulgarian operators and Telenor Bulgaria started accepting pre-orders for Sony's new flagship Xperia XZ2 smartphone . Mtel customers who order the device online at mtel.bg from 20 March to 3 April will get it with a Sony WH-1000XM2 wireless headset. Telenor is also offering those who buy the phone between 10 an 23 April the chance to win 20 of these headsets. 
The smartphone will be available in Mtel's retail network for BGN 49.99 per month or for BGN 999.99 in cash with a subscription plan for the ' Mtel
