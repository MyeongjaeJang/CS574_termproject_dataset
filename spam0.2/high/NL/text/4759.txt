PLDT to pay dividend of PHP 28 per share   11:53 CET | News Philippines operator PLDT's board of directors has declared a final regular dividend of PHP 28 per outstanding share of the company's common stock, payable on 27 April 2018 to the holders of record on 13 April 2018. The cash dividend was declared out of the audited unrestricted retained earnings of the company as at 31 December 2017, which are sufficient to cover the total amount of dividend declared.
