CWC launches Flow Sports 2 channel in the Caribbean   15:34 CET | News 
Cable & Wireless Communications (CWC) has announced that its Caribbean-wide Flow TV service has launched a new premier sports channel called Flow Sports 2 to carry its exclusive Indian Premier League (IPL) cricket and Commonwealth Games coverage. The channel went live on 12 March and will be available to TV customers of Flow and BTC.
