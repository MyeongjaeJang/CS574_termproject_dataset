Russian govt to reduce 2019 fees for frequency bands  49 CET | News 
The Russian Ministry of Communications and Mass Media has revised its plans on the size of fees for the use of frequency bands , reports Comnews.ru. The ministry earlier raised the fees and calculated that the total amount of levies paid by operators would grow annually by RUB 5 billion. 
The Ministry has now decided to return the levies to their earlier 2019 level. The annual fee for mobile services bands had been raised by more than 400 percent for this year.
