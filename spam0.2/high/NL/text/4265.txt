HBO offers Go streaming service as standalone subscription in Poland Monday 26 March 2018 | 13:37 CET | News 
HBO has started selling its streaming service HBO Go direct to customers in Poland, without the need for a subscription to the channel through a pay-TV provider. The first month is offered free, and the normal subscription price is PLN 29.90 per month. No minimum contract is required. 
HBO Go works over computers, tablets, smartphones, Samsung smart TVs, Playstation, Xbox360, Apple TV and Chromecast. Each subscriber can connect up to five devices and stream simultaneously on two devices. 
HBO Go is also still available as a subscription from pay-TV, broadband and mobile operators in Poland.
