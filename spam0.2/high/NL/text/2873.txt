TAIPEI, Taiwan – This is a reminder for tomorrow’s, Friday March 23, Taipei Cycle Forum 2018. This Conference on the transition of the Taiwan bike industry towards new online focused business models, takes place at the Fullon Hotel – Liphao Land in Taichung, Taiwan. Taipei Cycle Forum 2018 is on the transition of Taiwan’s bike industry towards new online business models. – Photo Taipei Cycle Forum 
Some 300 industry peers from Taiwan and from outside Taiwan are expected to take part in this Conference which is organized by the Taiwan External Trade Development Council (TAITRA) in cooperation with Bike Europe. There are still seats left.
