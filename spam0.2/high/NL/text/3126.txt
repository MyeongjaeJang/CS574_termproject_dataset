Ivi grows revenues to RUB 2.4 billion in 2017 Friday 23 March 2018 | 12:51 CET | News 
Revenues of Russian OTT service Ivi totaled RUB 2.43 billion in 2017, up by RUB 0.86 billion from a year earlier, reports Comnews.ru. Analysts expect the company will exceed RUB 3 billion in revenues this year. 
The audience of the service grew by 45 percent last year, to 40 million unique users in December. Pay-TV services generated RUB 1.48 billion in revenue, up by 0.61 billion. Gross advertising incomes were RUB 1.5 billion.
