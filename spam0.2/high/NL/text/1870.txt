Bakcell offers app for VAT refunds   15:29 CET | News 
Azerbaijan mobile operator Bakcell has introduced to its customers the Bakcell TaxFree4U mobile application, reports Trend. The app facilitates applications for VAT refunds on purchases in European states. 
Up to 19 percent of the purchase price can refunded on purchases in Germany, France, Italy, Spain, Finland and the UK. The application can be downloaded on the App Store or Google Play.
