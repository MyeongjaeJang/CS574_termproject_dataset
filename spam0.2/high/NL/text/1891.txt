Huawei starts campaign in Warsaw to promote P20 launch   15:40 CET | News 
Huawei Poland installed a three-metre high statue of a butterfly in Warsaw to promote the upcoming launch of its new flagship smartphone, the Huawei P20. The new model will be presented in Paris on 27 March. The blue-violet butterfly symbolises beauty and spring and was unveiled on the spring equinox. It's expected to motivate people to explore the magic of photography, Huawei said. The motto of its campaign is #seemooore.
