Nkom launches Nettfart broadband speed measurement app based on Ookla system Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 11:49 CET | News 
Norwegian communications regulator Nkom has brought out an internet speed measurement application called Nettfart.no, based on a service provided by Ookla.
