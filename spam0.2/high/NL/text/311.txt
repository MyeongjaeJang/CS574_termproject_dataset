Galaxy S9, S9+ available in Cosmote, Germanos stores Monday 19 March 2018 | 11:38 CET | News Cosmote said the Samsung Galaxy S9 and S9 + smartphones became available in Cosmote and Germanos stores as well as at www.Cosmote.gr and www.Germanos.gr. Consumers can choose the new Samsung Galaxy S9 and S9 + in Midnight Black, Coral Blue, Lilac Purple models at EUR 879 (EUR 24.42 per month in 36 installments) and EUR 979 (EUR 27.19 per month in 36 installments), respectively.
