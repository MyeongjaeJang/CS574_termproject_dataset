ARPU of Russian mobile operators rise in 2017  01 CET | News 
Russian mobile operators had an a verage ARPU of RUB 299 in 2017, reports Vedomosti citing a study from TMT Consulting. The figure grew for the first time in last five years; it totaled RUB 290 in 2016. 
The mobile subscriber base of Beeline dropped by 0.3 percent, to 58.2 million. It decreased by 0.2 percent to 75.44 million for Megafon, and declined by 2.2 percent to 78.3 million for MTS.
