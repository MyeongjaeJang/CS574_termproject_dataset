Waystream wins SEK 3 million switch order from Lunet   12:22 CET | News 
Digital infrastructure provider Waystream said that Lulea’s metropolitan network, Lunet, has ordered Waystream’s MS4000 a stock 2 Gigabit Ethernet access switch for delivery in March - April 2018 to upgrade its grid to Gigabit speed. The contract is worth SEK 3 million and will lead to improvements for approximately 12,000 customer connections. 
Lunet has an existing relationship with Waystream, whose products include routers as well as switches. It broke away from PacketFront in 2012 and was listed on Nasdaq First North in 2015.
