Vodafone Spain pays out EUR 25 mln bonus to former Ono chairman   09:08 CET | News Vodafone Spain has paid out a EUR 25 million bonus to Jose Maria Castellano, the former chairman of Ono, the broadband provider it acquired in 2014 for EUR 7.2 billion, reports business daily Expansion. The operator initially challenged the payment but eventually lost its case as well as a subsequent corporate social responsibility action against Castellano, former ONO CEO Rosalia Portela and CFO Carlos Sagasta for alleged tax fraud. Vodafone has now proceeded to pay the bonus to Castellano, which will eventually rise to some EUR 33 million with interest, said the report.
