Ayst changes TV channel line-up   11:33 CET | News 
Russian ISP Ayst, working in the Samara region, has introduced to its customers the new 'Bridge TV Russian Hit' channel. The channel replaces the 'Bridge TV Classic' channel in the offer. 
Pay-TV from Ayst consists of 40 TV channels, including HD channels, for RUB 2.7 daily subscription. A RUB 0.33 daily discount is applied to subscribers of numerous internet tariffs from the ISP.
