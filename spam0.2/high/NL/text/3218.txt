AT&T offers 2018 Masters golf tournament in 4K HDR Friday 23 March 2018 | 08:31 CET | News AT&T said it's adding live 4K High Dynamic Range (HDR) broadcasts of the 2018 Masters golf tournament to its 4K channel lineup. The tournament starts on 5 April and takes place in Augusta, Georgia. AT&T is also giving DirecTV customers an exclusive look at political thriller “Backstabbing for beginners”. The movie is now exclusively on DirecTV Cinema before it arrives in theaters 27 April.
