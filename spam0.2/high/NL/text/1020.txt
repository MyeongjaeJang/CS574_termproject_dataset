Israeli cyber security specialist Solebit raises USD 11 mln for expansion Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 12:29 CET | News 
Israeli cyber security company Solebit said it has closed a USD 11 million Series A financing round led by ClearSky Security and with the participation of MassMutual Ventures and Glilot Capital Partners. Solebit will use the funds to accelerate the adoption and deployment of the SoleGATE Security Platform, immediately grow its sales and marketing capabilities, and to set up a new global headquarters in Silicon Valley, California. 
Based in Herzliya, Solebit was founded in 2014 by CEO Boris Vaynberg, CTO Meni Farjon, and VP product Yossi Sara, entrepreneurs with years of experience in defensive and offensive cyber-security approaches and all graduates of elite technology units in the Israel Defense Forces.
