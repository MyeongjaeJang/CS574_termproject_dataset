Rostelecom offers new premium TV package   13:06 CET | News 
Russian national operator Rostelecom has introduced its new Kinomania pay-TV package. The package includes more than 20 popular film channels, as well as the ViP Viasat Premium package including six channels and the Amediateka Home of HBO VoD service. Monthly subscriptions cost RUB 349, and the package can be activated by broadband and IPTV subscribers of the operator. Routers and set-top boxes are offered for RUB 1 monthly rental. 
Subscribers are able to watch the content on several devices, includind smartphones or notebooks, simultaneously.
