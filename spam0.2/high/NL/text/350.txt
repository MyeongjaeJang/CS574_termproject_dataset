M7 agrees new carriage deal with Viacom in Benelux Monday 19 March 2018 | 14:13 CET | News 
Viacom and satellite TV provider M7 announced a new carriage agreement for the Benelux. M7's Dutch and Belgian providers Canal Digitaal, Online.nl and TV Vlaanderen will increase the Viacom channels Nickelodeon and Spike to 24 hours a day as well as continue to carry the channels Nick Jr, MTV Music, VH1, VH1 Classic, Comedy Central and MTV NL. 
Viacom will distribute the channels over the Astra satellite at 23.5 degrees East and can also use the capacity for reaching other TV providers in the region.
