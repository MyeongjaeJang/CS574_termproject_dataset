US govt IT services provider joins Google Cloud Partner Program Friday 23 March 2018 | 12:11 CET | News CSRA, a provider of IT services to the US government, said it has joined the Google Cloud Partner Program. The move will give CSRA access to Google Cloud’s portfolio of products, including Google Cloud Platform and G Suite. The company already has strategic alliances with Amazon’s AWS cloud services, Cisco Systems, Microsoft and Oracle, among others.
