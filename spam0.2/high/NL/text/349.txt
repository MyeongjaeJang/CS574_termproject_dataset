NOS extends rights to Eredivisie football highlights Monday 19 March 2018 | 14:20 CET | News 
Dutch public broadcaster NOS has extended its rights to highlights from the first-division football league. The current agreement with Eredivisie Media & Marketing (EMM) was to expire in mid-2019 and has been extended for another three seasons.
