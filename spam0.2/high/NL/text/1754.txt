View Toni Munns's LinkedIn Profile 
With more than 15 years of international tour operator and hotel expertise, Munns has held senior cluster sales roles for a number of international luxury brands in the region. 
Munns joined IHG in 2016 as director of sales and marketing for the Crowne Plaza Dubai on Sheikh Zayed Road. Prior to that she held a director of sales position with Jumeirah Group. 
Originally hailing from the UK, Munns holds a Masters of Business Administration from Middlesex University Dubai.
