KPN CEO to join investment group Reggeborgh  08 CET | News 
Eelco Blok, the outgoing CEO at KPN, is joining the investment company Reggeborgh. He will take up the position after handing over to Maximo Ibarra at KPN's AGM on 18 April. 
Reggeborgh founded the main Dutch fibre operator Reggefiber and eventually sold the company to KPN. It also invests in real estate and controls the construction group VolkerWessels. Blok is expected to focus on tech investments at the company. 
Blok will also join the supervisory board of Philips Lighting.
