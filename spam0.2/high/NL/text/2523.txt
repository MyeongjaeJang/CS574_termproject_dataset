Beeline to start retail partnership programme Thursday 22 March 2018 | 11:27 CET | News 
Russian mobile operator Beeline, controlled by the Veon Group, plans to re-fresh and expand its retail chain, reports Tdaily.ru. The operator plans to start a retail partnership programme by the end of March. Cafes, bank agencies, travel agencies and other retail and service companies will be asked to open up their offices in shops of Beeline. As a result, Beeline expects to save around 15-20 percent on renting premises. 
Beeline also plans to open 350 new mono-branded shops across the country this year, and around 7,000 new staff will be employed by the company. The operator plans to have 3,000 shops in operation by the end of the second quarter.
