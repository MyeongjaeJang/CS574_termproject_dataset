Cosmote names winners of educational robotics contest Thursday 22 March 2018 | 19:20 CET | News Cosmote announced the winners of the WRO Pan-Hellenic Educational Robotics Competition 2018. The operator is the strategic partner of the contest that is organised by non-profit organisation WRO Hellas. A total of 1,230 students from 265 schools from all over Greece took part in the contest.
