Econocom appoints new CEO, founder becomes chairman   10:40 CET | News 
European IT services group Econocom has appointed Robert Bouchard its new CEO, with immediate effect. Bouchard succeeds Jean-Louis Bouchard, who founded the group and will continue as chairman for the board of directors.
