Wow! to use Intraway's provisioning, activation platform for new business services   14:40 CET | News 
Intraway will provide its Symphonica orchestration platform to US operator Wow! Internet, Cable & Phone as part of Wow!’s digital transformation initiative. Initial deployment will be focused on Wow!’s business services, with residential services targeted for future phases of deployment. 
The platform allows end-users of Wow!’s business services to set the framework to support future operation models such as Self-Care, Self-Provisioning, and APIs for easier integration into analytics and advanced diagnostic tools. Intraway said its platform allows service providers like Wow! to deliver a more effective onboarding and provisioning experience with the platform’s automation benefits.
