MTN South Africa raises some prepaid call prices   09:49 CET | News 
MTN South Africa has announced price changes to certain prepaid call rates with effect from 23 March, reports Mybroadband. MTN Pay Per Second prices rise from ZAR 0.79 to ZAR 0.99 per minute for voice calls. For customers on MTN Talk Free, the cost of calls to other networks will go up from ZAR 0.79 to ZAR 1.20. 
MTN executive Jacqui O'Sullivan said the price increases are part of a prepaid service overhaul, and not linked to recent VAT-related price hikes.
