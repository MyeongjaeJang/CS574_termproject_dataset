Hayu reality TV SVoD service available on Roku players in UK Monday 19 March 2018 | 11:06 CET | News 
NBCUniversal International's hayu reality TV show subscription video-on-demand (SVoD) service is now available on Roku streaming players in the UK. The hayu service offers access to more than 5,000 episodes of US and UK reality TV shows.
