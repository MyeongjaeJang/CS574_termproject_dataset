Swedish regulator holds 5G forum on 13 April Thursday 22 March 2018 | 12:47 CET | News 
Swedish postal and telecoms regulator PTS is inviting registrations by 06 April for its sixth 5G forum, which takes place on 13 April in Stockholm. The meeting will discuss overarching and strategic matters related to 5G development, and PTS aims to listing to interested parties' views, requirements and ideas. 
PTS said developing 5G networks in Sweden is likely to cause changes to the market for telecommunications. The sixth forum will attempt to look at the complexity of 5G business models.
