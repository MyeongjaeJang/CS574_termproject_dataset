Vodacom Tanzania offers free Instagram access with TZS 1,000 top-ups Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 09:29 CET | News 
Vodacom Tanzania is running a promotion providing free access to Instagram whenever customers purchase an airtime bundle worth TZS 1,000 or more. This follows a successful launch of Tigo's SuperNet Campaign. Vodacom's product team Head, Linda Riwa, said it has seen growing demand from both current and prospective customers to access Instagram for free.
