Meloni appointed Telecom Italia HR head Monday 19 March 2018 | 10:36 CET | News 
Telecom Italia (TIM) has announced the appointment of Riccardo Meloni as its new head of human resources and organisational development. A law graduate, Meloni has worked for multinationals including Wind, Enel and Leonardo, where he has been head of HR since January 2016, having started his career at Telecom Italia back in 1989.
