MTS upgrades GSM network in Lipetsk Thursday 22 March 2018 | 12:55 CET | News 
Russian mobile operator MTS has upgraded its GSM network in the Lipetsk region, reports Cnews.ru. Work was carried out in 50 settlements including more than 700,000 residents. The list includes the cities of Lipetsk and Yelets, the towns of Chaplygin, Gryazi, Zadonsk, Dankov and Lebedyan, as well as numerous villages.
