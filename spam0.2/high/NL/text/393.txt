Telefonica rolls out marketing campaigns service for mobile operators Monday 19 March 2018 | 09:51 CET | News 
Telefonica Business Solutions, the B2B unit of Spanish telecommunications operator Telefonica, has announced the launch of a service designed to help operators enhance their mobile customer experience via personalised content. The fully virtualised service, dubbed Marketing Campaigns Manager, enables operators to monetise subscriber interactions with contextual real-time marketing via multiple channels, allowing end-users to make a variety of different purchases at specific moments, said the company. Telefonica said its new service would empower mobile operators to create stronger engagement with their subscribers and ultimately capture new revenue streams.
