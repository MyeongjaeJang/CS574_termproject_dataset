Peru and Japan sign ICT development deal   09:53 CET | News Peru's transport and telecommunications ministry (MTC) has signed an agreement with Japan's domestic affairs and communications ministry (MIC) to work together on the development of Peru’s digital economy, above all digital terrestrial television (DTT) and the Internet of Things (IoT), reports business daily Gestion. Under the terms of the cooperation deal, Japan will also help boost Peru’s digital ecosystem in areas such as e-commerce, education, health, disaster risk management and customs processes.
