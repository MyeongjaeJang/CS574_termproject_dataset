IP-Only connects over 1,000 people in Halland to fibre in January and February   11:47 CET | News 
Swedish operator IP-Only said it connected more than 1,000 people in Halland to fibre in January and February 2018, adding that the pace of connections will only increase during the year. Since it signed a cooperation agreement with Halland county council in 2016, IP-Only has provided fibre to almost 20,000 residences or approximately 40,000 people, it said.
