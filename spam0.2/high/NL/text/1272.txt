Samsung's Galaxy Tab Active2 available for US workforce Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 17:33 CET | News 
Samsung Electronics America announced the US availability of the Galaxy Tab Active2 in the US. This rugged tablet is designed for mobile workers who conduct business outdoors or beyond office walls, including in the field, industrial locations, and harsh weather. The device provides easy access to essential functions in extreme conditions and advances workflow by combining high durability and LTE connectivity with a range of innovative capabilities for business users. 
The Tab Active2 is MIL-STD -810-certified as well as IP68-rated for water and dust resistance. Physical buttons are included for easy operation with work gloves, and the display can be used while wet, enabling access in rainy or inclement weather.
