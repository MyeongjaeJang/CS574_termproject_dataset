Ecuador, Germany sign digital transformation deal Friday 23 March 2018 | 12:04 CET | News 
Ecuador’s industries and productivity ministry has announced the signing of an agreement with the Ecuadorian-German Chamber of Industries and Trade to transform the local digital economy to take full advantage of the benefits of the 4th industrial revolution. "Promoting this change, following the example of Germany, is a joint effort with the public sector, private sector and academia, with a view to creating the necessary conditions to adapt information technologies as a whole to the Ecuadorian industrial sector and project it into the future,” said industries and productivity minister Eva Garcia in a statement. 
The collaboration is above all focused on transforming the industrial and agricultural sectors and accelerating the use of smart technologies, added the ministry.
