Megacom launches new data plans aimed at young people Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 11:36 CET | News 
Kyrgyzstan mobile operator Megacom has launched its new tariff line aimed at young people. The S package includes a 20 GB mobile data bundle for KGS 250 monthly, the M package contains 50 GB for KGS 400 per month, and the L package includes 100 GB for KGS 600 monthly.
