Mada enables online payments via Mastercard gateway   10:33 CET | News 
Saudi Arabia’s domestic payment network Mada and entering a partnership to enable online transactions using Mastercard’s Payment Gateway Services. Local internet merchants will enable to accept Mada cards as a primary mode of payment on their websites. 
Using Mastercard to facilitate online payments via the Mada network will enable Saudi businesses to increase their eCommerce sales significantly by offering customers more flexibility and convenience, said Ziad Al Yousef, general director of the payment systems department at the Saudi Arabian Monetary Authority (SAMA). 
The Mada network connects all financial transactions from automated teller machines (ATMs) and point-of-sale (POS) terminals throughout Saudi Arabia to merchants' banks and their card issuer bank.
