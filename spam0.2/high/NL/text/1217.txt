Megafon offers Eset anti-virus software for Connect subscribers  29 CET | News 
Russian mobile operator Megafon has started a cooperation with the anti-virus services provider Eset, reports Cnews.ru. A special version of the mobile anti-virus product Eset Nod32 Mobie Security for Android has been offered to subscribers of the new ' Connect !' tariff line from the operator. 
The product can be downloaded onto smartphones or tablets by subscribers of any tariff under the new line. The product also supports an anti-theft function to locate stolen devices and block them remotely.
