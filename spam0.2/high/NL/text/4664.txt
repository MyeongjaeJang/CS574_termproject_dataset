Telkom adds 2 independent directors Monday 26 March 2018 | 15:59 CET | News 
South Africa operator Telkom has appointed Sello Moloko and Dolly Mokgatle to is board as independent non-executive directors with effect from 20 March. Moloko is the Executive Chairman and co-founder of Thesele Group, a wholly Black-owned diversified investment holding company, and is the former CEO of Old Mutual Asset Managers.
