KPN supports coding lessons for schoolchildren Monday 26 March 2018 | 11:38 CET | News 
KPN announced a partnership with FutureNL, to support coding lessons for schoolchildren. FutureNL operates the 'School of Code' to introduce 10-12 year-olds to coding, and KPN will host at its office in Amsterdam 50 of the classes this year.
