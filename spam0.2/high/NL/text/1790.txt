Drillisch and Bild Connect add Samsung Galaxy S9, S9+ to portfolio Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 13:24 CET | News 
Drilisch and Bild Connect, the mobile service from German publisher Axel Springer, have both added the Samsung Galaxy S9 and S9+ to their product portfolio following the release of the new phones. Both the Galaxy S9 and the S9+ are available for a one-time fee of EUR 49.99 in connection with the Flat 2000 LTE tariff offering 2 GB of monthly data at speeds up to 50 Mbps and flat rate telephony and SMS in all German networks. The Galaxy S9 then costs EUR 39.95 per month with the Flat 2000 LTE tariff, while the S9+ costs EUR 44.95 per month. Customers can also pay an extra EUR 6 per to reset their monthly data usage and thus receive another 2 GB of LTE data, while tariff also includes the Bildplus option at no extra cost.
