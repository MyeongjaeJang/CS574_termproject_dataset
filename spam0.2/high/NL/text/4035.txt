Supervisory Board of Orange Poland, Jean-Marie Culpin and Valerie Therond, have submitted their resignations, with effect from 20 April. Due to the fact that two members resigned and the mandates of other four members expire at the Annual General Meeting scheduled for 20 April, the company has introduced candidates for members of the Supervisory Board of Orange: Henryka Bochniarz (Independent Director) – for a new term of office; Thierry Bonhomme; Ramon Fernandez – for a new term of office; Maria Paslo-Wisniewska (Independent Director) – for a new term of office; Wieslaw Rozlucki (Independent Director) – for a new term of office; and Jean-Marc Vignolles.
