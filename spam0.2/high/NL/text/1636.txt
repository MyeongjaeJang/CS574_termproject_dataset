Sigmax director Rijk to step down   11:15 CET | News 
Walter Rijk is stepping down at the end of this month as director of Sigmax, after helping found the Dutch company in 1998. Sigmax specialises in implementation and management of ICT infrastructure for medium-sized companies, as well as 'smart' software for the public and private sectors. Rijk has gradually handed over a number of tasks to the rest of the management team, and Sigmax is in talks with candidates to take over his position as business unit manager for IoT Solutions and SequriX. Rijk will remain a member of the advisory board and shareholder in the company.
