Deutsche Telekom launches carrier billing for Apple App Store  31 CET | News 
Deutsche Telekom has introduced carrier billing for Apple’s App Store, enabling customers to pay for purchases on the store through their mobile bill, reports OnlineKosten.de. Both prepaid and contract customers can make use of Telekom’s new payment method on Apple iPhones and iPads as well as through the iTunes Store on their computer.
