MultiChoice gives KES 500,000 sponsorship for 2018 Kenya Open Monday 19 March 2018 | 13:54 CET | News MultiChoice Kenya has announced a KES 500,000 sponsorship towards the 2018 edition of the Barclays Kenya Open Golf Tournament. MultiChoice Kenya supports various local sports in the country through sponsorships and has grown hand-in-hand with various sporting fraternities by forging long-term partnerships to develop sports and local talent in the country.
