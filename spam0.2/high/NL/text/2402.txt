Tele2 NL opens new shop in Utrecht  17 CET | News 
Tele2 Netherlands will on 22 March open a new store on the Lange Elisabethstraat 32 in Utrecht. The store will be open seven days a week. 
To celebrate the opening, the operator is organising a Beat the Pro competition together with football club FC Utrecht. FIFA2018 fans can compete with two eSporters from FC Utrecht, Lode de Boo and Danny Hazebroek, on Friday, 23 March from noon. 
Tele2 NL will be the shirt sponsor and connectivity partner of FC Utrecht for the next three seasons. The agreement was signed in January.
