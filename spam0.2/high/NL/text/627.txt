Nova to distribute Metro Goldwyn Mayer hit films, TV series in Greece Monday 19 March 2018 | 20:33 CET | News 
Greek payTV platform Nova entered into a new agreement with Metro Goldwyn Mayer (MGM) to secure rights to a selection of its most successful new and classic movies, including all four James Bond movies starring Daniel Craig, and Emmy and Golden Globe TV series The Handmaid’s Tale for Greece and Cyprus. On March, Nova will exclusively premiere The Handmaid’s Tale. Furthermore, Nova acquires rights to a collection of top MGM films, which will be distributed via the Novacinema channels in Greece and Cyprus and will be also available through Nova On Demand and Nova Go services.
