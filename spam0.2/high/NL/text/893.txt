TalkTalk TV adds Viceland channel from 27 March Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 10:16 CET | News 
UK telecoms provider TalkTalk is adding the Viceland lifestyle and entertainment channel from global youth brand Vice Media to its TV platform from 27 March (channel 338). This is the latest in a series of UK distribution deals for Viceland, which is also available on the Sky (channel 157), NOW TV and TVPlayer platforms. 
Following the launch of TalkTalk's new multi-screen platform, Viceland will be available to view through the set-top box as well as through TalkTalk TV app across multiple devices.
