Ukraine postpones switch-off of analogue TV Monday 19 March 2018 | 15:41 CET | News 
Ukrainian national broadcasting regulator has postponed the switch-off of analogue TV services in Ukraine, reports BizLigaNet. The action will be carried out gradually, in three stages. 
The first stage is planned to take place later this August: analogue TV services will be switched off in one region. In January 2019, it will be switched off across the country, except for national TV-channel UA First, some local broadcasters and some areas.
