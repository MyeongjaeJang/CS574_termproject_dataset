Amazon Italia hikes monthly Prime subscription to EUR 36   09:43 CET | News 
Amazon Italia has announced that it’s raising the monthly subscription rate for its Amazon Prime service to EUR 36 from EUR 19, starting 04 April for new registrations and 04 May for existing customers. Anyone signing up or renewing before those dates will have one year’s subscription at the lower price before it goes up, said the company. A Prime subscription in Italy offers advantages such as 24-hour shipments with many products, access to Prime Video, special offers and unlimited cloud photo storage. 
As in Spain , the company has also launched a new monthly version of its Prime service costing EUR 4.99, equivalent to nearly EUR 60 a year.
