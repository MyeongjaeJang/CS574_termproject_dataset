IP phone provider Snom opens Madrid office   12:03 CET | News 
IP telephony specialist Snom has announced the inauguration of a permanent office in Madrid to meet the rising demands of the Spanish IP phone market. The company said it had decided to open a Madrid office to provide its Spanish partners and customers with local support for an increasingly significant market that has grown consistently over the last two years. 
Snom recently launched a revamped Partner Programme and announced several new additions to its product portfolio such as the executive IP desk phone Snom D785 and the Snom D120 entry-level VoIP system.
