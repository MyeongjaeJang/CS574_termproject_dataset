Channel 4 to show European Champions Cup rugby Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 17:52 CET | News 
UK broadcaster Channel 4 has agreed a 4-year deal with European Professional Club Rugby to broadcast the European Rugby Champions Cup. It will show nine live matches from The Champions Cup each season until 2022.
