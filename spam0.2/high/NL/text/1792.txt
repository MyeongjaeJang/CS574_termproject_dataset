Portuguese average mobile spend stable at EUR 12.2 per month Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 12:42 CET | News 
The Portuguese pay an average EUR 12.23 per month for mobile service, excluding the costs of a handset, according to the latest study by Marktest for February. The amount has remained relatively stable over the past three years, the market researcher said. 
Some 65 percent of respondents in its survey said they don't pay more than EUR 15 a month for mobile services, 10.4 percent pay between EUR 16 and EUR 25, and 2.9 percent spend more than EUR 25 per month.
