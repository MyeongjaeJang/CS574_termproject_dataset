Voxbone offers free on-net calls   12:11 CET | News 
Voxbone announced its customers will receive zero-rate voice services for all phone calls made within its global network. This latest offering allows users to engage in unlimited voice connections between other Voxbone phone numbers, including toll-free, local, and mobile, regardless of location or duration and at zero cost. Voxbone customers with outbound dialing capabilities will have access to more than 60 countries and 9,000 area codes within Voxbone’s network at no extra charge. This news also follows the recent release of Voxbone’s AI-based Speech Analytics and recording service, also offered through its web portal and via API.
