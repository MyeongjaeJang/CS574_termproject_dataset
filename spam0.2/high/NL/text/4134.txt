Mobily signs up to Mecca province Haj awareness campaign  25 CET | News 
Saudi operator Mobily has agreed to sponsor the national media campaign run by Mecca Province for guests during the 1439 Hijri Haj season, Saudi Gazette reported. The campaign under the slogan "The Haj is worship and civilised behaviour" focuses on the holiness of Haram during Haj and Umrah. It concerns respecting the place and its holiness, and complying with regulations and instructions. 
Mobily sponsors the campaign every year and this year, its sponsorship includes the Haj and Umrah season. Mobily enhances its network in the focused region and holy sites with the latest communication equipment to help serve the huge number of pilgrims.
