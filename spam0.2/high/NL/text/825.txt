Dialog Axiata, Arimac test AI-powered humanoid robot at Dialog store   08:52 CET | News Sri Lanka operator Dialog Axiata, in partnership with Arimac Lanka, have introduced an intelligent humanoid robot to serve as customer assistant at the Dialog Iconic flagship store. The cloud-based humanoid robot is designed specifically for commercial use in the retail, hospitality, healthcare and education industries. 
The Humanoid Customer Assistant is designed to greet and assist with providing advice on promotions and offers on its HD visual touch screen and further elaborate on any of the selected options with a detailed explanation of a promotion or offer along with an accompanying video/music and can entertain. The robot is also designed to engage with customers using its facial recognition and voice interaction capabilities. It can also locate the charging station and charge itself automatically enabling uninterrupted service.
