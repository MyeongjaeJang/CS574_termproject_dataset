MGTS expands pay-TV offer   13:00 CET | News 
Russian regional fixed operator MGTS, a subsidiary of the operator MTS working in the city and region of Moscow, plans to modify its pay-TV offer from 5 April. Russian channels 'Wild Hunting HD' and 'Wild Fishing HD' will become available under the Base packages from the operator, and Russian film channel Telenovella will be offered for testing. Russian entertainment channels 'Sarafan Plus' and 'Country.Super.Plus', as well as shopping channels Discount TV and Topshop TV will also become available, as will the news channel Belarus 24.
