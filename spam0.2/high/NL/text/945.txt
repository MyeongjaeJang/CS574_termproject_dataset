ProSiebenSat.1, Discover add new broadcasters to 7TV streaming platform Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 11:32 CET | News 
German broadcaster ProSiebenSat.1 and Discovery Networks’ joint-venture streaming platform 7TV has reached an agreement with the stations Sport1, Welt and N24 to air their content on 7TV. The agreement encompasses the stations’ live streams as well as catch-up content and is the first such deal made by ProSiebenSat.1 and Discovery Networks to air content from broadcasters outside their television groups. 
7TV has also appointed Alexander von Woikowsky as Chief Content Officer and Managing Director for 7TV responsible for partnerships and distribution. Woikowsky has been at ProSiebenSat.1 since 2012 where he was most recently a director for key account management in distribution.
