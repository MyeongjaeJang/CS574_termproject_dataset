Vodafone Iceland holds start-up competition with smart city theme Friday 23 March 2018 | 13:39 CET | News 
Vodafone Iceland is cooperating with Startup Iceland to run the third annual Stokkpallinn business development contest. This year's theme is the IoT smart city, and Vodafone is inviting the participation of enterprises with a produce ready for sale within twelve months. 
Up to five participants will be shortlisted and get the opportunity to create an idea to bring to market in cooperation with Vodafone.
