InfoVista application aware SD-WAN selected by SoTel  36 CET | News InfoVista entered into a collaborative agreement with SoTel Systems to offer new application performance and SD-WAN products delivered from the cloud to SoTel’s service provider and small and mid-size enterprise customers in the US, Canada and Latin America. SoTel, a cloud service provider and distributor of business-class communications and networking equipment, is using the unique application flow optimization features offered by InfoVista’s Ipanema Virtualized Network Function (VNF) to deliver application-aware SD-WAN services via its cloud infrastructure.
