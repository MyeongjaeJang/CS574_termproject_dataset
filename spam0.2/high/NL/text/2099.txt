21 March 2018, 12:10 Law enforcers identify those killed in Chechen CTO 
During the counterterrorist operation (CTO) conducted in the Achkhoi-Martan District of Chechnya, natives of Ingushetia and Dagestan were killed, the Ministry Internal Affairs (MIA) has reported today. 
The " Caucasian Knot " has reported that in the evening on March 20 in the above district, in a forest near the village of Bamut, near the border with Ingushetia, four men were killed in an armed skirmish with law enforcers. 
All the four casualties have been identified, a source from the Chechen MIA told the " Caucasian Knot " correspondent today. "Three of them were residents of the Malgobek District of Ingushetia, and the fourth one was a resident of Dagestan; the oldest of the criminals is 33, the youngest – about 20 years old," the source said, adding that submachine guns, hand grenades and a sniper rifle were found on the killed militants, which, in his opinion, indicates that the bandits intended to commit terror acts.
