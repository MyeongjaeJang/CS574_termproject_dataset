ER-Telecom launches network monitoring service  27 CET | News 
Russian operator ER-Telecom, working under the Dom.ru brand, has launched its pro-active network monitoring system in 36 cities across the country, reports Comnews.ru. The system helps the operator identify users facing connection problems due to errors on communication nodes. It enables the company to resolve the problem before even receiving complaints from customers. 
The operator claims that the amount of complaints and applications to support centres on technical problems has dropped by 18 percent since the launch of the new service. 
A test of the system for monitoring digital TV services was carried out in five cities in 2016-2017. Digital TV and broadband services are currently being monitored.
