Huawei starts sales of new P20 Lite smartphone   10:34 CET | News 
Huawei has announced the start of sales of the new P20 Lite smartphone. The launch comes ahead of a planned global event on 27 March in Paris to launch its new flagship P20 smartphone. 
The Huawei P20 Lite is the entry-level model in the new series and will sell for a recommended price of EUR 369. The device comes with a new design compared to last year's P10 , with a 'top notch' in the screen for the front camera, sensors and speaker. The display is 5.84 inches with a Full HD+ resolution of 1,080x2,280 pixels. 
The Huawei P20 Lite runs the company's Kirin 659 octa-core processor, Android 8, 4 GB RAM and 64 GB internal memory. It also comes with a microSD card slot for up to 256 GB external storage.
