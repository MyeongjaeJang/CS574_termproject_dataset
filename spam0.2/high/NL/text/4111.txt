T-Systems South Africa names Molefe as new MD  18 CET | News 
T-Systems' South African operations have announced the appointment of Dineo Molefe as the new managing director. Molefe has been CFO of the company for two years and takes over from Gert Schoonbee, who has been managing director for six years. After more than eighteen years at T-Systems South Africa, Schoonbee will hand over the reins to Molefe effective 30 April. 
After qualifying as a CA, Molefe went on to complete a Masters in Commerce at the University of Johannesburg and the Advanced Management Programme at University of Pennsylvania's Wharton Business School. Molefe adds that as part of the transition, she will now lead the Future Fit programme, which is focused on shaping T-Systems SA for the future.
