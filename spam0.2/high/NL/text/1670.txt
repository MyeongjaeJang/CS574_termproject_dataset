Beeline offers 3 mths free service with Meizu smartphone Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 11:35 CET | News 
Russian mobile operator Beeline introduced a promotion for customers buying the Meizu M5c 16 GB Black LTE smartphone. Customers buying the device costing RUB 8,990 are offered free services under the 'EverythingMy2' tariff from the operator for three months.
