Samsung unveils Emergency Services Network smartphone   11:10 CET | News 
Samsung has unveiled its new smartphone device to be issued to frontline emergency workers as part of the UK Emergency Services Network (ESN) roll-out. The device is being showcased at the British APCO public safety technology event. 
The new Samsung smartphone is optimised for the emergency environment and supports all emergency services functionality and critical voice services. Users will have access to both prioritised voice calls and rich data applications on the same device through 'Public Safety-LTE' 4G networking. A 'push to talk' button allows users to communicate instantly across emergency service talk groups. The rugged device has a damage-resistant, Gorilla Glass 4 screen, and is IP68 water and dust resistant.
