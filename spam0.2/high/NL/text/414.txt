Velcom introduces Samsung Galaxy S9, S9+ Monday 19 March 2018 | 15:18 CET | News 
Belarusian mobile operator Velcom has started selling the Samsung Galaxy S9 and S9+ at its shops. The devices were presented at the MWC-2018 in Barcelona. The price of the Samsung Galaxy S9 totals BYN 2,399 at the operator's shops and a BYN 2,099 discount rate for Velcom subscribers. The price of the Samsung Galaxy S9+ totals BYN 2,639 for everyone, and BYN 2,339 for subscribers.
