Nkom publishes permit fee schedules for 2018 Friday 23 March 2018 | 12:52 CET | News 
Norwegian telecoms regulator Nkom has published its final schedule of fees for frequency usage permits for 2018 , as well as its rates for broadcasting, professional mobile radio, aviation, and for using telephone numbers. The watchdog will generate revenue of NOK 81.39 million in frequency usage fees this year.
