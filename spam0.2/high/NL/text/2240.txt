Swisscom brand Wingo introduces convergent fixed-mobile offer Wednesday 21 March 2018 | 16:12 CET | News 
Swisscom's brand Wingo introduces a convergent fixed-mobile offer for CHF 94 a month. The plan includes internet at home and mobile internet, unlimited telephony and SMS to all Swiss networks. Wingo TV costs an extra CHF 5. 
Customers using both fixed and mobile internet paid CHF 130 a month previously for the services, meaning a savings of EUR 36 a month with the new offer. In addition, Wingo TV's price has been reduced from CHF 14 to CHF 5. The TV service includes 150 channels, including 80 in HD, up to 100 hours of recording and 7-day replay.
