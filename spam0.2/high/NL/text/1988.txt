Appointed Director Of Business Development - UK and Ireland At Nor1 Inc. in London , United Kingdom View Stuart Derricott's LinkedIn Profile 
Nor1, the leader in hospitality merchandising technology is proud to announce and welcome Stuart Derricott as Director of Business Development for UK and Ireland. Mr. Derricott will be responsible for expanding adoption of the Nor1 Pricing & Merchandising Intelligence Platform and developing and strengthening relationships with hotel groups and Hospitality Industry partners in the region. 
Mr. Derricott most recently held positions as VP Global Sales in the Connectivity and Channel Management sectors of the Hospitality Industry and previously had an extensive career in senior management roles in the Retail Supply sector. Mr. Derricott holds a degree in Business Management and has gained many years of experience in Sales and Marketing though developing the UK market for both Multinational and Startup businesses.
