Sarana Menara to pay full-year dividend of IDR 1.2 trillion for 2017  50 CET | News Indonesian telecom infrastructure provider SMN, parent company of PT Profesional Telekomunikasi Indonesia (Protelindo), has announced plans for a full-year 2017 dividend payment of IDR 1.2 trillion. The proposed total dividend will be subject to approval at the annual general meeting of shareholders to be held no later than May. 
SMN paid its first dividend totaling IDR 700 billion for fiscal year 2016. The planned 2017 total dividend of IDR 1.2 trillion includes the interim 2017 dividend of IDR 306 billion paid in December 2017.
