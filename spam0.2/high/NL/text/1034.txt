Telia Finland asserts active role in tackling child sexual abuse Tuesday 20 March 2018 | 12:31 CET | News 
Telia Finland claims to take a pioneering role in acting in cases of sexual exploitation of children, saying it uses an internet blocking list provided by the Finnish National Bureau of Investigation (Keskusrikospoliisi or KRP). The operator said it cooperates with the charity Save the Children, adding that any suspicions of child abuse can be referred anonymously through the charity's nettivihje.fi website.
