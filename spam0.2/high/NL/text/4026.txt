M1, Keppel Electric launch joint product bundles   08:54 CET | News Singapore operator M1 and Keppel Electric have agreed to launch joint product bundles. Customers staying in the area with postal codes starting with 60 to 64 can now sign up for electricity plans offered by Keppel Electric at the M1 Shop located at IMM Jurong to access exclusive offers and discounts during the Jurong OEM soft launch. They can sign up for electricity plans in-store and receive SGD 80 off Samsung Galaxy S9 and S9+, or a free 6 months of Upsized Data worth SGD 35.40. Offers are valid for new and re-contracting mobile customers.
