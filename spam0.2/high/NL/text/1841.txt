Beltelecom to raise internet prices from April   15:09 CET | News 
Belarusian national operator Beltelecom has announced plans to raise prices for its internet services from 1 April. The rates will increase, on average, by 3 percent. Rates will remain unchanged under the Yasna tariff line. 
Prices for telegraph services, ILD telephony calls, calls to Belarusian mobile networks, as well as rates of some VAS options will also increase. 
The increase is related to the need to acquire modern equipment, to develop new solutions and services and to upgrade the network, Beltelecom said.
