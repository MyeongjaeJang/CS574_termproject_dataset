Afghanistan govt slashed internet service rates   11:27 CET | News The Ministry of Communications and Information Technology of Afghanistan has reduced internet service prices. According to the Ministry, 3G internet service rates will drop by 10 percent for Salaam Network, which represents a 20 percent decrease compared to other domestic network. A 37.5 percent discount will be applied to Afghan Telecom Digital Subscriber line (DSL) internet services. The Ministry has also announced that it plans to offer a 20 percent discount to private internet service providers, "if this discount reaches directly to consumers".
