Svyaznoy starts sales of Xiaomi Redmi 5 Thursday 22 March 2018 | 13:28 CET | News 
Russian mobile retail chain Svyaznoy has started sales of the Xiaomi Redmi 5 smartphone in its shops. The device is powered by the octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 450 processor and features a 5.7-inch IPS screen.
