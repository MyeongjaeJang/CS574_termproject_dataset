Thaicom appoints Bunsumpun as chairman, Israsena resigns Monday 19 March 2018 | 10:00 CET | News Thaicom has announced that Paron Israsena has resigned from his positions of chairman of the board of directors and independent director, effective 24 March. Thaicom has appointed Prasert Bunsumpun as chairman of its board of directors in replacement of Israsena.
