Spark launches first live 5G mobile test site   11:38 CET | News New Zealand operator Spark has launched its first live 5G mobile test site in Wellington, with Minister of Broadcasting, Communications and Digital Media Hon Clare Curran becoming the first member of the public to run a 5G speed test achieving speeds of over 9 Gbps. The trial, located on the Stout Street Exchange Building, was deployed with specialist equipment and technical support from Huawei and is located directly across from Parliament. It will test speeds, coverage and the parameters of the spectrum in a real-world environment.
