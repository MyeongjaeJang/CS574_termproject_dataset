Notre Dame Cathedral in Paris is falling apart A foundation in France hopes Americans will donate money to help save the Notre Dame Cathedral in Paris. It's home to some of Catholicism's most important relics. Conservationists say the building is in desperate need of repair. Roxana Saberi reports.
