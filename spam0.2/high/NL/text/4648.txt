MTS offers cloud cash register for SMEs Monday 26 March 2018 | 16:00 CET | News 
Russian mobile operator MTS has started sales of its new 'MTS Cash Desk' cloud service for SMEs, reports Cnews.ru. The offer contains free communication services, software, support and services of the fiscal data operator. The package costs RUB 18,900, and new customers will be offered free services until 1 July.
