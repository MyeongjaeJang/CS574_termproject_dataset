GOK - 26 Mar 2018 4 killed in revenge attack in Gok state 
At least four people were killed in Abiei Chok area of Gok state, in a revenge attack between Pagok and Pathiong clans on Friday. 
Speaking to Radio Tamazuj on Sunday, Gok State minister of local government Malek Mayom, said members of the Pathiong clan attacked Pagok in retaliation to the killing of one of their members at the beginning of the month. 
Mayom said during the attack four people from the Pagok clan were killed. 
He called on the communities to settle their diferences peacefully adding that the two clans have been living peacefully since August last year.
