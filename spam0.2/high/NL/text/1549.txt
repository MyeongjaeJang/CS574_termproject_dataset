MyFC names Weber as chief technology integration officer   09:34 CET | News Swedish fuel cell manufacturer MyFC appointed Sebastian Weber as chief technology integration officer, effective from 1 May. Weber will be responsible for one of the company's four core areas, full integration of myFC’s technology in smartphones. Weber has extensive experience from the mobile industry, including Ericsson and most recently Fingerprint Cards. He was instrumental in building both technology and organisation for Fingerprint Card’s integration into smartphones, from the very first customer to reaching volume business. At myFC, he will lead the work towards full integration of the company's hybrid platform, based on the Lamina fuel cell, in smartphones.
