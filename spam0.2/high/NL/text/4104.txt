Cellcom Israel appoints DIC CEO Lapidot to board   09:53 CET | News 
Cellcom Israel has appointed Sholem Lapidot as a member of its board of directors for an initial term until the next annual general meeting, at which time his re-election for another term will be brought up for shareholder approval. Lapidot serves as CEO of DIC, Cellcom's indirect controlling shareholder, and of IDB. 
Lapidot is also on the board of directors of IDB and DIC, is chairman of the board of directors of Modi'in - Energy Management (1992), substitute director at Elron Electronic Industries, committee member at the IDB Fund and a member of the board of directors of other private companies owned by DIC and IDB. 
From 2015 to 2016, Lapidot served as a member of the board of directors of ADAMA Agricultural Solutions, from 2009 to 2013 as the CEO and member of the board of directors of CLADD, and from 2008 to 2012 as a member of the board of directors of Australtex. Lapidot has studied Rabbinical Studies.
