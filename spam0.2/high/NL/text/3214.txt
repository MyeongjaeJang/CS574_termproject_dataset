Skylink to bring Formula 1 on Sport 1, Sport 2 channels Friday 23 March 2018 | 11:37 CET | News 
Czech and Slovak satellite platform Skylink, belonging to M7 Group, will bring live broadcasting of the Formula 1 races on Sundays, as well as Friday trainings and Saturday qualifications, starting from 25 March with the race on the Australian lap Albert Park. Skylink will offer Formula 1 in cooperation with AMC, the exclusive rights holder for Formula 1 races. 
Skylink and AMC extended their cooperation in February. Skylink customers can watch Formula 1 on the TV channels Sport 1 and Sport 2, which are part of the programme packages Multi, Combi and Complete.
