AWEIL - 20 Mar 2018 High increase in commodity prices in Northern Bahr al Ghazal 
Northern Bahr al Gahzal chamber of commerce raised complaint about the high prices of goods this month. 
Wol Amuk, representative of Northern Bahr al Ghazal chamber of commerce, told Radio Tamazuj that the price hikes are unbelievable in the state.
