WEST KORDOFAN - 20 Mar 2018 Student stabbed to death in West Kordofan State 

A student at Al-Mujlad Secondary School in Sudan’s West Kordofan State was stabbed to death by a fellow student on Saturday evening. 
Speaking to Radio Tamazuj on Sunday, Hamida Khalifa, the father of the late Khalifa Hamida Khalifa said his son who was a candidate of the Sudan School Certificate was preparing for the national exams which begun on Monday before he was murdered on Saturday. 
Khalifa said his son was found dead after he was stubbed severally leading to his death. 
He revealed that the suspect has been arrested and investigations have started. 
“I believe in what God has planned and I hope justice will prevail,” he added.
