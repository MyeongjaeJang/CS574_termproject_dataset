Megafon expands partnership with Tachography Centre   13:02 CET | News 
Russian mobile operator Megafon has expanded its partnership with the Regional Tachography Centre (RTC), an integrator of M2M solutions, reports Cnews.ru. More than 1,300 M2M Sims from the operator have been installed on tachographs based on passenger and freight vehicles of RTC customers. More than 140 staff of the company use mobile services from Megafon.
