Mail.ru says 3 of its apps in top 10 in Russia   15:32 CET | News 
Three mobile applications form Russian internet company Mail.ru Group are in the list of the top-10 mobile applications in Russia by number of downloads, according to a study from the company App Annie. Data from Apple's App Store and Google Play was taken into account. 
Mobile applications of social networks VKontakte and Odnoklassniki, as well as the advertising mobile application Yula are in the list. VKontakte and Yula ranks among the top five apps.
