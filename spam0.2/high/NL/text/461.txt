MTS Belarus starts selling Samsung Galaxy S9, S9+   15:58 CET | News 
Mobile operator MTS Belarus has started selling the Samsung Galaxy S9 and S9+ smartphones in its shops. The devices are offered to subscribers for BYN 2,099 and BYN 2,339, respectively. The devices are also available on installment with either 12, 18 or 24-month contracts. There will be no initial rate or price increase with the longer contracts.
