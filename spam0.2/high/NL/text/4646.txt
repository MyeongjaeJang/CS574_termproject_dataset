SimbaPay launches chatbot to facilitate SMS remittances Monday 26 March 2018 | 16:04 CET | News 
SimbaPay announceed the launch of a chatbot service to support international money transfers over SMS. The SimbaPay chatbot will also enable 24/7 instant customer service for its customers across Africa and Europe. 
SimbaPay platform senders can now send money with just a SMS, reaching contacts without smartphones. All they require is the recipient’s phone number, and the chatbot will automatically obtain the recipient bank account or mobile money details. 
The chatbot provides the scalability to handle thousands of customer enquiries per hour. The SimbaPay chatbot service is also accessible on SimbaPay’s social media channels including Facebook. 
Popular queries that are resolved by the chatbot include exchange rate queries, how to send money instructions and coverage questions.
