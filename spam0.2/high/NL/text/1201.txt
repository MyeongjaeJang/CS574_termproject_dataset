UPC Poland adds Polsat Sport Fight HD channel  27 CET | News 
UPC Poland added the TV channel Polsat Sport Fight HD to its offering and increases thus the number of HD channels to 120, reported Media2.pl. Polsat Sport Fight HD will be part of the packages Select and Max. The cable operator thus completes the portfolio of sports channels offered by the Group Polsat. UPC already offers the channels Polsat Sport, Polsat Sport Extra and Polsat Sport News.
