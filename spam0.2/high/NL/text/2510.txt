Vivo launches V9 smartphone in Asia Thursday 22 March 2018 | 14:07 CET | News 
Vivo has unveiled in Thailand the new V9 smartphone, set to launch across Asia in the coming weeks. Featuring a 19:9 full-screen display with top notch and a 23-megapixel front camera, the Android 8.1 phones offers a range of photo options including AI techniques to improve images. 
The device features a 6.3-inch full HD+ IPS display and runs the octa-core Qualcomm Snapdragon 626 processor, 4GB of RAM and 64 GB of storage. The dual back cameras offer 16 and 5-megapixel sensors with HDR, bokeh and 3D effects as well as artificial intelligence software to improve and edit images.
