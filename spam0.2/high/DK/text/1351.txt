Lars Boje Mortensen on Medieval Prose (Milan) April 9, 2018 Lars Boje Mortensen on Medieval Prose (Milan) 
Today, Lars Boje Mortensen opens a conference at the University of Milan on medieval prose and its production and circulation. He will expand on the reflections about a connection between the rise of French and Old Norse prose in the period c. 1190-1230 that was hinted at in his publication last year on the sudden success of prose .
