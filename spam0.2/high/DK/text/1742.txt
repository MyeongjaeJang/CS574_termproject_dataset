Tekst: Anders Baad Mai Publiceret den 13 apr 2018 kl. 11 
Det svenske studie Fatshark annoncerede i dag, at på et måned, har Warhammer: Vermintide 2 formået at sælge mere end 1 million kopier. For at sammenligne det med et andet nyt spil, så har Far Cry 5 formået at sælge 750.000 kopier på PC. 
Gamereactor fik en samtale med Fatshark CEO, Martin Wahlund: 
Hi Martin, how does this feel? - It feels truly fantastic! Very inspiring and this gives us so much inspiration to work on loads of great upgrades. 
How much had the first game sold in the same amount of time? - Just about 320 000 copies. 
Does this mean that we will get Vermintide 3? - You never know... We still have loads of stuff we want to get into Vermintide 2. Relaterede tekster
