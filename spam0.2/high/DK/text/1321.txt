In the late 1970’s and 80’s, Nashville wasn’t the tourist attraction it is today. Murfreesboro hosted a private disco bar that was the premiere destination for locals and travelers. 
Jackson Jones, creator of Journalist Vibes, dives into the forgotten disco bar known as Faces. 
He also interviews NOVA Talent creator Rahsaan Robinson about how he and his team are adapting to the times keeping the music alive in Middle Tennessee.
