Discover Vampyr's disturbing story in a new trailer Dontnod's vampyric RPG is expected this summer. Text: Fabrizia Malgieri Published on the 5th of Apr 2018 at 13 
Dontnod and Focus Home Entertainment have today published a new story trailer dedicated to Vampyr, the highly anticipated RPG that will debut this summer on consoles and PC. In the new narrative-centric video, the game's mysterious plot is explored in more detail (more on that here ), all accompanied by a distressing cover of "(Do not Fear) the Reaper".
