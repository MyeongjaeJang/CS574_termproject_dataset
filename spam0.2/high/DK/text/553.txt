Pillars of Eternity II: Deadfire ankommer lettere forsinket den 8. maj. Obsidian Entertainment disker dog i dag op med en “features” trailer, hvor du kan se mere af hvad der venter. 
Det er ikke mere end små to uger siden, at Obsidian Entertainment og deres indie-udgiver med det alt for cool’e navn, Versus Evil, meddelte, at Pillars of Eternity II: Deadfire ikke ville nå frem den 3. april, men i stedet for ankomme en måned forsinket den 8. maj . 
Du kan i dag måske trøste dig lidt med, at toeren faktisk byder på en række nyskabelser hvilket du kan se i dagens trailer. 
Irvine, California – March 26th, 2018 – Independent developer Obsidian Entertainment, in partnership with indie powerhouse publisher Versus Evil today revealed their new ‘Features’ trailer for Pillars of Eternity II: Deadfire. 
Get a sneak peek of the new adventure that awaits you in Pillars of Eternity II: Deadfire, sequel to the award-winning, party-based RPG Pillars of Eternity. Embark on a dangerous voyage of discovery through the tropical region of the Deadfire Archipelago as you hunt a god to save your soul. Your adventure starts May 8th on PC, MAC and Linux.
