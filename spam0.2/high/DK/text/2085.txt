Professor Lupo and his Horrible Pets is coming to Switch Get out, or get eaten. Text: Suzanne Berget   15 
Beautifun Games, the studio behind Nihilumbra and Megamagic: Wizards of the Neon Age, is releasing a new game called Professor Lupo and his Horrible Pets , and it's slated to hit PC and Nintendo Switch sometime during the autumn of this year. 
Professor Lupo and his Horrible Pets is a puzzle-adventure game where you play as the professor's intern, tasked with finding a way to escape from a spaceship infested with all of Lupo's horrible pets after an accident where they're all released from their cages. It's escape or be eaten!
