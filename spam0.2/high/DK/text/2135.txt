Electric cars The Renault Kango Z.E. continues to lead Argentina’s transition to the electric vehicle (EV) market by providing fleet cars for at least four companies. The four are: Buenos Aires-based electricity provider Edenor; Sergio Chiconi S.R.L. which is an electric installation and maintenance company for the mining industry; electricity provider Energía de Entre Rios (Enersa); and Teleworld Argentina which is a telecommunications installation and technical service company. The Kango Z.E., which was introduced at the Brussels auto show in June 2017, has an autonomy of 270km. Photo SOURCE: Renault
