Star Citizen free fly trial available this weekend If you've been curious about the space epic, now's the time to check it out. Text: Mike Holmes Published on the 14th of Apr 2018 at 08 
Star Citizen, the crowd-funding behemoth (it's currently closing in on $190 million in pledges and investments), isn't just a well-financed project, it's also an expansive sci-fi game being built to facilitate a number of contrasting experiences. It's certainly ambitious, and if you'd like to take a look at the game without making a pledge, now's the time as developer Cloud Imperium Games is this week holding a "free fly" event that gives prospective players a chance to take a closer look at what it has to offer.
