Laurence Noël has been appointed Senior Vice President ASEAN and CEO of the NAM joint venture in Malaysia, effective 1 May 2018. She will report to Carlos Gomes, Executive Vice President China & South East Asia. 
Ms Noël joined Groupe PSA in 1995. After holding various positions in powertrain projects and programmes, she took charge of powertrain, platform and module programmes in 2014. 
In her new position, Ms Noël will be based in Kuala Lumpur. Her main target will be to accelerate the group's development in South East Asia as announced in the Push to Pass plan in 2016.
