
SF Motors, a subsidiary of the Chinese automaker Sokon Motors, introduced its first vehicle at an event in Silicon Valley last week. The SF5 (pictured) is a luxury electric SUV. 
Preorders are set to start by the end of the year for a delivery date in 2019. 
The SF5 has a projected 300 miles of range and a 1,000 hp powertrain that can accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in under three seconds.
