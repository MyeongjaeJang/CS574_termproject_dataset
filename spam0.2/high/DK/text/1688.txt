Mike Albert Fleet Solutions 
Mike Albert Fleet Solutions and WEX Inc. have recently extended their partnerships. 
Both companies have been strategic partners for many years, which helped clients gain control of their fleet and fuel expenses, the companies said. 
WEX Inc. is a provider of payment processing and information management services to the commercial and government vehicle fleet industry in the United States, Canada, New Zealand and Australia. 
Mike Albert Fleet Solutions is a top-10 fleet management company in the US.
