Male characters in FF XIV are getting bunny costumes This will come with patch 4.3. Text: Petter Hegevall Published on the 15th of Apr 2018 at 09 
Square Enix and lead producer Naoki Yoshida have just confirmed what was first talked about almost two years ago during one very special fan Q&A where the developers promised to bring some gender equality to Final Fantasy XIV. Yes, that's right - the male characters of the game can later can dress up as Playboy bunnies just like the girls. 
As reported by AllGamesDelta on Twitter , once the 4.3 patch hits the game (next week, rumour has it) we can all dress up and look extra sexy.
