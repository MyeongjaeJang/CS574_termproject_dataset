Mugsters coming to PC, PS4, Switch & X1 this summer New trailer shows the terrifying invasion in action. Text: Mike Holmes Published on the 8th of Apr 2018 at 12 
Team 17 just invaded our inbox with a new trailer and the news that Mugsters is coming to PC, PS4, Switch and Xbox One sometime this summer. The game has players freeing their fellow humans as they make their way through levels overrun by alien invaders of various shapes and sizes. Finnish outfit Reinkout Games is mixing physics-based puzzles with sandbox environments filled with vehicles and the like, and you can check out their efforts in the trailer below. Related texts
