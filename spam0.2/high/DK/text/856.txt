The new Infinity War posters are straight up amazing Featuring all the different Infinity Stones. Text: Suzanne Berget Published on the 30th of Mar 2018 at 14 
Avengers: Infinity War is not too far off now, and Marvel has decided to grace us excited fans with some new and very colourful posters. Each poster is centered around one of the Infinity Stones, and showcases the different characters that are set to appear in the film, but as you may have noticed, at least two important ones are missing; Ant-Man and Hawkeye.
