The first trailer for The Incredibles 2 has arrived Are you excited for the return of Pixar's superhero family? Text: Magnus Groth-Andersen Published on the 13th of Apr 2018 at 18 
For several years, we've been awaiting anxiously for Pixar to finally return to one of their very best films, The Incredibles. A fully-fledged sequel has been in the works for ages, and now we're nearing its premiere. 
In anticipation of a reunion with the Parr family, Disney and Pixar have finally unveiled the first proper trailer for the movie, promising over-the-top action and beautiful animation.
