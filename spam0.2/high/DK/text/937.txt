Brazil Brazilian fleet management and car rental company Unidas has concluded the issue of 500mn reais (US$151mn) in simple non-convertible bonds it offered on March 22. This 11 th bonds issue involves 50,000 units of 10,000 apiece. They are set to mature on March 29, 2023 and is remunerated at 117.5% of the cumulative variation of the average daily interbank deposit rate, according to a company statement. The money is being used for cash reinforcement, aiming to align the capital structure and the debt amortization schedule of Unidas with the strategies adopted by its partner Locamerica. The two merged in January .
