Jotun comes to Switch this month Valhalla Edition to drop on Nintendo's console in two weeks time. Text: Magnus Groth-Andersen Published on the 13th of Apr 2018 at 18 
Thunder Lotus Games' smash hit Jotun has been doing the rounds on several platforms already, debuting on PC, before moving on to PlayStation 4, Wii U, and Xbox One in the form of Jotun: Valhalla Edition . Now, Switch owners will be able to join in and defeat the mighty foes, and they can do that this month! 
The game launches on April 27th, and in conjunction with the reveal, Thunder Lotus Games has announced that they've "welcomed more than 1 million players".
