The Division 1.8.1 patch bidrager med Xbox One X forbedringer Af 0 
The Division er med 1.8.1 den seneste af Ubisofts spiltitler til at blive optimeret med 4K opløsning og flere og bedre in-game effekter. 
Udover en (dynamisk) 4K opløsning, er der tale om 16x anisotropic filtering, object detail, forbedrede reflections og screen-space shadows. Der er dog også mere gameplay i form af Global Even 5 – Blackout. Det er flot at se Ubisoft stadig give The Division stor opmærksomhed og forbedringer til trods for at The Division 2 nu er annonceret . 
Du kan læse 1.8.1 patch notes herunder eller springe forbi Ubisofts Division website for en lidt mere fyldig udgave af ændringer. 
Following the success of Tom Clancy’s The Division substantial Update 1.8 “Resistance,” Ubisoft® today announced Update 1.8.1, available on all platforms. It includes Xbox One X Enhanced 4K Ultra HD optimization and two new global events – Blackout and Onslaught, respectively available end of April and end of May. 
Additional updates include: • Increased drop rate for Classified Gear • Legendary difficulty added for Amherst’s Apartment and Grand Central Station missions • More than 40 new commendations • Global Events offer new Classified Gear Set catches specific to each set • Legendary Missions have 20% chance to reward Classified Gear • Assignments provide more Division Tech • Season Pass supply drop awards 100 Division Tech • Bug fixes
