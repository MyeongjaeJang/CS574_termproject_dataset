Reuters: Financial Services and Real Estate Reuters: Financial Services and Real Estate SHANGHAI, March 23 (Reuters) - China's primary money rates fell this week on ample liquidity in the banking system, shrugging off a slight increase in short-term rates by the central bank following the Federal Reserve's hike in U.S. ones. The People's Bank of China's (PBOC) move was a reminder Beijing is keeping an eye on global market trends even as it cracks down on financial risks at home.
