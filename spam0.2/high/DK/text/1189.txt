We talked to Blizzard about WoW: Battle of Azeroth It's going to be a busy summer for World of Warcraft players. Text: Mike Holmes   16 
World of Warcraft players are preparing for hostilities to resume between the Horde and the Alliance in Battle of Azeroth, the next major expansion coming to Blizzard's evergreen MMORPG. To that end, we spoke to lead encounter designer Morgan Day at PAX East 2018, who had plenty to tell us about the expansion that takes players to new locations in search of reinforcements as the age-old battle between the two factions rumbles on. To find out more about the new features coming to the expansion check out the interview below, and head this way for the recent reveal of the various editions of the game that are set to launch on August 14, 2018.
