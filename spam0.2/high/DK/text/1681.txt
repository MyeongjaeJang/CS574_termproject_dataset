Twitter Cross River State Governor ,Prof Ben Ayade has officially signed the historic 2018 budget worth N1.3trillion into law. The Governor feeling so emotional reportedly broke down in tears, while prayers where being said by Rev Fr Bob Etta (Special Adviser on Religious Matters). During the signing of the budget which was tagged “Budget Of Kinetic Crystallisation” Gov Ayade stressed that “ Cross River State must create a new vista, prospect, horizon and income pattern that will define us by what we are and not what the world sees us to be,” pointing out that, “we must put an end to the civil service status of Cross River and we can only achieve this when we think out of the box”.
