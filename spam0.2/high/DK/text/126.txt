Nissan has announced a number of changes to its senior management team for North America as the company is working towards its MOVE to 2022 plan to achieve sustainable growth and to lead the technology and business evolution of the automotive industry. 
Rhis reshuffle includes the following changes: Judy Wheeler is appointed division vice president for US and Canada Dealer Network Strategy. Billy Hayes is named division vice president for trucks and light commercial vehicles. He is also responsible for South/Mountain Regions. Claudia Marquez, senior director Sales Operations for Infiniti USA, is promoted to vice president Sales and Vehicle Operations, Nissan Division US Michael Colleran is named vice president Infiniti Americas. Beth Osburn was appointed director NMAC Credit Services.
