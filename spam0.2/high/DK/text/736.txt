New York International Auto Show 
At the New York International Auto Show, the Range Rover Velar won the World Car Design of the Year award for 2018. A jury of 82 motoring journalists from 24 countries believed the Velar to be the most beautiful car of today. 
Last year, the F-Pace of sister brand Jaguar took home the prize.
