Horizon Chase Turbo races onto PC and PS4 in May Ready for some nostalgic arcade racing? Text: Suzanne Berget Published on the 17th of Apr 2018 at 10 
Horizon Chase Turbo is a charming racing game inspired by the classic of the 80s and 90s like Out Run, Lotus Turbo Challenge, Top Gear (SNES) and Rush, and features "reinvented" 16-bit graphics, split-screen multiplayer for up to 4 players, 12 Cups, 48 Cities, 110 tracks, 26 Unlockable Cars and 10 Upgrades and music by Barry Leitch, who was the sound designer for Top Gear and Lotus Turbo Challenge! 
Get ready for a proper nostalgic trip around the world when the game launches on PC and PlayStation 4 on May 15. For more information check out the official website and the announcement trailer below: Related texts
