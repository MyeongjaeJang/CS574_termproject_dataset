Police carrying out 'urgent enquiries' to locate missing Torbay man 61 SHARES (Last Updated On: March 29, 2018) 
Police say they are carrying out urgent enquiries to locate a 53 year old man missing from Torbay. 
Andrew Haworth has not been seen since Sunday 18th March. 
Police say they are concerned for his welfare and are urging the public to report any sightings to them. 
He is described as white, 5ft 9ins tall, of thin build, with a bald head. He was last seen wearing brown/green combat trousers and an army jacket. 
If you have seen him or know of his whereabouts, please call police on 101, quoting log number 865 of 18/03/18.
