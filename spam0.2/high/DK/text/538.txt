Denza 
Daimler and its Chinese partner BYD have presented their new Denza 500 electric vehicle for the Chinese market. The new car offers customers an extensive upgrade, new features and a longer range of around 500 km. The Denza 500 combines Daimler's vehicle architecture and safety expertise with BYD's battery technology. 
2017 was the best performing year for Denza to date. The Chinese carmaker is now also offered through selected Mercedes-Benz dealers. Denza also works with Chinese ride hailing platforms, allowing more Chinese customers to get acquainted with its vehicles.
