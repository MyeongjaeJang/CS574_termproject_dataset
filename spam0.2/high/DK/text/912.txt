Ligue 1 Review: Depay Brace Boosts Lyon UCL Hopes 
by Busybuddies April 2, 2018 
Dutch winger Memphis Depay scored a brace to help his French Ligue 1 side Olympique Lyon beat Tolouse 2-0 at home in a Matchday 31 clash played on Sunday night. 
The former Manchester United star scored the opening goal on 23 minutes before completing his brace from the spot with two first half minutes left to help his side secure the three points at stake. 
The win ensured that Lyon (60 points) move to within two points of fellow Olympique side Marseille (62 points) who occupy the final UCL place (3rd position).
