Marion Morad is one of Switzerland’s best business mediators and a lawyer with an expert background in business. With 27 years of experience as a lawyer, Marion Morad has today become known in the Swiss business world as an effective conflict solver and a solution orientated legal adviser. Not everything runs smoothly in business, and especially when larger disputes arise, they can very quickly turn into legal issues; internal disagreements within a company or the different ways in which business partners can interpret existing contracts, a conflict between shareholders, or planning for company succession. Before going to court however, working with a mediator might be the right solution. “Next to actually preventing conflicts through good contracts, I can work with all parties involved to find a solution,” says Marion Morad.
