New Mythical Pokémon announced for Ultra Sun/Ultra Moon It's electric-based and called Zeraora. Text: Ricardo C. Esteves Published on the 9th of Apr 2018 at 14 
The Pokémon Company International and Nintendo have revealed a new Mythical pokémon, Zeraora, exclusive to Pokémon Ultra Sun/Ultra Moon on Nintendo 3DS. It's an electric-type pokémon that, according to the press release, can't be discovered through conventional gameplay. How can it be discovered? We have no idea, but more details will be announced soon.
