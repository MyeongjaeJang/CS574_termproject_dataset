This is the Police 2 gets first gameplay trailer Slated for release on PC and consoles later this year. Text: Bengt Lemne Published on the 13th of Apr 2018 at 17 
We weren't too satisfied with the original This is the Police (read our review ), but nevertheless we recognised that the concept itself had promise and so we're hopeful that the sequel will improve on the execution. 
Now developer Weappy and publisher THQ Nordic has published the first gameplay footage from the sequel, showcasing some of the improvements that have been applied.
