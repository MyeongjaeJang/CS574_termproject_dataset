Hearthstone's upcoming Echo cards explained in new video Coming in The Witchwood expansion. Text: Sam Bishop Published on the 25th of Mar 2018 at 12 
Hearthstone's latest expansion The Witchwood is on the way, and to get players ready for the new content Blizzard has released a new Hearthside Chat dev diary with lead initial designer Peter Whalen, who's here to tell us a bit more about the new Echo keyword coming with The Witchwood. 
Cards with the Echo keyword can be played over and over on the same turn if you have the necessary mana, and one of the goals is to encourage the question of whether you should play it once or wait until you can play it multiple times later in the game, all of which is discussed in the video below.
