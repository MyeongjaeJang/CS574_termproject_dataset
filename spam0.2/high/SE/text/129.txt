Saab AB raises MSEK 500 in the bond market Press release 28 March 2018 
Saab AB has today, March 28 2018, issued a floating rate bonds (FRN) of total MSEK 500 using the existing Medium Term Note programme (MTN). 
The bond has been issued in one tranch according to following: Maturity 500 MSEK FRN 
Saab currently is in a growth phase. The purpose of the transaction is to increase the financial flexibility and to diversify the debt profile during this phase. 
Nordea has acted as bookrunner for the transaction.
