411 lediga jobb på företaget Elgiganten CPH seeks Finnish or Norwegian speaking part time IT-advisors 
Would you like to work with IT, support, customer service and technique? Do you speak Norwegian or Finnish and are you able to work part time in Copenhagen? Then you might just be the right candidate for Elgigantens Helpdesk! Information om uppdraget: As a technical advisor you will become part of Elgigantens helpdesk located in Ørestad, Copenhagen. Your tasks will consist of recieve incoming calls from Elgigantens Norwegian or Finnish customers, that have problems with their computer, TV, phone and the like. You have to provide technical support by phone and/or forward the inquiry to the manufacturer. Elgiganten offers: - Great development opportunities in a professional work environment- A convenient work location in Ørestad, Copenhagen, close to train and metro station. - Fitness and lunch program.- Have an interest in technique- Are stable and patient - Can work a minimum of 10 hours per week and approximately 2 Saturdays a month- Are fluent in one of the nordic languages (Danish, Swedish, Norwegian or Finnish) Start up is 9 April 2018. Interviews will be held continuously, so send your application today!We look forward to receiving your application. Ansök nu Ansök nu Skriv ut Skicka och dela!
