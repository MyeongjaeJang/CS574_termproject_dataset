639 Student Apartments to Lyngby Image: Lars Gitz Arkitekter Denmark Basecamp Student are in charge of the project, worth approximately EUR 71M. 
2018-03-28 
tweet The international Basecamp Student group continues to establish their concept for student apartments. The new plan will be the largest student accommodation construction in Denmark in modern times. In August 2020 at the latest, the 639 apartments in Lyngby will be done. “A first step can be taken towards a unique campus environment, that apart from apartments will encompass the erection of other accommodation types. In total there will be approximately 40,000 square meters of apartments,” says Jesper Dam, Partner and CEO at Basecamp Student Scandinavia.
