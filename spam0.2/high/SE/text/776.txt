Everything ready for the 2018 Tokyo IACDS annual convention Published Today at 10:56 The International Association of Concrete Drillers and Sawers, IACDS, has announced that its annual convention will be held from 21 to 23 May in Tokyo, Japan. The event will be celebrated in the framework of the Japanese Association general assembly, which is to be held on 23 May. The convention combines professional knowledge sharing through a number of conference sessions, with a showcase of products and services for the industry which will be presented in a dedicated exhibition area. Social and leisure activities have been arranged for participants to network and share experiences and ideas. For more information about the event, please visit: www.iacds.org/convention
