Invitation to press and analyst conference in Stockholm Thu, Apr 12, 2018 13:00 CET 
AB Volvo hereby invites to a press and analyst conference on April 24th, 2018 in conjunction with the release of the Volvo Group report for the first quarter 2018. The press conference will take place at Tändstickspalatset in Stockholm. The report is scheduled to be released at 7.20 a.m. CEST April 24th . 
Press conference The press conference will begin at 9.00 a.m. CEST and will be held at Tändstickspalatset, V Trädgårdsgatan 15 in Stockholm. President and CEO Martin Lundstedt and Deputy CEO and CFO Jan Gurander will participate in the press conference.
