A. Enggaard and PKA to Construct 57,000 sqm in Århus Denmark The entrepreneur group has purchased a 25,000 square meter large foundation by Godsbanen in Århus. 
2018-03-26 The first construction stage will start this summer and encompass 700 apartments and shops. The project amounts to approximately EUR 161M. It was recently announced that A. Enggaard and PKA will construct 300 apartments in Odense and now it is Århus’ turn.
