Home National Christians celebrate Palm Sunday Christians celebrate Palm Sunday Posted By: Times Reporter on: March 26, 2018 In: National No Comments 
Christians from all walks of life celebrated Palm Sunday to commemorate the triumphant entry of Jesus Christ into Jerusalem. Palm Sunday falls on a Sunday before Easter. In Blantyre, thousands of Church of Central Africa Presbyterian members from Mount Olives, Saint James, Matamando and Mount Carmel walked the streets to mark the celebrations.
