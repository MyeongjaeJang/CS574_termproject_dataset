Dar es Salaam. Tanzania Revenues Authority (TRA) offices will remain open on Saturday to enable customers to access tax services. 
A statement released by TRA on Thursday, March 29 by Taxpayer Education director Mr Richard Kayombo revealed TRA offices will be open from 8:30 AM to 1:30 PM. 
However, Mr Kayombo told The Citizen in a telephone interview that TRA offices would be closed tomorrow (Friday). 
“Tomorrow is Good Friday that’s why our offices will remain closed…to compensate the day, the offices will be opened on Saturday,” said Mr Kayombo. advertisement
