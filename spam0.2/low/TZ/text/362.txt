Arusha. Kenya’s opposition leader Raila Odinga is in Tanzania on vacation. 
The leader of Kenya’s Orange Democratic Movement (ODM) and the opposition coalition, National Super Alliance (Nasa) will be in Tanzania for an unspecified short vacation, the Arusha Mayor, Mr Kalist Lazaro said on Tuesday, 03 April 2018.
