China to hit US soybeans, cars, planes with retaliatory tariffs Advertisement 
Beijing, China. China announced Wednesday plans to hit the United States with new tariffs of 25 percent on soybeans, airplanes, cars and other imports worth $50 billion, hours after Washington unveiled its own target list. 

China's commerce ministry listed 106 products that will be targeted and said a date for the implementation of the tariffs would be announced separately. advertisement
