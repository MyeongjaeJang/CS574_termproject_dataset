Bulawayo City player out for twelve weeks 
Soccer24 on 20 Mar, 2018 
Bulawayo City defender Morris Kadzola will be out of action for twelve weeks due to injury, the club has confirmed. 
Kadzola was substituted in the second half after he picked an ankle injury during match-day one encounter against Chapungu. The club’s medical staff revealed that he suffered a fracture. 
Part of the statement released by Bulawayo City reads:
