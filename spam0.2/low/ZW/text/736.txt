Dawn Properties profits up 200% Published: 23 hours ago Listed property firm, Dawn Properties, yesterday said its profits went up by 200 percent to $3 million in the full year to December 2017 due to improved rental collections. The group's chairperson, Phibion Gwatidzo said Dawn achieved revenue amounting to $5, 1 million compared with $4,3 million for the same period in 2016, representing an increase of 18 percent. Operating expenses were at $2.9 million. - Daily News
