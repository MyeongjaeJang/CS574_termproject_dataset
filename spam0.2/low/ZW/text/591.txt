Baroka confirm signing Chipezeze 
Soccer24 on 27 Mar, 2018 
ABSA Premiership club Baroka FC have confirmed the signing of goalkeeper Elvis Chipezeze from Chicken Inn. 
Baroka CEO,Morgan Mamilla told KickOff.com that Chipezeze has signed a three year deal. 
“We have signed the goalkeeper. It’s a three year deal. He will join us in the 2018/19 season,” said Mamila. 
The 28-year-old keeper is set to replace Oscarine Masuluke who parted ways with the Limpopo based club after found guilty of misconduct.
