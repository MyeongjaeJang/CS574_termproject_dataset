Pastor Evan is taking the Zimbabwe Republic Police(ZRP) to court for the unlawful detentions and arrests he suffered from the time he became an outspoken critic of the governmemt in 2016. 
Speaking on his Facebook the cleric said: 
Police have now been notified of our intention to sue over my arrests & harassment since 2016. No Citizen should ever have to suffer at the hands of Police & the State in our nation. We must set a precedence 4 the pursuit of freedom & justice #TheStruggleContinues #ThisFlag 
He also shared documents of his intention to sue.
