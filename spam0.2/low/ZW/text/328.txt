Highlanders to launch Bosso TV 
Soccer24 on 23 Mar, 2018 
Premiership giants Highlanders have announced the launch of Bosso TV. 
The Bulawayo based side did not reveal much, but a video posted on their official Facebook page with a caption “Bosso TV coming soon” confirmed the news. 
On their website, there has been a category of the same name, giving a hint the project could also be internet based. 
In the video, there are scenes showing Highlanders players celebrating a goal against Black Rhinos and their coach Madinda Ndlovu giving instructions on the touchline. In the end, it fades out with a writing saying ‘Bosso TV’
