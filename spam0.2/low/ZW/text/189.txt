Zambia mulls Zimbabwe soya exports ban Published: 22 March 2018 ZAMBIAN oil crushers are said to be pushing their government to ban soya bean exports to cushion themselves from tightening supplies, according to a report by Zimbabwe's Stockfeeds Manufacturers Association (SMA).The development could negatively affect Zimbabwe, which relies heavily on the non-genetically modified (GM) soya beans produced in Zambia. - fingaz
