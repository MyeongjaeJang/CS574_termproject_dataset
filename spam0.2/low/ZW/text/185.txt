EMA confiscates 40 000 kaylite containers Published: 22 March 2018 THE Environmental Management Agency (EMA) has confiscated 40 000 expanded polystyrene (EPS), commonly known as kaylites, from small-scale distributors this year, as it moves to mop up the banned packaging.EMA information and publicity manager, Steady Kangata, told The Financial Gazette that the ban on kaylites remained in place. - fingaz
