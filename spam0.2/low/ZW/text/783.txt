Royal Bank directors to face claim Published: 19 hours ago THE High Court has cleared the way for a civil claim against the directors of Royal Bank of Zimbabwe for alleged gross negligence and mismanagement, which the liquidator says resulted in the collapse of the financial institution.Judge President, George Chiweshe, dismissed an application by the directors challenging the appointment of John Chikura, the chief executive officer of the Deposit Protection Corporation (DPC), as the liquidator of the financial institution that collapsed in 2012. - fingaz
