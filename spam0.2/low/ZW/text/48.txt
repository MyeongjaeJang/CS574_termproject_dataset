Mnangagwa commissions Nestle Cremora plant Published: 19 hours ago President Emmerson Mnangagwa has commissioned a Cremora Filling Plant for Nestlè Zimbabwe worth $500 000.Nestlè Zimbabwe Managing Director Ben Ndiaye says the plant will save Zimbabwe $2 million annually in import substitution.Nestlè Zimbabwe is now exporting to South Africa, Malawi and Zambia. - zbc
