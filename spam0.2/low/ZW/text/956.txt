NMBZ plans to maintain LSE listing Published: 31 March 2018 NMBZ Holdings says it will maintain its listing on the London Stock Exchange (LSE) because it is one of its biggest platforms for raising cash.NMBZ is listed on the Zimbabwe Stock Exchange and the LSE."We believe it is a good platform for raising funding," chief executive officer, Benefit Washaya, told analysts after presenting results for the year ended December 31, 2017, last week. - fingaz
