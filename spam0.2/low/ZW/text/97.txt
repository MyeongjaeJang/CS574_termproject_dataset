Tristan Nydam pulls out of England squad 
Soccer24 on 20 Mar, 2018 
Zimbabwe-born Tristan Nydam pulled out of England Under-19 squad for the European Championships qualifying mini-tournament to be held in Macedonia later this month. 
The 18-year-old midfielder picked up an injury which could not allow him to play in the tournament.
