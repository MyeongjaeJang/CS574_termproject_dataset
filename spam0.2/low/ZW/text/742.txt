Mnangagwa reaches out to Mugabe Published: 22 hours ago President Emmerson Mnangagwa and his predecessor, Robert Mugabe, are laying the ground for talks to thaw frosty relations between them. The face to face meeting between the erstwhile comrades since the soft coup that ousted Mugabe last November is likely to be held mid next month. While details of the discussion remain a closely-guarded secret, speculation has it that the mediators are preparing the ground for talks between Mugabe and Mnangagwa by defining the rules of engagement and establishing substantive issues from both sides to form the agenda of the discussion. - Daily News
