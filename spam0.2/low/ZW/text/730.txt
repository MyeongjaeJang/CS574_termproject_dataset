Another stadium approved to host PSL matches 
  
The First Instant Body has approved another stadium to host Premiership matches. 
Shabanie Mine’s Maglas Stadium is the latest to be approved, bringing the total number of venues to 11, from seven at the start of the season. 
The Chinda Boys will now host Nichrut at the new venue on Friday, shifting base from Mandava.
