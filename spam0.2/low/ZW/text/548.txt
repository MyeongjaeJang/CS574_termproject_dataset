TOP 10 MOST MYSTERIOUS DEATHS OF AFRICAN PRESIDENTS 
These are the top 10 most mysterious deaths of African presidents. 10 African leaders died in office between 2008 and 2012 compared to only three in the rest of the world. 
Two of those African leaders, Guinea-Bissau’s Joao Bernardo Vieira and Libya’s Muammar al-Qaddafi, died under not so peaceful circumstances. But in 2012 alone, the presidents of Ethiopia, Ghana, Malawi, and Guinea-Bissau (a different president, Malam Bacai Sanhá) died in office of natural causes.
