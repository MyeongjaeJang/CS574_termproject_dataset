Zimbabwe mulls board positions for diasporans Published: 22 March 2018 GOVERNMENT has initiated moves to draft Zimbabweans living abroad into the boards of State companies, as it seeks to clean up the parastatals and turn them around.Public enterprises have been crippled by mismanagement and poor corporate governance over the years. Millions of Zimbabweans fled into the Diaspora to seek opportunities at the turn of the century after a blazing economic crisis led to the collapse of industries. - fingaz
