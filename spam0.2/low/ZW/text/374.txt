Bonne fires Orient to first home win of 2018 
  
Macauley Bonne scored a brace when Leyton Orient beat Woking 3-0 in a match played on Saturday evening. 
Bonne found the back of the net just 10 minutes into the game before he converted a penalty seven minutes later. James Brophy netted the third one with ten minutes to play to seal Orient’s first home win of 2018. 
The Zimbabwe international had last scored a month ago and is now on 18 league goals in this season.
