Mnangagwa's forex list fiasco Published: 22 March 2018 BUSINESS bodies this week condemned President Emmerson Mnangagwa's list of alleged exchange control violators, saying it had many errors and undermined the country's investment prospects.The business organisations complained that they were not consulted before the list was published and are scurrying to engage the authorities over the contested disclosures, which they say had faulty legal premises and could cost affected companies business. - fingaz
