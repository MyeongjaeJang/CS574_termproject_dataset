Zimra grants yet another tax amnesty Published: 26 March 2018 Zimra has issued another tax amnesty on outstanding taxes that were accrued prior to December 1, 2017, with the moratorium set to close on June 30, 2018. Zimbabwe's tax collecting agency has been issuing perennial moratoriums since 2015 in the wake of economic challenges that have affected many businesses and individuals. - Dailynews
