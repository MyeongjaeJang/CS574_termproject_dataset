Dembare hike charges for Bosso clash 
Soccer24 on 1 Apr, 2018 
Dynamos have hiked gate charges ahead of their Premier League match gainst Highlanders. 
The Glamour Boys are hosting Bosso on Monday at Rufaro Stadium. 
The cheapest ticket has been pegged at $5, up from the ordinary $3. VIP is going for $10 while VVIP ticket is costing $20. 
Ecocash and swipe services will be available. Gates open at 12 pm. 
Gate Charges: 1.Rest of the ground 5.00 2.VIP. 10.00
