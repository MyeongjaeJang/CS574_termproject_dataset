'Harare population to hit 5 million in 10 years' Published: 27 March 2018 It is anticipated that the population of Harare will increase to five million in 10 years time and there is need to urgently upgrade its infrastructure to meet demand, Environment, Water and Climate Minister Oppah Muchinguri-Kashiri has said. She was speaking at the launch of the Zimbabwe International Hydrological Programme National Committee in Harare last week. Currently, Harare has close to two million residents. The committee is expected to proffer solutions for effective and sustainable water resource management. - The Herald
