Matsaira calls it a day at TSL Published: 28 March 2018 TSL group chief executive officer Washington Matsaira, is leaving the agro-focused conglomerate after six years of steering the ship. The announcement was made yesterday by board chairperson Antony Mandiwanza at an AGM in the capital. Matsaira was appointed as group CE of TSL Limited on January 1, 2012 after a sterling career as a banker, a period over which he presided over one of the top local banks. - Daily News
