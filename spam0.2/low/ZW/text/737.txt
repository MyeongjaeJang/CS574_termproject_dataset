Chamisa to drag Khupe to court Published: 23 hours ago Two rival camps of the MDC led by Nelson Chamisa and Thokozani Khupe have clashed over ownership of the party name and symbols. MDC acting chairperson Morgen Komichi yesterday said they will drag Khupe to court if she continues using the name MDC T but Khupe's camp is not bothered. The faction's spokesperson Obert Gutu yesterday rubbished Komichi's threats, saying Khupe remains the MDC's rightful leader. - Daily News
