The State of Qatar and the United Kingdom met on Sunday for the fourth Qatar-UK strategic dialogue (Sharaka). The Qatari side was chaired by Minister of State for Foreign Affairs HE Sultan bin Saad al Muraikhi and the UK side was chaired by UK Minister of State for the Middle East Alistair Burt. Sharaka is one of the most important mechanisms for the development of bilateral relations between Qatar and the UK. It is responsible for follow-up of decisions, files and projects, supporting the bilateral track and exploring opportunities for cooperation for the benefit of both countries. (TNN)
