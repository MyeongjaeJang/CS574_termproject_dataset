Tribune News Network
DOHA
Scotland's Fraser Cockburn defeated former Indian cricket captain Kapil Dev in an exciting end to claim the Qatar Senior Open Golf Championship, held at the Doha Golf Club.
Cockburn scored plus four after two days and Kapil Dev finished second with plus five. Sweden's Claes Byberg secured the third spot with plus six.
The tournament, the first in the region for the senior golfers, saw 143 players from different parts of the world come Doha and compete for the top honours.
Qatar Golf Association General Secretary Fahad bin Nasser al Naimi, who awarded the winners, said the championship for over 45 years old players was unique and have a very good reputation in the region.
