Chairman of the National Human Rights Committee (NHRC) HE Dr Ali bin Smaikh al Marri met with Minister of Justice of Morocco Mohamed Aujjar in Marrakesh on Tuesday.
They discussed means of enhancing cooperation on issues of human rights as well as exchange of expertise.
The NHRC is participating in an international conference organised by Morocco's Ministry of Justice, the Supreme Judicial Council and the Public Prosecution, entitled 'Independence of the judiciary between guaranteeing the rights of litigants and respect for the rules of justice'.
The NHRC was invited to the conference in recognition of its regional and international efforts to establish a human rights culture and to achieve best practices in this regard through international conferences organised in Doha. (QNA)
