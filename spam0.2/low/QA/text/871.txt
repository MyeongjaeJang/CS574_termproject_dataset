PRESIDENT of the Republic of Turkey Recep Tayyip Erdogan met with Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister HE Sheikh Mohammed bin Abdulrahman al Thani in Ankara on Tuesday.
At the outset of the meeting, the FM conveyed the greetings of the Emir His Highness Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad al Thani to the Turkish President as well as his wishes of more progress and prosperity to the Turkish people. Erdogan entrusted the FM to convey his greetings to HH the Emir, wishing him good health and happiness and Qatari people further progress and prosperity.
During the meeting, they discussed bilateral relations and means to further develop and promote them, in addition to a host of issues of common concern. (QNA)
