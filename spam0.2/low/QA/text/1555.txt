Chief of Staff of Qatar Armed Forces HE Major-General (Pilot) Ghanim bin Shaheen al Ghanim met separately with Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff of the US General Joseph Francis Danford, Vice Chief of Staff of the Air Force General Stephen Wilson and Assistant Secretary of Defence for International Security Affairs Robert Karem in Washington on Tuesday. The meetings discussed relations between Qatar and the US, and means of strengthening them in defence and military fields, in addition to a number of issues of mutual interest, especially the fight against terrorism and extremism. (QNA)
