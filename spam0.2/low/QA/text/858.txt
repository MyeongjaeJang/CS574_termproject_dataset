HH Sheikha Moza bint Nasser, Chairperson of the Education Above All Foundation, will head to Geneva, Switzerland, to participate in a joint high-level forum organised by Education Above All and the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) at the United Nations Office at Geneva on Wednesday (March 28).
The event, titled 'Quality Education for a Sustainable Future: A Comprehensive Approach for Refugees and Internally Displaced People', will gather global leaders to shed light on the barriers of delivering quality education to refugees and internally displaced children.
The discussion will also highlight the worsening displacement crisis and the devastating effect of ongoing conflicts on educational opportunities for millions of children. (QNA)
