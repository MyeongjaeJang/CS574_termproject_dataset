Doha Exhibition and Convention Center (DECC) is set to participate in IMEX Frankfurt for the third time. The event will take place from May 15 to 17, in Germany.
The DECC will be part of Qatar pavilion, which aims at strengthening ties with industry stakeholders in European markets.
Doha Exhibition and Convention Center praises Qatar Tourism Authority for its active role in promoting Qatar as a leading MICE destination in the Middle East, and for its continuous cooperation with industry partners to showcase the State's opportunities for international business events.
IMEX Frankfurt is one of the world's leading trade shows for Meetings, Incentives, Conferences, Exhibitions and industry suppliers, as well as showcasing international destinations for business tourism. (TNN)
