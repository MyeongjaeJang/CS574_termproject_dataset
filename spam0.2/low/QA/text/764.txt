The Prime Minister and Minister of Interior HE Sheikh Abdullah bin Nasser bin Khalifa al Thani met with Deputy Prime Minister and Interior Minister of the Republic of Macedonia Oliver Spasovski and the accompanying delegation, on the occasion of their visit to the country, on Monday. During the meeting, they reviewed relations of cooperation between the two countries and ways of developing and strengthening them, especially in the security fields, in addition to a number of regional and international issues of common concern. After that, the PM witnessed the signing of a Letter of Intent for security cooperation between the Ministry of the Interior of the State of Qatar and the Ministry of Interior of the Republic of Macedonia.
