Doha
The combined net profit of all 45 companies listed on the Qatar Stock Exchange (QSE) increased 1.1 percent to QR38.56 billion in the fourth quarter of 2017 from QR38.14 billion for the corresponding period in the previous year.
Vodafone Qatar's financial year changed from March 31 to December 31. Therefore, the financial information for the year ended December 31, 2017 represents only nine-month period.
The financial statements of banks listed on QSE showed a rise of 21.3 percent year-on-year in profits during the fourth quarter of 2017. Lenders' profits amounted to QR4.6 billion, up from QR3.8 billion in the same quarter a year earlier.
