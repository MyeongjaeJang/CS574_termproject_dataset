Tribune News Network
Doha
Barwa Bank has launched its new salary transfer promotional campaign offering customers a chance to win cash and other rewards totaling QR1 million, the bank announced in a press statement on Tuesday.
Customers opening new salary accounts with Barwa Bank during the six-month campaign, until August 31, 2018, will be eligible to win exciting prizes, including monthly draws for five winners with total cash prizes worth QR20,000 per draw.
In addition, one lucky customer will win a brand new Lexus LX 570 on September 5 draw, the statement added.
