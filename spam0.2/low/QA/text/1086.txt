The Mahaseel Souq festival, organised by the Cultural Village Foundation (Katara), will conclude its activities on Saturday.
Sulaiman al Nuaimi, head of the Organising Committee of the festival, said Mahaseel Souq has achieved outstanding success this year through introducing varieties of Qatari agricultural crops from fresh vegetables to flowers, among others.
Nuaimi said Mahaseel Souq witnessed a large turnout of visitors and shops and company owners participating in the festival.
He stressed that Mahaseel Souq has fulfilled Katara's aspiration of supporting national products of high quality and encouraging the Qatari farm owners.
He added that the festival is part of Katara's initiatives to achieve food security and self-sufficiency, which contributes to achieving the Qatar National Vision 2030. (TNN)
