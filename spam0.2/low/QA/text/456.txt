THE State of Qatar and the Tunisian Republic's civil aviation authority concluded on Friday their meetings, which took place in Tunisia. Qatar's delegation was chaired by Chairman of Qatar's Civil Aviation Authority HE Abdullah bin Nasser Turki al Subaie.
The meetings focused on a number of subjects related to the civil aviation industry in the two countries. The two sides agreed to update an aviation services agreement they signed in 1984.
They also initialed the updated agreement, ahead of taking the necessary approvals to sign it, permanently. (QNA/Tunis)
