Moscow: The training session of the Russian football squad, which is set to play a friendly match here against Brazil, was disrupted on Wednesday morning due to a sudden doping control inspection, according to the national teams press service. The press service reported that a doping control officer arrived at 6.30 a.m. Moscow time and the procedure of doping samples collection lasted at least until midday, while the training session had been scheduled to start at 11.30 a.m, reports Tass news agency.
