DOHA Qatar Airways has revealed that it will further strengthen ties with Russia through a five-year partnership with the forthcoming Cosmoscow International Contemporary Art Fair, commencing from September, during the Russia-Qatar Year of Culture celebration. A Letter of Intent (LoI) between Cosmoscow and Qatar Airways was signed by QA Group Chief Executive Akbar al Baker and the Founding Director of Cosmoscow International Contemporary Art Fair Margarita Pushkina in Moscow on Monday.
