The Qatar-US Economic Forum will kick off in Miami (Florida) on Wednesday, as part of Qatar's economic tour of the US. The forum is organised by the Ministry of Economy and Commerce, in collaboration with the Qatar Chamber, the American Chamber of Commerce, the Qatari Businessmen Association and several US official bodies.
The forum seeks to promote and expand trade and economic relations between Qatar and the US. More than 200 businessmen and Qatari officials, along with 200 businessmen and executives from major US companies, will attend the forum. On the sidelines of the forum, discussions will be held between representatives of the business sector to strengthen economic partnership between the two countries.
The ministry said the forum represents an important interactive platform that brings together senior officials, investors and economic experts from both the countries to explore investment opportunities in various sectors, especially trade, agriculture, tourism and services. (TNN)
