Emir congratulates PM designate of Ethiopia
THE Emir His Highness Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad al Thani sent on Wednesday a cable of congratulations to Dr Abiy Ahmed Ali on the occasion of his election as Prime Minister of the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia. (QNA)
Deputy Emir, PM greet Ethiopia's PM designate
THE Deputy Emir HH Sheikh Abdullah bin Hamad al Thani sent on Wednesday a cable of congratulations to Dr Abiy Ahmed Ali on the occasion of his election as Prime Minister of the Federal Democratic Republic of Ethiopia. The Prime Minister and Minister of Interior HE Sheikh Abdullah bin Nasser bin Khalifa al Thani also congratulated Dr Abiy Ahmed Ali on the occasion. (QNA)
