THE Ministry of Public Health (MoPH) has announced that 9,929 students have been vaccinated in the annual campaign activities to vaccinate the students in grade one to 10 against diphtheria and tetanus and whooping cough. Altogether, 5,740 students in 60 public schools and 4,189 students in 72 community and private schools received the vaccination during the campaign recently organised by the MoPH in cooperation with the Ministry of Education and Higher Education.
