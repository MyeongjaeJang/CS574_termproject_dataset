Doha
The Al Rayan Qatar ETF (QATR) began trading on the Qatar Stock Exchange on a positive note by rising 3.3 percent on its debut day. The ETF, which began trading with a price of QR23.04 per unit closed at QR23.80. It generated a trade value of QR56.36 million on the first day and accounted for 20 percent of the total exchange volume on the day. It was also the most liquid exchange on the day.
