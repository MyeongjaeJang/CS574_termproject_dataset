QNA
Doha
Qatar has condemned the hostage-taking of a shop in southern France and its unfortunate consequences that caused deaths and injuries.
In a statement on Friday, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs expressed Qatar's shock at this terrible crime and affirmed its full solidarity with the French government.
It offered Qatar's full support for all measures France takes to maintain security and stability.
The statement reiterated Qatar's firm stance rejecting violence and terrorism. It offered Qatar's condolences to the families of the victims, the government and the people of France, wishing the injured a speedy recovery.
At least three people were killed in the attack when a man claiming allegiance to the Islamic State group went on a shooting spree and held people hostage in a supermarket in southwest France before being shot dead by police.
