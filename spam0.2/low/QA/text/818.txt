DOHA: Qatar International Center for Conciliation and Arbitration (QICCA) signed a cooperation agreement with the Istanbul Arbitration Center (ISTAC) to promote awareness on alternative ways to resolve trade disputes and promote a culture of commercial arbitration.
QICCA board member Sheikh Thani bin Ali al Thani and President of ISTAC Ziya Akinci signed the cooperation agreement on the sidelines of the third International Arbitration Conference, which concluded its work in Doha recently.
A statement issued by QICCA on Tuesday said the agreement provides for cooperation and coordination between the two centres regarding the organisation of conferences, seminars, and lectures. Moreover, it aims to share information and experience in the field of arbitration. (TNN)
