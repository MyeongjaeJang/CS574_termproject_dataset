Doha: Qatar General Insurance and Reinsurance Company (QGIRC) on Sunday said that its subsidiary Mozoon Real Estate Company will receive QR100 million in annual revenues from operating Mozoon Towers project. QGIRC owns 50 percent of Mozoon Real Estate Company, while the remaining 50 percent stake is owned by Al Sari Trading Company, the company said in a statement to Qatar Stock Exchange website on Sunday. During the full-year 2017, QGRIC's profits surged 40.1 percent year-on-year to QR307.4 million. (TNN)
