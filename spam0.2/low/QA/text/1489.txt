DOHA The Emir HH Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad al Thani held a telephone conversation with US President Donald Trump on Tuesday. During the phone call, they reviewed the strategic relations between the two countries and means of enhancing them to advance the common interests. A number of current issues were also discussed, particularly the fight against extremism and terrorism and the two countries' joints efforts in this field. The Emir and the US president also exchanged views on the latest events and developments on the regional and international stages. (QNA)
