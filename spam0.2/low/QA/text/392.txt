Attorney-general, Spanish minister discuss judicial cooperation: Qatar's Attorney-General HE Dr Ali bin Fetais al Marri met with Minister of Justice of Spain Rafael Catala in Madrid on Thursday. During the meeting, they discussed issues related to international efforts in the field of combating corruption and terrorism, and ways to strengthen these efforts. The two sides also discussed bilateral cooperation between the two countries in the legal and judicial fields. Ambassador of Qatar to Spain HE Mohammed Jaham al Kuwari and Spain's Ambassador to Qatar HE Ignacio Escobar attended the meeting. (QNA)
