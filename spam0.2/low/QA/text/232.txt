QNA
DOHA
The Emir HH Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad al Thani chaired the first meeting of the board of directors of the Supreme Committee for Delivery and Legacy (SC) for 2018 at the Al Bahr Palace on Wednesday.
The meeting reviewed the latest developments of the 2022 Qatar FIFA World Cup projects and what has been completed during the last period.
The meeting was attended by the Deputy Emir HH Sheikh Abdullah bin Hamad al Thani, and Personal Representative of the Emir HH Sheikh Jassim bin Hamad al Thani, Vice-Chairman of the board.
Prime Minister and Minister of Interior HE Sheikh Abdullah bin Nasser bin Khalifa al Thani, who is also a board member, attended the meeting.
