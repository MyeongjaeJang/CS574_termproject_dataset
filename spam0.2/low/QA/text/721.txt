Moscow
Qatar Airways (QA) is planning to take a 25 percent stake in one of Moscow's largest airports.
QA Group Chief Executive Akbar al Baker said on Monday that the airline expected the deal on Vnukovo to be concluded within the next eight weeks. The airport, Russia's third-largest, served 18m passengers last year and is used by the government for official travel.
Businessman Vitaly Vantsev and partners own a 74.9 percent stake in Vnukovo.
