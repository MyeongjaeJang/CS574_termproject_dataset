Berlin: UEFA has opened disciplinary proceedings against Zenit St Petersburg over racist chants reportedly directed at Leipzig midfielder Naby Keita. Football's European body said on Wednesday that Zenit was charged with racist behaviour, throwing of objects and for blocked stairways in their stadium during the Europa League match on March 15 in Russia.
UEFA's control, ethics and disciplinary body will deal with the case on May 31, two weeks before the start of the World Cup in Russia.
