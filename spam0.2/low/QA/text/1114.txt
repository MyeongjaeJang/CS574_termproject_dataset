INDIAN expatriate, EKP Abdulla Haji, passed away in Doha on Thursday.
The deceased, aged 63, hailed from Kannur district of Indian state of Kerala. Abdulla was in Doha for the last 36 years and worked as an employee at a city bakery.
Family sources confirmed that the mortal remains were sent to India on Friday. Abdulla is survived by his wife Muthari Mariyam and children Siyad Abdulla, Noushad Abdulla and Shanid.
