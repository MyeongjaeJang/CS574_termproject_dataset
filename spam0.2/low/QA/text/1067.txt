Qatar's Gross Domestic Product (GDP) at current prices (nominal GDP) increased by 9.4 percent year-on-year in the fourth quarter of 2017, according to Ministry of Development Planning and Statistics (MDPS) data.
The GDP at constant prices (real GDP) increased by 1.8 percent year-on-year. The quarterly GDP at current prices in Q4 of 2017 was estimated at QR160.88 billion.
This represents an increase of 9.4 percent compared to the estimate of Q4 of 2016 at QR147.04 billion.
When compared to the previous quarter (Q3 of 2017) revised estimate of QR152.21 billion, an increase of 5.7 percent was noticed.
The quarterly GDP at constant (2013) prices in Q4 of 2017 (QR204.22 billion) shows a growth of 1.8 percent compared to the estimate of Q4 of 2016 (QR200.69 billion). (QNA)
