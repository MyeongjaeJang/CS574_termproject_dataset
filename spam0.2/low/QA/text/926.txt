Tribune News Network
Paris Avenue Perfumes, dealer of branded perfumes since 2010, opened its new showroom recently at Automart Petrol Station, Al Gharaffa, with a new and wide variety of perfumes, Ghutrah, Agal & Cufflink collections. The opening was attended by dignitaries, invitees and well-wishers. The showroom is easily accessible from Shamal Road and located opposite Ezdan Mall. According to General Manager Abdul Salam, the showroom boasts of an impressive collection including branded perfumes.
