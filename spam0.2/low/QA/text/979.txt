Tribune News Network
Doha
The Public Works Authority (Ashghal) will implement a temporary closure of the Olympic Intersection (Sports Intersection) on the main lanes of the four sides of the intersection for a period of 36 hours from the evening of Thursday, March 29, 2018 till the morning of Saturday, March 31, 2018, in coordination with the General Directorate of Traffic.
During the period, traffic heading to the Olympic Intersection from its four sides can turn right only and use the next intersection.
The temporary closure will facilitate the completion of the final asphalt layer at the Olympic Intersection as part of the development works implemented on intersections and roundabouts in various areas of the Greater Doha, which aim to enhance the flow of traffic and reduce congestion in vital areas.
The Public Works Authority will instal road signs to guide motorists about the closure.
