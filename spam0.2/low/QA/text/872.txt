Prime Minister and Minister of Interior HE Sheikh Abdullah bin Nasser bin Khalifa al Thani opened the 23rd session of the General Assembly of the International Civil Defense Organization (ICDO) at Doha Sheraton Hotel on Tuesday.
The opening ceremony was attended by a number of senior officials concerned with emergency and civil defence from a number of countries around the world.
The PM met with a number of ministers and senior officials concerned with emergency and civil defence participating in the meetings of the 23rd session of the General Assembly of ICDO. He met with Minister of Emergency Situations of Kyrgyzstan Kubatbek Boronov, Minister of Emergency Situations of Armenia Davit Tonoyan, Chairman of the Committee of Emergency Situations and Civil Defence of Tajikistan Lieutenant-General Rustam Nazarzoda and ICDO Secretary-General Dr Vladimir Kuvshinov.
Talks during the meetings dealt with relations, especially in the field of civil protection and civil defence, and ways of enhancing them, in addition to topics on the agenda of the ICDO meetings. (QNA/Doha)
