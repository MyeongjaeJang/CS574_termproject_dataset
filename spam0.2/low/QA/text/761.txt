THE Embassy has received a number of requests for assistance, citing non-availability of seats in Indian schools in Doha, a media statement issued by the Embassy of India stated on Monday.
"In order to take up the issue with the Supreme Education Council, Ministry of Education, we need to get a better sense of the number of children who have failed to get admission in an Indian school in Doha."
