JET Airways has announced a special fare offer on the occasion of 'Easter' holidays.
The four-day sale will provide an opportunity for guests to spend the weekend with their family and friends.
Commencing from March 30, the four-day sale lasts until April 2. Guests can avail discounts of up to 20 percent on fares for premium and economy travel.
The special fares can be availed for bookings on select destinations in India as well as travel to Bangladesh, Hong Kong, Nepal, Singapore, Sri Lanka and Thailand.
The special fares are available for one-way and return journey. Guests can travel immediately as the tickets are valid from the time of purchase and also applicable to travel beyond the weekend.
