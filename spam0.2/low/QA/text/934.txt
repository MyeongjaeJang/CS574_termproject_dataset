DOHA Qatar Foundation (QF) and global energy leader Rosneft on Wednesday signed an agreement to establish a groundbreaking research and innovation hub at Qatar Science & Technology Park (QSTP) that will pave the way for development of new technologies, collaborations, and training programmes. The deal was signed by Omran al Kuwari, Executive Director, CEO Office at Qatar Foundation, and Rosneft CEO Igor Sechin at the opening of the Rosneft Research and Development Center at QSTP. The agreement will also support Qatar's drive towards economic diversification and sustainability by enabling and accelerating the creation of new technologies.
