New York UN Secretary-General Antonio Guterres has thanked the Emir HH Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad al Thani for Qatar's generous pledge of $50 million to the United Nations Relief and Works Agency for Palestine Refugees in the Near East (UNRWA). The came in a statement released by Spokesman for the UN Secretary-General in New York on Friday. Qatar's donation was among other pledges made by countries at a donor conference to support UNRWA in Rome on March 15. (QNA)
