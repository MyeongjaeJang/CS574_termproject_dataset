Doha Qatar has condemned and rejected a statement by Eritrea's Ministry of Information on March 22 that Qatar was one of the military threats to the Horn of Africa, a QNA report said on Sunday.
Quoting a Foreign Ministry source, QNA said the claims in the statement were false and contradicts Qatar's foreign policy principles that respect the sovereignty of other countries and promotes peaceful coexistence.
Qatar is keen on the security and stability of the Northeast African peninsula. The historical bond between the peoples of Qatar and Eritrea were strong, the report Quote: d the source as saying. QNA
