Attorney-General HE Dr Ali bin Fetais al Marri, who is also the chairman of the Qatari-French School Voltaire, signed on Saturday a cooperation agreement with Rector of Salamanca University Ricardo Riveira to facilitate the acceptance of Voltaire students into the Spanish University. The four-year agreement will see the university allocating 50 seats for students of the French school in law, sciences, economic and administrative specialisations. Qatar's Ambassador to Spain HE Mohammed Jaham al Kuwari attended the signing of the agreement. (QNA)
