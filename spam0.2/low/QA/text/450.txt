Attorney-General HE Dr Ali bin Fetais al Marri, who is currently on a visit to Madrid, has met with President of the Senate of Spain Pio Garcia-Escudero. Discussions during the meeting dealt with a number of topics of mutual interest, as well as the efforts of Qatar in combating terrorism and its financing. Marri also met with Spanish People's Advocate F Fernandez, where he learned about the Spanish experience of creating the position of the People's Advocate, his powers and the procedures he carries out within the framework of the rule of law. (QNA)
