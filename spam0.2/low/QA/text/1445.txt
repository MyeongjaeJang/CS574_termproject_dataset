THE Executive Board of International Association of Police Academies (INTERPA) held a meeting on the sidelines of the seventh INTERPA conference in Doha on Monday. Besides, the general council of INTERPA also held a meeting with all its members present. During the meeting, multiple agreements were signed with regard to states joining the association, including Cameroon, Tunisia, East Timor, Uzbekistan and Zambia. It was also agreed to hold the eighth INTERPA conference at Gujarat Forensic Sciences University in India in 2019 with focus on cybercrimes. The members also agreed on holding the next executive board meeting in Belarus in October 2018. (QNA)
