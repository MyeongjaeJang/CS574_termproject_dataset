Hamad International Airport (HIA) has been ranked the 5th best airport in the world at the Skytrax World Airport Awards 2018 in Stockholm, Sweden. HIA was also named the 'Best Airport in the Middle East' for the fourth year in a row and 'Best Staff Service in the Middle East' for the third year in a row.
The airport has been described as the most architecturally significant terminal complex in the world, as well as the most luxurious.
Engr Badr Mohammed al Meer, Chief Operating Officer at HIA, said,"HIA sets itself apart from other airports with its optimised passenger journey, unmatched retail offering, five-star customer service, punctuality, cleanliness, iconic architecture and unique art programme, making the airport a destination on its own."
