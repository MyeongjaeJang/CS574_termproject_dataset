QNA
Doha
Qatar Chamber (QC) has held separate meetings with trade delegations from Malaysia, the United States and Moldova to discuss partnerships and trade alliances in the banking, health, technology, entertainment and media sectors.
In a meeting with the Malaysian delegation, Dato Abdul Malik Kassim, State Minister for Religious Affairs and Domestic Trade & Consumer Affairs, said that Malaysian companies are seeking to explore investment opportunities in Qatar. He said the delegation was also looking to enhance cooperation between the private sectors in Qatar and Malaysia and cooperation between companies in all sectors.
Qatar Chamber Vice President Mohammed bin Ahmed bin Towar said,"Qatar has potential investment opportunities to build partnerships between the private sector in Qatar and its counterparts in various countries in a way that enhances joint cooperation and serves the mutual interests of the two sides."
