AMBASSADOR of India will hold Open House on Wednesday (March 28), 2018, on the Embassy premises in Doha from 3pm to 4pm to listen and redress any urgent labour and consular issues of the Indian nationals in the State of Qatar, according to an Embassy of India press release on Monday.
The Embassy of India in Doha will remain closed on Thursday (March 29), 2018, on the occasion of Mahavir Jayanti. (TNN)
