Doha HH Sheikha Moza bint Nasser, Chairperson of the Education Above All Foundation, will head to Geneva on Wednesday to participate in a joint high-level forum organised by Education Above All and the United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) . The event,"Quality Education for a Sustainable Future: A Comprehensive Approach for Refugees and Internally Displaced People," will gather global leaders to shed light on the barriers of delivering quality education to refugees and internally displaced children.
