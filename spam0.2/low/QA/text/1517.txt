DOHA Commercial Bank (CBQ) is planning to issue bonds worth $1 billion soon. The bonds will be channeled to markets around the world, especially in Asia and Australia, the bank said. Parvez Khan, General Manager of Investments and Strategies at CBQ, the bank is working on issuing bonds in the in Formosa, Taiwan, worth $250 million-$300 million. If the markets are ready to accept these bonds, they will be issued by the end of April. Bonds for Australian market worth $200 million is expected to be issued in May.
