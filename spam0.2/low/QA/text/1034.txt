QNA
Doha
The Ministry of Development Planning and Statistics (MDPS) has released the preliminary estimates of gross domestic product (GDP) at current and constant prices for the fourth quarter (Q4) of 2017.
Qatar's gross domestic product (GDP) at current prices (nominal GDP) increased by 9.4 percent year-on-year (y-o-y) in the fourth quarter of 2017, MDPS data shows.
GDP at constant prices (real GDP, 2013=100) increased by 1.8 percent year-on-year.
The quarterly GDP at current prices in Q4 of 2017 was estimated at QR160.88 billion. This represents an increase of 9.4 percent compared to the estimate of Q4 of 2016 at QR147.04 billion.
When compared to the previous quarter (Q3 of 2017) revised estimate of QR152.21 billion, an increase of 5.7 percent was noticed.
The quarterly GDP at constant (2013) prices in Q4 of 2017 (QR204.22 billion) shows a growth of 1.8 percent compared to the estimate of Q4 of 2016 (QR200.69 billion).
However, compared to the previous quarter (Q3 of 2017) revised estimate of QR209.00 billion, a decrease of 2.3 percent was recorded.
The nominal gross value added (GVA) estimate of mining and quarrying activities was estimated at QR53.44 billion in Q4 2017, which shows an increase of 15.2 percent over the estimate of Q4 2016 at QR46.37 billion.
Compared to the previous quarter (Q3 of 2017) revised estimate, there has been an increase of 7.1 percent in the GVA of this sector.
The real GVA of these activities in Q4 2017 was estimated at QR95.67 billion, which shows a decrease of 0.4 percent over the estimate of Q4 2016 (QR96.04 billion).
Nonetheless, compared to Q3 2017 revised estimate, there has been a decrease of 6.4 percent in the real GVA of this sector.
The nominal GVA estimate of non-mining and quarrying activities was estimated at QR107.44 billion in Q4 2017, which shows an increase of 6.7 percent over the estimate of Q4 2016 (QR100.67 billion).
Compared to the previous quarter (Q3 of 2017) revised estimate, an increase of 5.0 percent has been recorded.
The real GVA of these activities totalled QR108.55 billion in Q4 2017, which shows a growth of 3.7 percent over the estimate of the corresponding quarter in 2016 (QR104.65 billion).
However, compared to the Q3 2017 revised estimate, the real GVA of non-mining and auarrying activities increased by 1.6 percent.
