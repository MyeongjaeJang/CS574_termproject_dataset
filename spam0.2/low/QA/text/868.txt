Special Envoy of the Foreign Minister for Combating Terrorism and Mediation in Dispute Settlement HE Dr Mutlaq bin Majid al Qahtani met separately on Tuesday with National Security Advisor of Afghanistan Mohammed Haneef Atmar, Germany's Special Representative for Afghanistan and Pakistan Markus Potzel and US Acting Assistant Secretary of State for South and Central Asian Affairs Alice G Wells on the sidelines of the Tashkent High-level Ministerial Conference on Peace in Afghanistan. Discussions during the meeting dealt with the peace process in Afghanistan and other topics of common concern. (QNA)
