Khartoum The Emir HH Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad al Thani on Tuesday sent a written message to Sudan President Omar Hassan al Bashir. The message pertains to bilateral relations and the ways to develop them. Qatar's Ambassador to Sudan HE Rashid bin Abdulrahman al Nuaimi handed over the message to Sudanese Minister of State of Foreign Affairs Mohammed Abdallah Idris. (QNA)
