Bus driver who knocked dead traffic officer arrested   
Police have arrested the runaway Divine Coaches driver on suspicion of murdering a traffic officer at Old Kampala. The suspect identified as Godfrey Kakonge after a serious manhunt was found hiding in Kyetume Kyazanga, a place he sought for refugee after knocking dead Corporal Wilfred Tweituk a traffic officer attached to Old Kampala Police Station. 
Kakonge who was driving bus no. UBA 684T belonging to Divine Coaches, knocked dead officer who was controlling traffic on Lubaga Road at that time was charged on TSD 25/9/03/2018 pending court. - Advertisement -
