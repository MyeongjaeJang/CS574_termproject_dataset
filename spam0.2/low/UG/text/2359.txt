LIVE: Age limit case hearing continues in Mbale Advertisement 
The hearing of the high profile consolidated presidential age limit case continues in the Constitutional Court on Wednesday. Deputy Chief Justice Alfonse Owiny-Dollo is presiding over the case. About 10 government officials were summoned last week to appear and be grilled over their role leading to the enactment of the age limit law last year. On Tuesday, Gen David Muhoozi, the Chief of Defence Forces, Mr Keith Muhakanizi, the Secretary to Treasury, Mr John Mitala, the Secretary to Cabinet and Head of Civil Service testified.
