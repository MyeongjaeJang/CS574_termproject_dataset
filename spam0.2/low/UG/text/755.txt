Motor accident paralyses traffic along Kasese-Kabarole road The wreckage of one of the trucks that was involved in the accident Advertisement By Kato Kamulegeya 
Traffic along Kabarole - Kasese road was on Wednesday morning paralysed for about two hours following a motor accident. 
Eye witnesses say a truck registration number UAY 845V collided with another, registration number UAM 747 Y at Rwimi Bridge which connects Kabarole to Kasese district there by cutting off the road. 
The 6am accident occurred after one of the trucks reportedly failed to brake.One person has been injured and taken to hospital.By the time of filing this story, traffic police and motorists were still trying to clear the road. advertisement
