Mukono road accident claims three   
Three people travelling on a boda boda were on Wednesday knocked dead by a speeding bus at Lwanyonyi, Mukono District. According to the eyewitness, the accident happened after boda boda rider coming from Igar petrol station in Mukono town , carrying two passengers entered the main road, rammed into a speeding Gateway coaches bus registration number UAX002A. 
The bus was from Kampala heading to Jinja. Police identified the deceased as; Brian Mugoyo, Alozious Mbogo and unidentified man, all residents of Nazigo, Seeta in Mukono District. - Advertisement -
