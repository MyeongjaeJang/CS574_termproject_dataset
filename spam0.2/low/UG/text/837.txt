UMU make fine start in UFL Advertisement By Elvis Senono 
KAMPALA. 
Uganda Martyrs University (UMU) delivered a major statement of intent on the opening day of the Pepsi University Football League with a resounding 5-0 thrashing of Bishop Stuart at home in Nkozi yesterday.The reigning champions were 2-0 up in the opening 30 minutes with Derrick Mudaali getting a first half brace.Jamil Nvule then put the result beyond doubt with his own double shortly before the hour mark before Tavin Kyeyune rounded off the scoring on 80 minutes.UMU is aiming at clinching successive titles, a huge turnaround from their early days most remembered for the 11-0 battering at the hands of Nkumba University five seasons ago.
