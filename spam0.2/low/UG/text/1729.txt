Winnie Nanyondo bags bronze in 1,500m in Commonwealth Games   
Winnie Nanyondo has won a second bronze medal for Uganda at the on-going Gold Coast Commonwealth Games in Australia. In the 1,500 final on Tuesday morning Nanyondo came third finishing in her personal time of 4:07.93 at the Carrara Stadium. Melissa Courtney (Wales) won the race in 4:05.82, while England’s Sarah McDonald was second in a time of 4:05.48. 
Uganda now ranked 12th on the medal table with two gold medals and two bronze medals. On Sunday Joshua Kiprui Cheptegei bagged Uganda’s first gold medal when he won the 5,000m final.
