Kampala, Uganda | THE INDEPENDENT | After winning two of their three games on the opening day, Uganda Rugby Sevens take on fancied Germany in the World Series Qualifiers quarter-finals of the Hong Kong 7s on Saturday. 
Led by tries by Phillip Wokorach, Uganda beat Chile 14-10, beat Georgia 26-10 before falling to Japan 24-10. Against Georgia, Wokorach grabbed three tries while Solomon Okia one. 
The Uganda team will after the tournament fly to Gold Coast for the Commonwealth Games on April 9. Pool E
