Measles breaks out in Amuru District   
More than 12 children have been hospitalized at Atiak Health Centre III Atiak Sub County in Amuru District following measles outbreak in the District. The district health officer, Dr Patrick Olwedo Odong, confirmed the outbreak. 
“We have confirmed the outbreak clinically but we are taking the report to the Ministry of Health for further investigations,” Dr Odong said. - Advertisement - 
He said the district is undertaking a number of measures to control the spread of the disease including sensitizing the public about the signs, symptoms and dangers of the disease and immunising children.
