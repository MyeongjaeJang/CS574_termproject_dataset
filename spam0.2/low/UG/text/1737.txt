Man kills wife over returning home late   
Police at Lira Central Police Station are holding a 47-year-old man, who handed himself to police on Monday after allegedly killing his 48-year-old wife. The North Kyoga Region police spokesperson, Mr David Ongom Mudong, says the suspect confessed to killing Sarah Akidi for returning home late on Sunday. 
The suspect is a resident of Abor ‘A’ village, Adekokwok Sub-county in Lira District. Mr Ongom said the suspect said in a statement that on Sunday his wife returned home at about 8pm, which prompted a fight between the two. - Advertisement -
