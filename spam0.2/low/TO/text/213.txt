“Floating Budget Analysis” to boost Parliaments’ capacity to review budget in the Pacific 
Wednesday, April 4, 2018 - 17:15 “Floating Budget Analysis” to boost Parliaments’ capacity to review budget in the Pacific Wednesday, April 4, 2018 - 17:15 In early March, a “Floating Budget Analysis Unit” that includes seven researchers from the Parliaments of Fiji, Tonga, Australia, New Zealand and New South Wales travelled to the Solomon Islands to increase the capacity of the National Parliament’s secretariat during the parliamentary review of the 2018 budget.
