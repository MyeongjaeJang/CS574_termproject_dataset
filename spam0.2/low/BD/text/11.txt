Channel Swimming: Saiful Islam Russel emerges champion with record Published on: 2:57   
Currentnews.com.bd 
Saiful Islam Russel of Dhaka University emerged champions in the Fortune Bangla Channel Swimming Competition with new record swimming 16.1 kilometers in the Bay of Bengal with timing of three hours eight minutes and seven seconds on Monday. He has broken the previous record made by English Channel winner Ritu Kedia of India, who covered the distance with a timing of 3 hours 40 minutes. Besides, Mitu Akhter of Bogra (16 years) emerged as the youngest swimmer of the competition to cover the distance clocking 4 four hours fortytwo minutes.
