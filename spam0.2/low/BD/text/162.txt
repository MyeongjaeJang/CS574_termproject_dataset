Indefinite bus strike begins in Gopalganj Published on: 7:32   Country / Exclusive   Bus owners and workers of Gopalganj district went on an indefinite strike since Monday morning protesting torching a bus and demanding to stop plying illegal vehicles on Dhaka-Khulna highway. Bus services on inter-district routes remained suspended since 6am, causing immense sufferings to people. 
On Sunday evening, the decision to enforce the strike came from a meeting of District Bus Owners’ and Workers’ Associations. They also threatened to continue the strike until plying illegal vehicles will not be stopped. Recent Updates
