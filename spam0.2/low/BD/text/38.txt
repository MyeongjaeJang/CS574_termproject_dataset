Fifth Harmony on indefinite hiatus Published on: 2:39 pm - Thursday | March 22, 2018 
Currentnews.com.bd 

Fifth Harmony’s group members, Dinah Jane, Normani Kordei, Ally Brooke and Lauren Jauregui, announced the news about their indefinite hiatus via Twitter, reports EOnline.com. 
The group members attributed the hiatus to their desire to pursue “solo endeavors”. “Reflecting on the past six years since we started on X-Factor, we’ve realized just how far we’ve come and we appreciate everything so much, more now than ever (sic),” a note from the group said.
