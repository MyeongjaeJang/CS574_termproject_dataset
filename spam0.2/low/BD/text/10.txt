Biman flight makes emergency landing Staff Reporter | newsbangladesh.com Inserted: 16:06, Tuesday 20 March 2018 Dhaka: A Bangladesh Biman flight made an emergency landing at Hazrat Shahjalal International Airport in Dhaka Tuesday afternoon. The Dash-8 airliner took off for Syedpur around 12:00 noon but had to make the emergency landing 15 minutes later due to technical glitches, said a competent source at the airport said. The 63 passengers of the flight were waiting for the problems to be solved while the report was being filed at 2:30pm. All the passengers and crew members were reportedly safe after the technical glitch.
