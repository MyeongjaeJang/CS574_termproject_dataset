Jannat eyes fashion show for transgender Published on: 7:23   Entertainment / Exclusive   
Nazmee Jannat, a multifaceted talent with a passion in designing cloths, has already gained considerable fame for designing costumes for popular film Dhaka Attack. Now this fashion designer wants to design clothes for the country’s transgender people and launch a first-ever ‘all transgender’ fashion show. 
About her ambitious plan, she said, “Normally people keep away from them, but I think they are very soft minded people and they have a very discrete fashion sense.” About the challenges of designing clothes for the transgender people, this former designer of Ecstasy-one of the renowned fashion brands in the country– said that she needs to wear both feminine and masculine glasses to come up with designer clothing for the transgender models.
