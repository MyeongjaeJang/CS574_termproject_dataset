SSC, equivalent exam results likely May 6 Published on: 7:46   Education / Exclusive / Features   
The results of this year’s Secondary School Certificate (SSC) and its equivalent examinations are likely to be published in the first week of May. 

Examination controller of Dhaka Education Board Tapan Kumar Sarkar said, “We’ve sent a proposal to the Education Ministry to publish the results within May 3-7. 

Meanwhile, sources at the ministry said the results might be published on May 6. 

The SSC and its equivalent examinations began across the country on February 1 and ended on February 26. 

This year, a total of 2,031,899 students had been registered for the exams.
