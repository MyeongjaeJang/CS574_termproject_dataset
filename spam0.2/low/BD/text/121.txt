DU MBA student found dead Published on: 11:55 am   

A Dhaka University student was found dead on the campus on Saturday evening. The deceased was Tanvir Rahman, 28, a student of evening MBA programme in accounting and information systems. A security guard and a student noticed the body of Tanvir lying near the Faculty of Business Studies building and took it to Dhaka Medical College Hospital. He was brought dead, said the duty doctor. Dhaka University proctor Golam Rabbani told New Age that he had heard about the incident. ‘It won’t be possible to give any further detail before the post-mortem examination is conducted,’ he said.
