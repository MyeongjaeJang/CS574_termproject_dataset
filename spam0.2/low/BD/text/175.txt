
Glory Schhol & College become the champion of Prime Bank School Cricket Tournament 2018 Dhaka Metro Zone final by beating Kadomtola R.B School & College by 28 runs at Shaheed Sergeant Zahurul Haque Hall Playground, Dhaka University today (Wednesday). 
Glory School & College : 115/10 in 43.5 overs (Hossain 26, Taiyan 25, Remon Mia 4/32) 
Kadomtola R.B School & College : 87/10 in 32 overs (Milon 15; Arnob 5/11) 
Result : Glory School & College won by 28 runs.
