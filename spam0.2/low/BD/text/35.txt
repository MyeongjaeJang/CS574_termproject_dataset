Inter-Division/Inter District Basketball begins Thursday Published on: 2:43 pm - Thursday | March 22, 2018 The Walton Inter-Division/Inter-District Basketball Tournament will begin on Thursday at the Dhanmondi Basketball gymnasium here. Youth and Sports Secretary of the ruling Awami League and veteran sports organiser Harun-ur-Rashid will inaugurate tournament. Six team split into two groups will compete in the meet. Group A – Dhaka, Khulna and Jessore DSAs, Group B – Rajshahi Division, Chittagong and Chadnpur DSAs. Recent Updates
