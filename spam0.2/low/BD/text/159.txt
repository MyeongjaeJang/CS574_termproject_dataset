PM directs govt officials to explain quota system in media Published on: 7:35   Exclusive / Features / Focus / National   
Expressing disappointment on the ongoing movement against government jobs’ quota-system, Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina said quota scheme is integrated into the constitution to bring people forward who are lagging behind in society. 
The premier made the remarks in a regular cabinet meeting at Prime Minister’s office on Monday (April 9). 
Sheikh Hasina also directed government officials to give detail explanation about the quota system in media. 
According to sources, Prime Minister said, “Demonstration against quota system is unfortunate. Students have vandalized VC residence that never even happened during Pakistan period”. Recent Updates
