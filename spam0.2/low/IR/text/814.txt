FM Zarif arrives home after completing African, S American tour News ID: 4270061 - Politics TEHRAN, Apr. 13 (MNA) – Iran’s foreign minister and his accompanying delegation arrived back in Tehran last night after visiting Senegal, Brazil, Uruguay and finally Namibia on the 4-nation tour. 
Accompanied by a large politico-economic delegation, Mohammad Javad Zarif left Tehran for Senegal as the first leg of his 4-nation African and South American tour last weekend on Sunday. 
During his trip, the Iranian foreign minister met and talked with high ranking officials of Senegal, Brazil, Uruguay and finally Namibia and attended joint economic conferences in those countries’ capitals to boost bilateral economic relations as the main agenda of the tour.
