FM Zarif to visit Tajikistan Mon. News ID: 4271927 - Politics TEHRAN, Apr. 16 (MNA) – Iran’s Foreign Ministry Spokesman Bahram Ghasemi says the country’s FM Mohammad Javad Zarif will travel to Tajikistan on Monday, April 16. 
Accompanied by a political entourage, Zarif is due to attend the 23rd ECO Ministerial Meeting in the Tajik capital of Dushanbe, announced Ghasemi on Sunday. 
The Iranian foreign minister will also hold talks with senior Tajik officials and discuss ways to expand mutual cooperation, he added.
