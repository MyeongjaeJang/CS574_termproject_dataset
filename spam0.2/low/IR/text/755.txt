Yemen's air defense forces conduct airstrikes on Saudi oil facility News ID: 4269045 - Politics TEHRAN, Apr. 11 (MNA) – Yemen’s air defense forces have announced on Wednesday that it had conducted air strikes using drone planes targeting a facility belonging to the Saudi Aramco in the southern region of Jizan. 
A military source told our war correspondent in Yemen that the air strike was conducted with a drone plane of Qasef 1 type. 
On the other side, the Saudi company said its factory is working "normally and safely", a thing we (MNA) couldn't confirm from independent sources. 
However, sources in Abha'a airport said the airport has stopped flights for security reasons following the drone attacks on Saudi Aramco.
