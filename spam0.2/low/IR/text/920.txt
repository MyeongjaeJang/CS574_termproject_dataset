Iranian MPs leave for Tanzania’s Dar es Salaam News ID: 4272337 - Politics   Iran-Tanzania parliamentary friendship group has left Tehran for Dar es Salaam, Tanzania to meet with Tanzanian officials. 
According to the website of Iranian Parliament (Khaneh mellat), Iran-Tanzania parliamentary friendship group left Tehran for Tanzania yesterday. 
The Iranian parliamentary delegation, which is headed by representative of Shahr-e kord Ardashir Nourian, are expected to meet with the head of Iran-Tanzania parliamentary friendship group, the Tanzanian parliament speaker, the Tanzanian Parliament Foreign Relation committee as well as the Tanzania’s foreign minister during their visit.
