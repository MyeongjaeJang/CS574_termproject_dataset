Kindlian provides a comfortable way to manage your Kindle library. It copies all your books to PC and allows to create collections, organize, search and sort books. You can choose library layout and colour-scheme that better fits your style. You can view and edit book data like title, author, cover, series and description. 
Kindlian contains built-in ebook reader that allows you to preview and read Kindle books. The reader allows to open non-DRM protected AZW files, MOBI, PDF and txt books. You can select between the multiple e-reading templates and zoom pages and font size. The program automatically saves bookmarks.When you add non-Kindle book from your computer to Kindlian's library, the program automatically converts it to MOBI (the format that is supported on Kindle e-readers).
