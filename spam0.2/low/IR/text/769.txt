Iran’s Zarif lands in Uruguay News ID: 4268442 - Politics TEHRAN, Apr. 11 (MNA) – Foreign Minister of the Islamic Republic of Iran and his accompanying delegation arrived early Wednesday in Uruguay as part of his four-nation tour. 
The top diplomat, heading a high-ranking political and economic delegation, embarked on his trip on Sunday, April 8; he has visited Senegal, Brazil and Uruguay and is scheduled to go to Namibia as the last leg of his African tour. 
During his previous visits, Zarif met and held talks with officials of Senegal and Brazil on issues of mutual interest. He is now in Uruguay as the next to last leg of his tour.
