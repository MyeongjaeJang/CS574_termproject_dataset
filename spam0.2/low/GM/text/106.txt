The Last Day Of Campaign April 10, 2018 7 SHARES Today is the last day of the campaign to win the hearts and minds of the electorate for the council elections scheduled for Thursday this week. All candidates have intensified their efforts and are criss-crossing their wards in the battles for the 120 seats in eight councils.
