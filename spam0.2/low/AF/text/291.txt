AT News Report-KABUL: Over 100 insurgents have been killed and scores were received injures in different crackdowns carried out by the Afghan security forces across the country in the past 24 hours, security official said on Friday. In addition to that, seven other militant have been detained. 
In a statement issued by the Ministry of Defense, anti-militants operation was carried out in Nangarhar, Kunar, Kapisa, Ghazni, Logar, Paktia, Khost, Kandahar, Uruzgan, Zabul, Farah, Badghis, Faryab and Helmand provinces, in which huge casualties sustained to the militants.
