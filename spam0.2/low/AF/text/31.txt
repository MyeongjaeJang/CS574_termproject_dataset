  2:02 pm) Four people were killed and three others wounded in a traffic accident in the Shahr-i-Safa district of southern Zabul province on Thursday, an official said. The incident took place on Zabul – Kandahar highway at around 10am in the morning. A security official told Ariana News the incident occurred when a Felder model car collided with a truck. He added that a woman and a child were among the victims. Accidents are common in Afghanistan’s highways where roads are often in a poor state. Most of the traffic accidents are basically because of the carelessness, high speed driving, insufficiency of traffic signs, and bad condition of the roads.
