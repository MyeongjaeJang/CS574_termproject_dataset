  3:02 pm) The Ministry of Public Health (MoPH) has confirmed that 29 Killed and 51 others wounded on Wednesday’s deadly suicide attack in the capital Kabul. The Ministry of Interior (MoI) told Ariananews that a suicide bomber blew himself up in front of Ali Abad hospital near Kabul University, killing and wounding many people. The Taliban group in Afghanistan has rejected the involvement. but the Islamic State claimed responsibility for the suicide attack, the militant group’s Amaq news agency said. “The martyrdom operation carried out with a suicide vest struck a Shi’ite gathering during their Nawruz holiday celebrations in the city of Kabul,” the agency said. The incident comes as Afghan people celebrated the Persian new year known in Afghanistan as Nauruz and which is a national holiday. 2018-03-21
